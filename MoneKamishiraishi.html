Mone Kamishiraishi

Mone Kamishiraishi is a Japanese actress and singer. She is represented with Toho Entermainment. She is the older sister of Moka Kamishiraishi.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 27, 1998
  • Hometown: Kagoshima Prefecture, Japan
