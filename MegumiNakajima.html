Megumi Nakajima

Megumi Nakajima is a Filipino-Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with E-Stone Music. Her father is Japanese and her mother is Filipino. She voiced character who sing. A couple of her roles is voiced were Miku from Akikon!, Citron from Basquash, and Lyra from Fairy Tail.

  • Birthday: June 5, 1989
  • Hometown: Ibaraki, Japan
  • Favorite Actress: Brigitte Bardot
