Motomu Kiyokawa

Motomu Kiyokawa is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Haikyo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 9, 1935
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Fencing and Judo

Character Voiced by Motomu Kiyokawa

Wang Wei Cardcaptor Sakura Wang Wei is the attendant that comes with Syaoran Li when he first moves to Japan. He is a kind elderly man he has teaching martial arts to Syaoran and Meilin. He is very supportive, very polite and well-mannered.
