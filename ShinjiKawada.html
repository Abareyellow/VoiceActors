Shinji Kawada

Shinji Kawada or Wassyoi Tarou is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Ken Production. He owns a Bouldering Gym in Kouenji and also a trainer. He voiced Axel Laboo in Fairy gone, Serge IV Sneak in Geneshaft, and Toshio Kotegawa in Grand Blue Dreaming.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 6, 1971
  • Hometown: Tochigi, Japan
