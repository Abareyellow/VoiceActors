Chiyoko Kawashima

Chiyoko Kawashima is a former Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Aoni Production. She retired after her role of Maron's mother in Kamikaze Kaito Jeanne.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 2, 1954
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
