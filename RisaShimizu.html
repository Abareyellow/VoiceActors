Risa Shimizu

Risa Shimizu is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Aksent. Despite having already an established career, Shimizu is still attending a women's college for music.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 9, 1988
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skills: Piano, Percussion, Singing, and Dancing
  • Hobbies: Watching Kabuki

Character Voiced by Risa Shimizu

Nurse Joy Pokemon Sun and Moon Nurse Joy is a recurring character in every Pokemon series. Their are multiple Nurse Joys in every region. She enjoys the work of medicine and loves Pokemon. She is extremely patient and caring Nurse Joy is head of Pokemon Center.

Pokemon Voiced by Risa Shimizu
