Manami Numakuma

Manami Numakuma is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. She decided to become a voice actress while in high school after listening to one of her friends drama CD. She was a fan of Gundam franchise during her time of high school. Her debut as a voice actress was for the role of Hibiki Ganaha from the The Idolmaster game series. Her anime debut was in Gintamia as a student. Her other roles are Takao in Arpeggio of Blue Steel and Machi Tokiwa in Hanayamata.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 15, 1988
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
