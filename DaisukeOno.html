Daisuke Ono

Daisuke Ono is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He has won multiple awards for his roles in Black Butler, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders, Bleach and Attack on Titans. He host a radio show with Hiroshi Kamiya. He affiliated with Aoni Production during voice actor school. He was with Mausu Production from 2001 to 2016. He is currently freelance.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 4, 1978
