Daisuke Ono

Daisuke Ono is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He has won multiple awards for his roles in Black Butler as Sebastian Michaelis, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders as Joutarou Kuujou, Bleach as Mabashi and Shuukurou Tsukishima and Attack on Titans as Erwin Smith. He host a radio show with Hiroshi Kamiya. He affiliated with Aoni Production during voice actor school. He was with Mausu Production from 2001 to 2016. He is currently freelance.
