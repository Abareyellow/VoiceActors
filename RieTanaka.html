Rie Tanaka

Rie Tanaka is a Japanese singer and voice actress. She has starred in several anime series with Megumi Toyoguchi in which their characters are apart of the same team. Tanaka is apart of Steel Angels Kurumi, a singing trio composed of three lead seiyuu, with Atsuko Enomoto and Masayo Kurata. Rie, at one hand, had a fan club called Cafe de Rie. She is currently married to Kouichi Yamadera. Some of her notable roles are Lacus Clyne in Mobile Suit Gundam series, Chi in Chobits and Suigintou in Rozen Maiden. She voiced Neptune in the video game series Hyperdimension Neptunia.
