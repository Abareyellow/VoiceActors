Himika Akeneya

Himika Akeneya is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She is a member of the idol group iRis.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 16, 1994
  • Hometown: Akita, Japan
  • Hobbies: Collecting Super Sentai goods, Drawing Illustrations and Long-Distance Running

Character Voiced by Himika Akeneya

Jenna The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments Jenna is one of heads of the Druids along with her sister, Zaneri, in their sacred land Istar. Jenna is energetic, show interest of outsiders, and somewhat forgiving. She dislikes anyone who disobey her orders.
