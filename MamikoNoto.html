Mamiko Noto

Mamiko Noto is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. Noto is friends with Ayako Kawasumi and Nana Mizuki. She is a member of MORE PEACH SNOW with Chiwa Saito, Haruna Ikezawa and Ryo Hirohashi. Some of her major roles are Rin in Inuyasha and Mavis Vermillion in Fairy Tail.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 6, 1980
  • Birthplace: Kanazawa, Ishikawa, Japan
  • Favorite Foods: Chinese Food and Fatty Meats
  • Favorite Flower: Osmanthus
  • Hobbies: Flower Arrangement, Photography, Theater and Watching Movies
