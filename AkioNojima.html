Akio Nojima

Akio Nojima is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated Sigma Seven as of 2016. He is the father of the voices actors Hirofumi and Kenji Nojima. His other son, Satoshi, is a nature writer. He voiced Saga in Saint Seiya and Gasshu in God Mars.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 6, 1945
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Akio Nojima

Yapool Ultraman Yapool is Seiji's guardian and a genius scientist. He was once apart of Star Alliance.
