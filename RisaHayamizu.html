Risa Hayamizu

Risa Hayamizu is a Japanese voice actress. She is represented by Arts Vision. Her major roles are Kumiko Yamaguchi from Gokusen, Saori Eguchi in Mirmo and Kyoko Asechi in Pretty Rhythm.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 28, 1975
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Singing and Skiing

Character Voiced by Risa Hayamizu

Zoey Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Zoey is a Pokemon Coordinator of the Sinnoh region. She enjoys getting ready for the contests. Zoey is very confident and eager coordinators and she likes to help out other coordinators. She also will trounce other coordinators in the contests.
