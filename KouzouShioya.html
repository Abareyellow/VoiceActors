Kouzou Shioya

Kouzou Shioya is a Japanese voice actor. He is represented by Aoni Production. He has a younger brother named Yoku Shioya. Some non - anime roles of Kouzou Shioya are Bakuryuu Parasaurolophus from Bakuryuu Sentai Abaranger and Bulk from Japanese dub of Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: The Movie.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 18, 1955
  • Birthplace: Kagashima Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Kouzou Shioya

Principal
  • Pretty Cure
  • Pretty Cure Max Heart
The Principal is Verone Academy Institute Principal. He has a big-hearted personality and is a bit clumsy, but the students are important to him and he trains enthusiastic teachers.
