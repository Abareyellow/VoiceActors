Yuji Ueda

Yuji Ueda is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Office Osawa. He voiced Brock in Pokemon, Shiro Iori in Kill la Kill, and Yousuke Fumma in Wedding Peach.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 15, 1967
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Music, Photography and Violin
  • Skills: Japanese Sword Battle
  • Wife: Omi Minami
