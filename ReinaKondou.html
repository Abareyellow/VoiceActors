Reina Kondou

Reina Kondou is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Hirata Office. She is known for her roles of Hana in Slow Start and Suzuka Nagami in My Sister, My Writer.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 28, 1999
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Reina Kondou

Ronie Arabel
  • Sword Art Online: Alicization
  • Sword Art Online: Alicization - Recollection
Ronie Arabel is Kirito's valet who learns swordsmanship under him. She entered Sword Master Academy trying to raise her family status.
