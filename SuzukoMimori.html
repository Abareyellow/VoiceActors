Suzuko Mimori

Suzoko Mimori is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and singer. she is represented by Hibiki Cast. She was enrolled at Komazawa University until she dropped out. She met the president of Bushiroad during one of her stage musicals which led to her bring offered a chance to become a voice actress and auditioned for her first role in 2008. she was a member of singing group Cutie Pai. imori is best known as the voice of Sherlock Shellingford in the Tantei Opera Miky Holmes series and Umi Sonoda in Love Live Series! series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 28, 1986
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Going to the Movies and Singing
  • Skill: Dance
