Toshiyuki Toyonaga

Toshiyuki Toyonaga is a Japanese voice actor, actor, singer and singwriter. He is affiliated with the Super Eccentric Theater INC. He started acting when he was 10 years old. He voiced Hideyoshi Nagachika in Tokyo Ghoul and Mikado in Durarara!!

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 28, 1984
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dancing and Painting
  • Skills: Drums, Guitar and Singing

Characters Voiced by Toshiyuki Toyonaga

Arden The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments Arden is a Holy Knight of Liones who serves under Denzel as a member of Pleiades of Azure Sky. He is seemingly confident, very easy going, and lack consistency. He is very boastful about his powers.
Kento Mikoshiba Fresh Pretty Cure! Kento Mikoshiba is a friend of Daisuke Chinen. He rather timid, gets scared easily and addres everyone with the suffix -san. He is the heir to the Mikoshiba Congtomerate. He has a crush on Inori/Cure Pine. Kento is polite and nervous around Inori.
Satoshi Mashiba A Silent Voice Satoshi Mashiba is a boy who joins Ishida's group of friends. He is eager to become the leading actor and considered handsome and talented. He hates and reacts violently to any act of bully.
Yuri Katsuki Yuri!!! on Ice Yuri Katsuki is a Japanese competitive figure skater. He is known to be "the figure with world's biggest glass heart." He is soft hearted and easily devastated in the face of failure. Yuri is quiet, kind and easily embarrassed but shown to be stubborn and extremely competitive. He has a crush on Victor since he became his coach.
