Toshiyuki Toyonaga

Toshiyuki Toyonaga is a Japanese voice actor, actor, singer and singwriter. He is affiliated with the Super Eccentric Theater INC. He started acting when he was 10 years old. He voiced Hideyoshi Nagachika in Tokyo Ghoul and Mikado in Durarara!!

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 28, 1984
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dancing and Painting
  • Skills: Drums, Guitar and Singing

Characters Voiced by Toshiyuki Toyonaga

Satoshi Mashiba

Yuri Katsuki
  • Yuri!!! on Ice
Yuri Katsuki is a Japanese competitive figure skater. He is known to be "the figure with world's biggest glass heart." He is soft hearted and easily devastated in the face of failure. Yuri is quiet, kind and easily embarrassed but shown to be stubborn and extremely competitive. He has a crush on Victor since he became his coach.
