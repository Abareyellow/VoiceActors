Yoshimasa Hosoya

Yoshimasa Hosoya is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He was represented by with Mausu Promotion. He was a member of a pop duo MAxBoys along with Toshiki Masuda. He has voicing roles in Strike the Blood, Attack on Titans, Ace of Diamond and Yuri!! on Ice. On April 23, 2017, he undergo treatment for his throat. He came back to voicing on August 4, 2017.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 10, 1982
  • Hometown: Hiroshima, Japan
  • Skills: Gun Shooting

Characters Voiced by Yoshimasa Hosoya

Charles

Lance
