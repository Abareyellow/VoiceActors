Yuuto Uemura

Yuuto Uemura is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group. He played Hikaru Hiwatari in Tokusou Sentai Dekaranger. He voiced Jugo in Nanbaka, Richie in Listeners, and Thorfinn Thordarson in Vinland Saga.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 23, 1993
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan

Character Voiced by Yuuto Uemura

Yuusuke Yotsuya I'm Standing on a Million Lives Yuusuke Yotsuya is a middle school student. He is a social outcast who claims that he hates all the people in Tokyo. He is very uninterest in real life, spending his time video game rather friends or studies. He is disrespectful and selfish. Often times, he proves to be quite intelligent, calculating and cold, acting rationally and logically.
