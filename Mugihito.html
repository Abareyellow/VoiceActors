Makoto Terada

Makoto Terada or Mugihito is a Japanese voice and stage actor. He is affiliated with Media Force. He is the younger brother of Michie Terada. He voiced Keiichiro Koibuchi from Princess Jellyfish and Cain Distorol in Fairy Gone.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 8, 1944
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Sister: Michie Terada

Character Voiced by Mugihito

Genkichi Momozono Fresh Pretty Cure! Genkichi Momozono is Love's grandfather. He was a master at making tatami. He is very close to love. Everyone knows and loves him.
