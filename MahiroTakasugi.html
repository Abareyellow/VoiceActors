Mahiro Takasugi

Mahiro Takasugi is a Japanese actor. He is affiliated with Spice Power. His anime debut is Haruki Shiga in I Want to Eat Your Pancreas. He is best known for his role as Mitsuzane/Kamen Rider Ryugen in Kamen Rider Gaim.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 4, 1996
  • Hometown: Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Mahiro Takasugi

Haruki Shiga I Want to Eat Your Pancreas Haruki Shiga or Me is a silent high school student. He is friends with Sakura Yamauchi after meeting her at a hospital. He is not sociable and does not have many friends.
