Kotori Koiwai

Kotori Koiwai is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated from Peerless Gerbera. Kotori voiced Utsutsu Miya in Gatchaman Crowd, and Kigurumi Haroukitei in Joshiraku.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 15, 1990
  • Hometown: Kyoto, Japan
