Kazue Ikura

Kazue Ikura is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She is most known for her roles of Kaori Makimura in City Hunter, Leni Milchstrasse in Sakura Wars, and Ryunosuke Natsume in All Purpose Cultural Cat Girl Nuku Nuku.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 23, 1959
  • Hometown: Nagano, Japan
  • Hobbies: Scuda Driving, Skiing, and Swimming
