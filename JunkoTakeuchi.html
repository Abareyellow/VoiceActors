Junko Takeuchi

Junko Takeuchi is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She affiliated by Ogipro. THe Next Co. INC. She voices young male characters with generally very quicky and goofy personalities. Her notable roles is Naruto Uzumaki from Naruto, Metabees from Medabots and Gon Freecss from Hunter x Hunter. She is the voice of Gumball Watterson in Japanese dub of The Amazing World of Gumball. She is married of Kenji Hamada since 2006.
