Yuka Iguchi

Yuka Iguchi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. Her major roles are Index in A Certain Magical Index, Tamaki Irie in Majestic Prince and Miku Kohinata from Symphogear.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 11, 1988
  • Birtplace: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Yuka Iguchi

Chelia Blendy
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail OVA
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
