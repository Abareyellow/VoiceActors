Akemi Kanda

Akemi Kanda is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She sung with other voice actress and was associated with voice actor units Drops and Aice5. Her major roles in anime were Asuna Kagurazaka in Negima, Rihoko Amaha in Witchblade and Ryou Fujibayashi in Clannad. In video games she voices Estelle Bright in The Legend of Heros: Trails in the Sky and Nanako Dojima in Persona 4.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 10, 1978
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan
