Michie Tomizawa

Michie Tomizawa is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She is retired from voice acting when she got married in 2002. She is most known for Rei Hino/Sailor Mars in Sailor Moon, Linna Yamazaki in Bubblegum Crisis, and Sumire Kanzaki in Sakura Wars.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 20, 1961
  • Hometown: Takashi, Gunma, Japan
  • Birthplace: Nagano, Japan
  • Favorite Food: Melon and Watermelon
  • Hobbies: Golf, Jazz, Dancing and Shopping
  • Skills: Jazzing Dance, Speaks English, and Stage Fighting
