Akemi Okamura

Akemi Okamura is a voice actress and narrator from Mausu Promotion. She known for her roles in animes such as One Piece and Lovely Complex. Her debut role was in the anime film Porco Rosso.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 12, 1969
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Calligraphy
