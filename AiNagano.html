Ai Nagano

Ai Nagano has been voice-acting since 1997. Her debut role was in Cutey Honey Flash as the lead role. She was part of Office CHK, Wit Promotion, and Drama House in her past. She is currently freelance.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 13, 1974
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
