Reiko Suzuki

Reiko Suzuki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Art Vision.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 15, 1944
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Reiko Suzuki

Galea Pokemon Advanced Generations Galea is the owner a boat house along with her granddaughter, Tiffany. Her Pokemon, Vapereon, uses the lake to their advantage.
Vaness The Seven Deadly Sins Movie: Prisoners of the Sky Vaness is an older member of the Celestial Clan.
