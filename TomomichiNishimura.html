Tomomichi Nishimura

Tomomichi Nishimura is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. His well known roles are Mitsuyoshi Anzai in Slam Dunk, and Jamitou Hymen in Mobile Suit Zeta Gundam. Nishimura voiced Engine Jum-bowhale in Engine Sentai Go-Onger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 2, 1946
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
