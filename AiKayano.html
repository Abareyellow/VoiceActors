Ai Kayano

Ai Kayano is a Japanese voice actress. Before becoming a voice actress, she wanted a job that "heal people." After taking a course at a vocational school and then later she worked in beauty industry. She got an interest in anime after seeing the Aria series after work. She wanted to become a voice actress after watching the Aria series. She attended a voice acting school. Her first role was in the anime series Princess Resurrection. After that she got roles in Anohara, A Certain Magical Index II, Guilty Crown and No Game No Life. She won the Seiyuu Award at the 6th Annual Seiyuu Awards.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 13, 1987
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
