Rikako Aikawa

Rikako Aikawa or Nahomi Iwata is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Atomic Monkey. She is a former child actress. She is marry to Mitsuo Iwata. She voiced so many Pokemon and human character in the Pokemon series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 7, 1967
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Real Name: Nahomi Kawazoe
  • Husband: Mitsuo Iwata
  • Hobbies: Karate and Scuba Diving
