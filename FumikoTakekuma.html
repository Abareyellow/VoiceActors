Fumiko Takekuma

Fumiko Takekuma is a Japanese voice actress.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: Feburary 23, 1980
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Fumiko Takekuma

Sophocles Pokemon Sun and Moon Sophocles is a student at the Pokemon School on Melemele Island and Ash's classmates. He is an intelligent young boy with a knack for inventing helpful devices and machinery. He is skilled Trainer of Electric-type Pokemon. When he is not absorb in his technological activities, he is friendly and greatly care for his Pokemon and classmates. He likes to collect infomation on all sort of things. He has scotophobia, but never scared at night with his Togedemaru. Sophocles is not very athletic.
