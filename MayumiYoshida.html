Mayumi Yoshida

Mayumi Yoshida or Misono Moriya is a Japanese voice actress. She is from Yokohama, Japan. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 26, 1982
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Favorite: Figure Skating

Characters Voiced by Mayumi Yoshida

Brianna Pokemon Advanced Generations Brianna is a trainer from Hoenn that May met on her and Ash's journey in the Hoenn region. She is interest in May's love life.
Elicia Hughes Fullmetal Alchemist Elicia Hughes is the daughter of Maes and Gracia Hughes. She is an innocent, 3 year old girl. She loves her father and she is cute and talented.
Vivi Winstrate Pokemon Advanced Generations Vivi Winstrate is the only daughter of the Winstrate family. Her family loves to battle. Her Pokemon partner is Marill.
