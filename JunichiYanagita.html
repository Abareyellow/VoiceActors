Junichi Yanagita

Junichi Yanagita is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. He voiced Werner in Chain Chronicle and Daigo Aoki in Food Wars! Shokugeki no Souma.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 5, 1987
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Guitar and Vocals in a Band, Reading and Watching Movies
  • Skill: 3rd Dan in Kendo

Character Voiced by Junichi Yanagita

Kurobee That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Kurobee is a Fair Oni and a loyal follower of Rimuru Tempest. He is the type that gets engrossed in what he loves.
