Asami Tano

Asami Tano is a Japanese voice actress. She worked in animes such as Love! Live! Sunshine!! and Smile Precure!

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: Feb 12, 1987
  • Birthplace: Amagasaki, Hyogo, Japan
  • Education: Horikoshi High School

Characters Voiced by Asami Tano

Eclipse Ophiuchus
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Eclispe Ophiuchus is the Eclipse form of Celestial Spirit Ophiuchus. Her true desire is to grant the rogue Celestial King more power by stealing life force from the 12 Zodiac Spirit.
Nori
  • Sword Art Online II
  • Sword Art Online: Alicization - War of Underworld: Part II
Nori is a medium-range, damage-dealer member of the Sleeping Knights. She always cheers Yuuki on. She has an energetic personality.
