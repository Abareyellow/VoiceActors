Yuri Amano

Yuri Amano is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. Her real name is Tomoko Yoshikawa. Some of her major roles are Kiyobe Makibi in Tenichi Muyo!, Keiko Yukimura in YuYu Hakusho, and Alcyone in Magic Knight Rayearth.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 5, 1966
  • Birthplace: Kyoto Prefecture, Japan
  • Birthname: Tomoko Yoshikawa
