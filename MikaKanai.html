Mika Kanai

Mika Kanai is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Ken Production. She is the daughter of Akihisa Kanai, Seinenza Theater Company producer, and Kazue Tagami. She was married to Kouichi Yamadera but divorced in 2006. She voiced Vanilla H and Normad in Galaxy Angel, and Ash's Chikorita/Bayleef in Pokemon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 18, 1964
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
