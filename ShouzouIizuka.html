Shouzou Iizuka

Shouzou Iizuka is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He graduated from the fine arts department of Nihon University, He played major villians in the first eight Metal Hero series and 38 out of 50 Vadar Monsters. He voiced Ryu Jose in Mobile Suit Gundam and Heart in Fist of the North Star.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 23, 1933
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
