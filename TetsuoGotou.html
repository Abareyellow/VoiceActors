Tetsuo Gotou

Tetsuo Gotou is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced Derflinger from The Familiar of Zero and Hannayabal from One Piece.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 10, 1950
  • Birthplace: Gifu, Japan
  • Hobbies: Folk Dance and Japanese Dance
