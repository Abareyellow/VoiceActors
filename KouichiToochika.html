Kouichi Toochika

Kouichi Toochika is a Japanese voice actor. He works for Haikyo. He was born in Osaka and raised in Yamaguchi. He made voice appearances in Fairy Tail, Digimon and Eureka.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 20, 1971
  • Birthplace: Osaka, Japan
  • Hometown: Yamaguchi, Japan

Character Voiced by Kouichi Toochika

Shiba Rave Master Shiba is the first rave master. He is a cheerful man who has a heavy responsibility. Shiba can cry anywhere at any time. He has light-hearted personality. Kouichi Toochika voiced young Shiba.
