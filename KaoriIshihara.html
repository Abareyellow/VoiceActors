Kaori Ishihara

Kaori Ishihara is a Japanese voice actress, pop singer and idol. She is affiliated with Style Cube. She is half of the idol group YuiKaori with Yui Ogura and she is a solo artist as well. Her notable roles are Seira Otoshiro from Aikatsu! and Aladdin from the Magi series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 6, 1993
  • Favorite Animals: Cats and Dogs
  • Favorite Foods: Chocolates, Ice Cream, and Udon
  • Favorite Person: Yui Ogura
  • Hobbies: Reading Manga and Singing
  • Skills: Dancing, Sleeping, and Waking up Early
