Natsuki Yoshihara

Natsuki Yoshihara is a Japanese voice actress form Pokemon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 14
  • Hometown: Hokkaido, Japan

Characters Voiced by Natsuki Yoshihara

Diane Pokemon Movie 6: Jirachi Wish Maker Diane is a friend of Butler from when they were young. She is Butler's magic assitant. Natsuki Yoshihara voiced the younger Diane.
Taillow Pokemon Advanced Generations Taillow is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. Taillow is very gutsy and will stand up to powerful opponents without backing down. It typically hunts Wurmple. Natsuki Yoshihara voiced Ash's Taillow.
Whismur Pokemon Advanced Generations Whismur is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. Whismur's murmurs are barely audible, it lets out an ear splitting cry. It sometimes startled itself by it's cry. Natsuki Yoshihara voiced Matt's Whismur.
