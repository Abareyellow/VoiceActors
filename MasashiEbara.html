Masashi Ebara

Masashi Ebara is a Japanese voice actor, actor and narrator. Ebara gradulated Tokyo Toritsu Millennium Hill High School. After working with Gekidan Subaru, he joined 81 Produce. He dubs over Tom Hanks, Wesley Snipes and Andy Garcia in their films. He voiced Van Hohenheim in Fullmetal Alchemist and Mighty Guy in Naruto.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 4, 1953
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Masashi Ebara

Van Hohenheim
  • Fullmetal Alchemist
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: The Conqueror of Shamballa
Van Hohenheim is the father of the Alchemy Protagonists, Edward and Alphonse Elric. He is a brilliant and well known alchemist.
