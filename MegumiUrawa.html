Megumi Urawa

Megumi Urawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced Asuka from Super Bikkuriman and Peasuke Soramame from Dr. Slump.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 30, 1965
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
