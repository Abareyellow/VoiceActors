Tsugumi Higasayama

Tsugumi Higasayama is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Osawa Office. She voiced Myuu in Psychic Academy and Tsugumi Rosenmeier in Aquarion.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 21, 1978
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan

Character Voiced by Tsugumi Higasayama

Paige Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Paige is the daughter of Nurse Joy and Karston. She likes to copy her older sister Marnie. She is friends with a Suicune.
