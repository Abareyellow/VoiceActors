Noriko Uemura

Noriko Uemura is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She is currently married to Iriya Azuma. One of her major roles is Josephine Yamamoto from Go! Go! Ackman.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 15, 1963
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
