Kenichi Suzumura

Kenichi Suzumura is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is representation of the INTENTION, a voice acting company he founded in March 2012. He is part of the group Nazo no Shin Unit Starmen. Suzumura married to Maaya Sakamoto, a seiyu. His major roles are Momotaro Mikoshiba in Free!, Sogo Okita in Gintama, and Lavi in D.Gray-men. Kenichi voiced Ryutaros in Kamen Rider Den-O.
