Masaki Kawanabe

Masaki Kawanabe is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He voice several characters in the Fairy Tail anime series.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 24, 1972
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Driving and Illustration/Drawing
  • Favorite Color: Red
