Noriko Hidaka

Noriko Hidaka is a Japanese actress, voice actress, singer and narrator. Hidaka began her career as an idol star but switched to voice acting. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She appearedin Japanese TV commerical for Nivea skin milk. She voiced in Death Note, Ranma 1/2 and My Neighbor Totoro.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 31, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birthname: Noriko Nagai

Near
