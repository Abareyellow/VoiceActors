Kousuke Toriumi

Kousuke Toriumi is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He part of the singing group Phero Men with Junichi Suwabe. He voiced Soma Yagarai in Aldnoah.Zero, Azusa in Brother Conflict and Kiba Inuzuka in Naruto.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 16, 1973
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Music and Singing

Characters Voiced by Kousuke Toriumi

Curio
  • Romeo x Juliet
Curio is a close friend of Francisco and a skilled swordman who lost his right eye. He is a down-to-Earth and a serious man. He is dedicated to protect Juliet with his life.
Dennis
  • Scrapped Princess

Gilbert Nightray
  • Pandora Hearts
  • Pandora Hearts Specials
Gilbert Nightray is Oz's best friend and loyal servant. He is willing to sacrifice anything for Oz Vessalius. He is loyal but cold. Gilbert is a smoker.
Ren Fujii

Ren Fujii is a teenager boy who is mature but can lose his temper. He knows when to draws a clear line between himself and the rest of his schoolmates.
