Kumiko Asou

Kumiko Asou is a Japanese actress. She is a model and has appeared in several commericals. She has voiced in Colorful as Makoto's Mother and Wolf Children in Horita.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 17, 1978
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Driving, Watching Movies and Listening to Music
  • Sport: Softball
