Kento Yamazaki

Kento Yamazaki is a Japanese actor and model. In the second grade, he really loved playing soccer until his 3rd year of middle school. He dreamt of being a soccer player and that if he had not become an actor he would have become a professional soccer player. He debuted as an actor in 2010 when he was cast in Atami no Sousakan as a mysterious high school student. He acted in live action movies and shows based on popular manga series such as Your Lie in April as Kousei Arima, Death Note in L, and Orange in Kakeru Naruse.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 7, 1994
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
