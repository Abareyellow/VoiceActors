Akina Minami

Akina Minami is a Japanese gravure idol who has voiced in the anime movie Colorful. She is married to Japanese comedian Masaru Hamaguchi.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 15, 1989
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
