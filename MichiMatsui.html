Michi Matsui

Michi Matsui is a Japanese voice actress. Her only role is Adele from The Royal Tutor.

  • Gender: Female

Character Voiced by Michi Matsui

Adele von Glanzreichi The Royal Tutor Adele von Granzreich is the only princess of the Kingdom of Granreich and the youngest child of Viktor von Granzreich. Like any children, she is a happy and playful girl who adores her older brothers and her dog, Shadow.
