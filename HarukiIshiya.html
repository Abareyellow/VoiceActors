Haruki Ishiya

Haruki Ishiya is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated Pro-Fit. He voiced Shuichi Nakatoni in Ero Lover, Pearce Thorn in The Price of Smiles, and Chad Chadan in Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 14, 1992
  • Hometown: Miyazaki, Japan
  • Hobby: Jogging

Character Voiced by Haruki Ishiya

Rigur That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Rigur was the the second monster to be named by Rimuru. He supports his father, Rigurdo.
