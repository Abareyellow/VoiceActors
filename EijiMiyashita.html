Eiji Miyashita

Eiji Miyashita is a Japanese voice actor affiliated with Aptepro. His main roles are in Last Exile, Assassination Classroom and Fairy Tail.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 26, 1978
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan

Character Voiced by Eiji Miyashita

Vittorio de Frescobaldi Romeo x Juliet Vittorio de Frescobaldi is Benvolio's father. He is the leader of the House of Frescobaldi and an old friend of Conrad's.
