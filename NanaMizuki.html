Nana Mizuki

Nana Mizuki is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She represented Sigma Seven. She began to train singing enka when she was five years old. She is well known to voice female characters who are very shy kind hearted and gentle. She is admired by many of her fellow voice actors because she has a great amount of power and strength in her singing voice. Her debut role was Hinata Hyuuga in Naruto series. She is a Pretty Cure fan and got a lead in one of Pretty Cure series. She did the voice acting of Window 7 mascot.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 21, 1980
  • Hometown: Niihama, Ehime, Japan
  • Birth Name: Nana Kondou
  • Hobbies: Music, Making Sweets, Collecting French Antique
