Masashi Hirose

Masashi Hirose is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Production Baobab. On September 2014, Hirose was hospitalized for an unspecified illness. He is known for the roles of Ramba Rai in Mobile Suit Gundam and Nakano-sensei in Lovely Complex.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 6, 1947
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Skill: Judo

Character Voiced by Masashi Hirose

Kurt Pokemon Kurt is a Poke Ball smith who lives in Azalea Town. He is Maizie's grandfather. He oftens works extensive hours to meet customers needs. He enjoys his work and pay attention to detail. Despite his gruff nature, he is generous individual.
