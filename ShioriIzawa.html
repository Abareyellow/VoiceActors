Shiori Izawa

Shiori Izawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Early Wing. Izawa graduated from Nippon College and entered the A&G Academy Seiyuu training school in third graduating class. She was a part of Kaleidoscoped in 2009 but Early Wing in 2010. She voiced Miu Hayama in Seiren, Made in Abyss in Nanachi, and Matsuri Mizusawa in Citrus.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 1, 1987
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Illustration, camera, blog, and reflexology
