Daisuke Hirose

Daisuke Hirose is a Japanese actor and voice actor. In 2012, he made his first voice actor role as Riku Kaneda in Litchi DE Hikari Club.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 3, 1991
  • Hobbies: Games and Dance

Leonhard von Glanzreich

Leonhard von Glanzreich is the fourth prince of the Kingdom of Granzreich. He seems to be a haughty and vain young man who looks down on commoners. In reality, he is actually awkward and constantly beating himself up for small issues. He apply himself given the right encouragement, and has a strong sense of justice. He is more like a tsundere but a crybaby. He is honest, tending to say blunt or rude comments without thinking. Leonhard is more serious about athletics. He least likely able to survive as a commoner.
