Kenji Nojima

Kenji Nojima is a Japanese voice and singer. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He is one of the sons of Akio Nojima and younger brother of Hirofumi Nojima. He is married to Chie Sawaguchi and they have to 2 kids. He voiced Fumito Nanahara in Blood-C, Masaki in Time of Eve and Nobuchika Ginoza in Psycho - Pass.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 16, 1976
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Brother: Hirofumi Nojima
  • Wife: Chie Sawaguchi
