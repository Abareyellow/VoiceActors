Yuya Asato

Yuya Asato is a Japanese stage, film, TV, and voice actor. He is affiliated with Ruby Parade. After Asato graduated high school, he enrolled into a preparatory school. Asato quitted midway through to move to Tokyo. In 2011, he joined Ruby Parade after being introduced to an event company president. Since joining Ruby Parade, he has appeared in various type of musicals and stage plays adaptations such as Kuroke's Basketball and Osomatsu-san on Stage: Six Men's Show Time. In 2017, he debut as a voice acting role as Kai von Granzreich in The Royal Tutor along with protraying him in the stage adaptation and theatrical.
