Naoya Uchida

Naoya Uchida is a Japanese voice actor, actor, and singer. He acted in theater and song in opera before the he started acting. He is well known for his role of Madara Uchida in Naruto. Uchida played Tatsuya Midorikawa/DenziGreen in Denshi Sentai Denziman and Kukuruga in Uchu Sentai Kyuranger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 1, 1953
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Singing
  • Wife: Misao Arauchi
