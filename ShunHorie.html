Shun Horie

Shun Horie is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Pro-Fit.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 25, 1993
  • Birthplace: Osaka Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Walking, and Writing Poetry
  • Skill: Speed Reading

Characters Voiced by Shun Horie

Tsugiyoshi Sumino Juuni Taisen Tsugiyoshi Sumino or Nezumi is the Warrior of the Rat. He kills by swarms.

He is very confident in his abilities and enjoys making bets with Tamiel. He displayed no fear in battle. He is a person of honor who is not afraid of standing his will. Unlike Tarmiel, he is calm enough to fight aganist his comrades.
