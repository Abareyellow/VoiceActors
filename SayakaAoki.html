Sayaka Aoki

Sayaka Aoki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Bell Production. She voiced Miria Harvent in Baccano! and Kinu Fukushima in Lume-iro Senkitan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 19, 1972
  • Hometown: Akita, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading and Skating
  • Skills: Blind Touch, Making Pastry, Piano and Tai Chi

Character Voiced by Sayaka Aoki

Miria Harvent
  • Baccano!
  • Baccano! Specials
Miria Harvent is one half of the nonsensical thief duo who are completely immortal. She is partner and lover of Isaac Dian. She wholehearted believes and supports Issac in any form. Just like Isaac, she is whimsical, charismatic, oblivious and spreads happiness to everyone. She loves to meet the people and always believe in them. She and Isaac will put themselves in danger for their friends. She is extraordinarily kind people at her core, with or without Isaac.
