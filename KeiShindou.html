Kei Shindou

Kei Shindou is a Japanese voice actress. Some of her major roles are Gentoku from Ikki Tousen, Madoka Amano in Beyblade: Metal Fusion, and Naomasa in Horizon in the Middle of Nowhere.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 10, 1984
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Real Name: Kei Saitou

Characters Voiced by Kei Shindou

Assassin of Red
  • Fate/Apocrypha
Assassin of Red is a servant of Shirou as part of the Red Fraction in the Turifas Holy Grail War. Her identity is Semiramis, Queen of Assyria. Assassin is a very enthusiastic person who indulges in luxury. She uses poison as her weapon.
Lillie
  • Pokemon Sun and Moon
Lillie is a student at the Pokemon School on Melemele Island and one of Ash's classmate. She is a polite, innocent and kindhearted from a wealthy family. She cares for and concern for others. She is terrified of physically interacting with Pokemon. Despite her fear, she enjoys studying Pokemon from a researcher's perspective, and comfortable with being around them at a safe distance. She is knowledgeable about the behavior, types, and background of Alolian Pokemon. Lillie has strained relationship with her mother. She enjoys taking photos and sightseeing.
Miette
  • Pokemon XY
  • Pokemon XYZ
Miette is a profession PokePuff chef. She is also a Pokemon Performer and a rival with Serena. Miette has a stong passion for making and seemed to have an opinion of herself. She teased Serena about her crush on Ash.

Pokemon Voiced by Kei Shindou

Chikorita
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Chikorita is a Pokemon from the Johto region. Chikorita is a grass starter for Johto. Chikorita uses its leaf to determine the temperature and humidity of the surrounding air. Kei Shindou voiced Lyra's Chikorita.
