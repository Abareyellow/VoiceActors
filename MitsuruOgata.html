Mitsuru Ogata

Mitsuru Ogata is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. He voiced in The Story of Saiunkoku as Soutaifu and Macross Delta in Berger Stone.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 24, 1961
  • Hometown: Aomori, Japan
  • Hobbies: Bowling and Golf
