Mariko Honda

Mariko Honda is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Amuleto. She voiced Inutan in Etotama, Kurobe in Crossing Time, and Garasu Watari in Journal of the Mysterious Creatures.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 3, 1986
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Calligraphy, Violin, and Illustration
