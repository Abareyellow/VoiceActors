Susumu Chiba

Susumu Chiba is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Office Osawa. He voiced Isao Kondo from Gintama, Lance from Pokemon and Eld Gin from Attack on Titans.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 13, 1970
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball and Bowling

Characters Voiced by Susumu Chiba

Huey Laforet
  • Baccano!
  • Baccano! Specials
Huey Laforet is the father of Chane Laforet and one of Advena Avis immortals. Huey sees everyone as possible test subjects for his experiments. He has an enthralling personality. Huey keeps a pleasant, rather facade when interacting with others. He does not care about people outside of their use to him. He has some guilt about what has happen to his mother and his lover. He had a deep love for alchemy and knowledge, wanting to learn all he could. He is more open with other immortals.
Lance
  • Pokemon
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
Lance is a member of the Kanto's elite four and champion of the Johto region. He is one of the most powerful trainers in the world. He specializes in Dragon-types Pokemon. Lance is a honorable man and will not let anyone not get involved. He and Clair are cousins.

Pokemon Voiced by Susumu Chiba

Blaziken
  • Pokemon Movie 7: Destiny Deoxys
Blaziken is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. It is the final form of Torchic and can mega evolve. Blaziken has strong muscular legs that give it great lower body strength. Susumu Chiba voiced Rafe's Blaziken.
