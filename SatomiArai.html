Satomi Arai

Satomi Arai is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated Yu-Rin Pro. She is married to Yoshimitsu Shimoyama for 4 years unkonwon until 2012. Her son was born in 2012 in January. She created her own voice acting agency.

  • Birthday: July 4, 1980
  • Hobbies: keeping a diary, planting vegetables, traveling, and writing letters
  • Favorite Food: Bananas
  • Husband: Yoshimitsu Shimoyama
