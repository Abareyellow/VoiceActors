Daisuke Namikawa

Daisuke Namikawa is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer from the age of 8. He has voice several roles such as Tsubasa Chronicle as Fai D. Flourite, Black Lagoon as Rokurou Okajima, Bleach as Ulquiorra Cifer and Hunter x Hunter as Hisoka Morow. He is famous for his role of Engine Speedor from Engine Sentai Go - Onger and Cuervo from Uchu Sentai Kyuranger. He created his own talent agent "Stay Luck" after leaving Across Entertainment.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 2, 1976
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Handball
  • Skills: Aikido
