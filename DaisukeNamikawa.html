Daisuke Namikawa

Daisuke Namikawa is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer from the age of 8. He has voice several roles such as Tsubasa Chronicle, Black Lagoon, Bleach and Hunter x Hunter. He famous for his role of Engine Speedor from Engine Sentai Go - Onger and Cuervo from Uchu Sentai Kyuranger. He created his own talent agent "Stay Luck" after leaving Across Entertainment.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 2, 1976
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Handball
  • Skills: Aikido
