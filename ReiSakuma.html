Rei Sakuma

Rei Sakuma or Reiko Sakuma is a Japanese actress. She is affiliated 81 Produce. She is best known for her works in the long running series Soreike! Anpanman and Shampoo in Ranma 1/2.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 5, 1965
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birthname: Reiko Sakuma
