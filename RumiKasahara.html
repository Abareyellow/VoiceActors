Rumi Kasahara

Rumi Kasahara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced Koumi Natsuki in Sakura Dairies, and Kyouko Inaba in Hell Teacher Nube.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 8, 1970
  • Hometown: Niigata, Japan
  • Skills: Beauty Care License and Piano

Characters Voiced by Rumi Kasahara

Luka Pokemon Luka is a girl who is trying to find the Silver Wing like her great-grandfather before her.
Martel Fullmetal Alchemist Martel is one of the Chimeras that joined Greed to get revenge on the military for what they did to them in the 5th Laboratory.
