Shinya Takahashi

Shinya Takahashi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with ProFit. He voiced Koki Mimura from Assassination Classroom, and Mikihiko Kitafawa from High Score.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 5, 1978
  • Hometown: Yamagata, Japan

Character Voiced by Shinya Takahashi

Isao How to Keep a Mummy Isao is a dragon who lived with and befriended Motegi Asa. Out of all the "pets", he is the most mature dragon. He is like a big brother and takes care of others very well. He is very intelligent and able to write phrases in Japanese and English.
