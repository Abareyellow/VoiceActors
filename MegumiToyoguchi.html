Megumi Toyoguchi

Megumi Toyoguchi is a Japanese voice actress and radio personality. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. In elementary school until high school, she was very interested in Dragonball. She became a fan of Toru Furuya, the voice actor of Yamada. In her second year of junior high school, she started have an interest to be a voice actor. Toyoguchi was a student of Akira Kamiya. Her first anime debut as a voice actress was Alice from Alice SOS. As of now, Megumi has voiced Winry Rockbell from Fullmetal Alchemist, Revy from Black Lagoon, and Hikari(Dawn) from Pokemon. She voiced Aqua in Kngdom Hearts by Sleep.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 2, 1978
  • Hometown: Machida, Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Anime and Games
