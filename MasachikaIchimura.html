Masachika Ichimura

Masachika Ichimura is a Japanses actor and voice actor. He studied at Theater Art College in Tokyo after graduating high school. He is best known to western anime fans as the original voice of Mewtwo. He married to singer Ryoko Shinohara. He was the voice of Jack Skellington in Kingdom Hearts series.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 28, 1949
  • Wife: Ryoko Shinohara

Character Voiced by Masachika Ichimura

Mewtwo
  • Pokemon Movie 1: The First Movie
  • Pokemon: Mewtwo Returns
Mewtwo is a Pokemon cloned from Mew. It was created by scientists through manipulation of Mew's DNA. Its creator did not imbue him with compassion, making it one of the most savage Pokemon. Its combat abilities and psychic powers developed to be of the highest level. In order to always fight at full power, it remains motionless to conserve energy.
