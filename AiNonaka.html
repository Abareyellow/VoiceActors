Ai Nonaka

Ai Nonaka is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. In high school in Fukuoka, she was head of the drama club and her ambition was to play male roles in the all-female Takarazuka Revue. Her initial in voice acting after watching YuYu Hakusho. She pursued voice after high school and graduted from Aoni's Seiyu Coaching School. She is currently married to someone outside the voice acting business. Nonaka's most notable roles are Beauty from Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo, Kyoko Sakura from Puella Magi Madoka Magica, and Yukino from Real Drive.
