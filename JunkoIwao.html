Junko Iwao

Junko Iwao is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Her notable roles is Tomoyo Daidouji from Cardcaptor Sakura. She also voices Ceres in Ceres, Celestial Legend and Kikyo in Hunter x Hunter. In video games, she voiced Mint in Tale of Phantasia.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 18, 1970
  • Hometown: Oita, Japan
