Kiyotaka Furushima

Kiyotaka Furushima is a Japanese voice actor. He mainly does many of Pokemon characters in Pokemon. Furushima began voicing characters toward the end of Pokemon Advanced Generation. He voiced Engine Jetras from Engine Sentai Go-Onger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 19, 1979
  • Birthplace: Tochigi, Japan
  • Skills: Calligraphy
