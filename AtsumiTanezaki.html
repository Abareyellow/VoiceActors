Atsumi Tanezaki

Atsumi Tanezaki is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Toritori Office but now represents by Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society. She voiced in animes and video games. Her most notable role is Chise Hatori from The Ancient Magus' Bride.
