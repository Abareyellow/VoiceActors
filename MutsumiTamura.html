Mutsumi Tamura

Mutsumi Tamura is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 19, 1987
  • Birthplace: Aomori Prefecture, Japan
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Mutsumi Tamura

Akira Hayama Food Wars! Akira Hayama is a 92nd Tootsuki Generation and Jun Shiomi's assistant. He is quite the knowledge of usage of spices and is well known for sense of smell and mastery of aroma. He is confident and calm. Mutsumi Tamura voiced the younger Akira Hayama.
Alice The Ancient Magus' Bride Alice is a Sorcerer and Renfred's apprentice. She speaks her mind for the most part. She has a good relationship with Chise.
