Rina Sato

Rina Sato is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is currently married and has a baby boy. She voiced Mikoto Misaka in A Certain Magical Index, and Makoto Niijima in Persona 5.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 2, 1981
  • Birthplace: Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Archery

Characters Voiced by Rina Sato

Matrona
  • The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments
Matrona is a former warrior chief of the Giant Clan. She is the mentor of Diane and Dolores. She is a proud giantess but a kind and caring individual.
Shura Kirigakure
  • Blue Exorcist
  • Blue Exorcist: Kuro's Trip Away From Home
  • Blue Exorcist Movie
  • Blue Exorcist: Kyoto Saga
Shura Kirigakure is an Upper-First class Exorcist and Rin Okumura's mentor. She was trained under Shiro Fujimoto to obtain a Meister as a Knight. She works as an inspector for the Vartican. Shura has a laid-back personality and uses slang. She is blunt and speaks freely about sensitive topics. Even with her superiors, she is not afraid to challenge them. She is a very comptent Exorcist. She is rather self-concious about her true age. Later, she becomes open about her feelings.
Summer
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Summer is a maid at the Maid Cafe outside of Veilstone City. She is sister to Spring and Autumn. Like her sisters, she is kind and has a Miltank.
