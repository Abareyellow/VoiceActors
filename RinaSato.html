Rina Sato

Rina Sato is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is currently married and has a baby boy. She voiced Mikoto Misaka in A Certain Magical Index, and Makoto Niijima in Persona 5.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 2, 1981
  • Birthplace: Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Archery

Character Voiced by Rina Sato

Matrona The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments Matrona is a former warrior chief of the Giant Clan. She is the mentor od Diane and Dolores. She is a proud giantess but a kind and caring individual.
