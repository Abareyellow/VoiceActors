Chie Koujiro

Chie Koujiro is a Japanese voice actress. She is now affiliated with 81 Produce. She is well known for her role of Lisa Simpson in the Japanese dubbing of The Simpson.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 17, 1960
  • Birthplace: Shimonoseki, Japan
  • Hobbies: Japanese Dance

Character Voiced by Chie Koujiro

Otaka
  • Yes! Pretty Cure 5
  • Yes! Pretty Cure 5 GoGo!
Otaka is the mistress of the cafeteria in girls' school where they always eat lunch. She is a cheerful, middle-aged woman who sometimes gives them good advice.
