Wasabi Mizuta

Wasabui Mizuta is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced Doreaman starting in the 2005 series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 4, 1974
  • Hometown: Mie, Japan
  • Skills: Kansai Dialect
