Chiaki Kobayashi

Chiaki Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced Kyoichi Sano in Boogiepop and Others, Tony Freyd in Wise Man's Grandchild, and Hiroto Kuga in Gundam Build Divers Re:RISE.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 4, 1994
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
