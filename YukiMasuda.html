Yuki Masuda

Yuki Masuda is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She voiced Yuri Sakakibara in Sakura Wars and Hibiki Amawa in I Me My! Strawberyy Eggs.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 9, 1973
  • Hometown: Shizuoka, Japan
