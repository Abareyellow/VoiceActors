Toshiyuki Morikawa

Toshiyuki Morikawa is a Japanese voice actor and singer who is the head of Axlone. He attended Katsuta Voice Actor's Academy with Kotono Mitsuishi, Chisa Yokoyama, Wataru Takagi and Michiko Neya. He voiced Dante in Devil May Cry, Naraku in InuYasha and Tyki Mikk in D.Gray-man.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 26, 1967
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball, Cruising, Doing Pushup, Drinking, Painting Outdoors, Soccer, and Walking His Dog
