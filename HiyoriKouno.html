Hiyori Kouno

Hiyori Kouno is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with First Wind Production. She voiced Kon Akitsune from Anima Yell! and Pochi in Death March to The Parallel World Rhapsody.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 21, 1998
  • Hometown: Yamanashi, Japan
  • Hobby: Reading

Character Voiced by Hiyori Kouno

Phil The Promised Neverland Phil is an orphan who resided in the Grace Field House. Despite his young age, he is extremely observant and intelligent. He alos figured out something was off about the orphanage. He is a very bright, cheerful and outspoken boy. He loves his family very much and gets along with Emma, Sherry and Carol. He looks up to Emma.
