Miyako Endo

Miyako Endo is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She graduated from Aoyama Gakuin University.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 21, 1965

Characters Voiced by Miyako Endo

Hiromi Matsuno
  • Sailor Moon
Hiromi Matsuno is animator who works on the Sailor V anime. She is best friends with Kazuko Tadashita.
Unazuki Furuhata
  • Sailor Moon R
  • Sailor Moon S
  • Sailor Moon Super S
Unazuki Furuhata is the sister of Motoki Furuhata. She works as a waitress at the Crown Fruit Parlor.
