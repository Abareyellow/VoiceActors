Kana Ueda

Kana Ueda is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Ueda is a graduate of Koba College's department of General Culture. She is affiliated I'm Enterprise. She voices Fate/Stay Night series and Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 9, 1980
  • Birthplace: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Games and Mahjong
