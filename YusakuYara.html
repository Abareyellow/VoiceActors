Yusaku Yara

Yusaku Yara is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Vi-Vo. He is known voicing Saint Seiya as Sagittarius, Wicked City as Renzaburo and Snatcher as Gillian Seed.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 15, 1948
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobby: Golf
