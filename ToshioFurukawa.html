Toshio Furukawa

Toshio Furukawa is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and narrator. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He is currently married Shino Kakinuma. He has appeared at Anime Expo as a guest in 2011. He perfomanced in a bank named Slpastick with Toru Furuya, Kazuyuki Sogabe, Yuji Mitsuya and Akio Nojima. He owns a pet dog named Asuma who is a Shih Tzu and had owned a Ataru named Ataru Moroboshi. he is famous for his roles of Piccolo in Dragon Ball, Kai Shiden in Mobile Suit Gundam, and Shin in Fist of the North Star. His debut voice role is a soldier in Brave Raideen.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 16, 1946
  • Hometown: Tochigi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Action Figures, Making Dioramas, Reading and Sports Fishing
