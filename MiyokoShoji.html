Miyoko Shoji

Miyoko Shoji is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Jac in Production.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 12, 1936
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Miyoko Shoji

Clara Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Clara is an older woman who lives in a cottage. She is friends with a Turtwig that she met when she was in need.
