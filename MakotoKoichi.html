Makoto Koichi

Makoto Koichi is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced in Yuka Tokitate in I'm Standing on 1,000,000 Lives, Kufo in By Grace of God, and Rook Holmes in Infinite Dendrogram.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 17
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Choreography

Character Voiced by Makoto Koichi

Yuka Tokitate I'm Standing on a Million Lives Yuka Tokitate is an otaku and live streamer who got whisk away to another world. Yuka can be opinionated, which get her bullied sometimes.
