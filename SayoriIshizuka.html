Sayori Ishizuka

Sayori Ishizuka is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. She voiced Marsha in Drifters, and Shiina in Sekirei.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 30, 1977
  • Hometown: Shizouka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Playing Drums, Watching Stage and Watching Videos

Character Voiced by Sayori Ishizuka

Luigi Gosick Luigi is an orphan with a very sharp memory. He is a witness of the disappearance of young girls of Jeanton Department Store. He uses abilities to earn money for food.
