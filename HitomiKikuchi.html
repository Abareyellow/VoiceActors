Hitomi Kikuchi

Hitomi Kikuchi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aksent.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 13
  • Birthplace: Shizuoka, Japan

Character Voiced by Hitomi Kikuchi

Lana Pokemon Sun and Moon Lana is a student at the Pokemon School on Melemele Island and one of Ash's classmate. She is a calm and gentle girl who cares for Water-type Pokemon. She is highly protective of her Pokemon when they are in danger. When she was younger, she was shy. She enjoys many seafaring activities and she is an expert at fishing. She tends to get annoyed and flustered by her sisters and their antics. She has extrardinarily high courage and quite athletic. She has a playful side to her. She is good friends with Mallow.
