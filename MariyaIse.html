Mariya Ise

Mariya Ise is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Across Entertainment. She voiced Reg from Made in Abyss, Bonnie from Pokemon XY, and Levy McGarden from Fairy Tail.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 25, 1988
  • Bithplace: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobby: Watching Movies
