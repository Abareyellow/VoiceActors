Hisako Tojo

Hisako Tojo is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated withEarly Wing. Under the influence of an older sister loved anime and manga, she aim to a voice actress. When she was a junior high school, she played a famous role in a cultural festival drama for three years. This increased her interest in the play but still love to be a voice actress which last up to now. After graduating, she land an audition at Early Wing training school.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 21, 1990
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Favorite Food: Eggs and Chicken

Character Voiced by Hisako Tojo

Sayuri I'm Standing on a Million Lives Sayuri is an old friend of Iu Shindou. She is a nice person.
