Rika Kinugawa

Rika Kinugawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with With Line. Some of her major roles are Takako Chino from Orange, Iona from Death March to the Parallel World Rhapsody, and Liddy Steinberg from Hundred.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 17, 1993
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Aikido

Takako Chino
