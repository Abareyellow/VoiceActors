Orie Kimoto

Naoko Yabuta or Orie Kimoto is a Japanese voice actress and actress. She is well known for her role as Saki Hyuuga/Cure Bloom from Pretty Cure Splash Star.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 26, 1979
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Games, Piano and Tennis
