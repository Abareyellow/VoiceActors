Yukana Nogami

Yukana Nogami is a Japanese voice and singer. She is affiliated with Sigma Seven. Her major roles are Azuki in Azuki-chan, Meiling Li in Cardcaptor Sakura, C.C. in Code Geass and Kanna in Inuyasha. She voiced in Kamen Rider OOO as Mezool.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 6, 1975
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
