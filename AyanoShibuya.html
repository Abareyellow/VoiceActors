Character Voiced by Ayano Shibuya

Mimo Pokemon Sun and Moon Mimo is Kiawe's younger sister and the daughter of Sima and Rango. She gets annoyed whenever he is concerned about her safety. She views Kiawe as overprotective but still loves a lot. She is close with Kiawe's Trutonator.
