Masami Kikuchi

Masami Kikuchi is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He was once apart of Kenyu Office but now he is a freelance. He voiced Keiichi Morisato in Ah! My Goddess and Flemming in Marie & Gali.

  • Birthday: April 24, 1960
  • Hometown: Nagano, Japan
