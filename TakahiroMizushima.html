Takahiro Mizushima

Takahiro Mizushima is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with AXL-One. He voiced Son Shin in Soul Buster, and Torlie in Puzzle & Dragons X.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 14, 1976
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
