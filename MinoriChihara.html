Minori Chihara

Minori Chihara is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Horipro. In 2013, She maded her own company named M-Peace. She is well known for her roles of Yuki Nagato in The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya and Leon in The iDOLM@STER.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 18, 1980
  • Hometown: Utsunomiya, Tochigi Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Minori Chihara

Nami Full Metal Panic! The Invisible Victory Nami is a leader of a small local AS league. She is the owner of the RK-91 Savage that Sousuke pilots. She excelled at programming Arm Slve operating systems and one of people with the Whispered ability.
