Sachie Hara

Sachie Hara is a Japanese actor, model and voice actress. She is affiliated with Ken-On Group. In 2002, Hara was cast as Miyuki Kobayakawa in the live action version of Taiho Shichauzo. She made her first voice acting performance in Toki wo Kakeru Shoujo in 2006.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 1, 1978
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
