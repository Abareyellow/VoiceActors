Risa Taneka

Risa Taneka is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. Taneda put her career on hiatus because of treatment for her throat. A couple of voice actress took over her upcoming roles for a while. Some of her major roles are Mirai Kuriyama in Beyond the Boundary, Kaori Miyazono in Your Lie in April and Erina Nakiri in Food Wars.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 12, 1988
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Risa Taneka

Erina Nakiri
  • Food Wars!
  • Food Wars! OVA
  • Food Wars! The Third Plate
Erina Nakiri is a 92nd Generation Tootsuki Culinary Academy student. She is well known base on her ability taste food and judgement on the food. She is a typical rich girl but lacks normal teenage girl behavior. She lack any common sense since everything has not been done by her for years. Her assitant and friend is Hisako Arato.
Kaori Miyazono
  • Your Lie in April
  • Your Lie in April OVA
Kaori Miyazono is a free-spirited violinist. She is independent, unpredictable, and short-tempered. She can be aggressive at times but supports others. Her music style and performance are free spirited. She used to play the piano when she was little.
Yatorishino Igsem
  • Alderamins on the Sky
Yatorishino Igsem is a top and popular at the academy. She has great combat skills and sword play. She is untouchable when she uses sual blades. She is loyal and undefeated. She and Ikta Solork are childhood friends.
