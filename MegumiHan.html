Megumi Han

Megumi Han is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is employed by Atomic Monkey. Keiko Han is her mother. Before she was a voice actress, she acted in live action drama and films. Her debut was in Hunter X Hunter(2011). Hunter x Hunter is the first manga series Han brought while she was in elementary school. She voiced Kyumemon Shingetsu Kibaoni in Shuriken Sentai Ninninger. Han played Luna Kokanoe/MidoNinger in Come Back! Shuriken Sentai Ninninger Ninnin Girls vs. Boys Final Wars.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 3, 1989
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Mother: Keiko Han
