Saori Hayami

Saori Hayami is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is represnted I'm Enterprise. Hayami was interest in voice acting when she was in elementary school. In middle school, she attended voice acting school. She passed audition for I'm Enterprise in 2006. Her debut was Momoka Kawakabe in Touka Gettan. Her major roles are in Urara from Yamada-kun and the Seven Witches, Shouko Nisimiya in A Silent Voice, and Ayase Aragaki in My Little Sister Can't Be This Cute.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 29, 1991
  • Real Name: Saori Matsuo
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
