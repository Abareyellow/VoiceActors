Sayuri Yahagi

Sayuri Yahagi is a Japanese voice actress and narrator for Animax. As a child, she thought "Anime is a talking picture" and did not know what voice acting was until her friend talked to a voice actor. In Junior High, she want to become a seiyu after she told her friend she "sounds like anime." Due to no theater club in the school, she joined the softball club because of her father. Her debut role as a seiyu was Karin Maaka in Karin. She got married on January 31, 2015.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 22, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
