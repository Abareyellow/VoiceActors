Kanata Hongo

Kanata Hongo is a Japanese actor and model. He is affiliated with Starburst Talent agency. He has appeared in several times in all different medias such as music videos, movies, TV series, and magazines. His well known roles in the movie world is Ryoma Echizen in Prince of Tennis, Envy in Fullmetal Alchemist and Shin in Nana 2.
