Hidenobu Kiuchi

Hidenobu Kiuchi is a Japanese voice actor. In 2008, he was the first Japanese voice actor to guest at an anime convention in Australia. He voiced Paul Rudd(Ant-Man) in Japanese dub of Ant-Man movies. His well known anime roles are Hei in Darker Than Black, Shisui Uchiha in Naruto Shippuden and Jose in Gunslinger Girl.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 5, 1983
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Camping and Traveling
