Kouichi Hashimoto

Kouichi Hashimoto is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with CVTech and once affiliated with Arts Vision, Aksent, Ken Production, and Art 7. He is known for his unusual trembling and cold voice.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 12, 1953
  • Birthplace: Chiba Prefecture, Japan
