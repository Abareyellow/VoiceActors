Emiri Katou

Emiri Katou is a Japanese voice actress and singer working for 81 Produce. She has won award at both 2nd and 6th Seiyu Awards. She won Best New Actress, Best Singing Award and Best Supporting Actress. She created a singing duo with Koari Fukuhara called Kato*Fuku. The duo has been disbanded in 2016.
