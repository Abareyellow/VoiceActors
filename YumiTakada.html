Yumi Takada

Yumi Takada is a veteran Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Midori Yoshinaga from Crayon Shin-chan and Aeka Jurai Masaki from Tenchi in Tokyo.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 21, 1961
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading and Watching Movies

Character Voiced by Yumi Takada

Vivian Meridian Pokemon Advanced Generations Vivian Meridian is the Hoenn Contest host. She is the older sister of Lilian, the Kanto Contest host. She enjoys being the host of the contest. She hosts every competition in a formal and ceremonious way.
