Ao Takahashi

Ao Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Kenyu Office. She is married to Shinnosuke Tachibana.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 8, 1979
  • Birthname: Hiroko Takahashi
  • Birthplace: Saitama Prefecture, Japan
  • Husband: Shinnosuke Tachibana

Characters Voiced by Ao Takahashi

Matilda Simmons
  • Black Butler
Matilda Simmons is a nun in a cult that resides in an abandoned Catholic monastery in Preston, Lancashire.
Noriko Paku
  • Blue Exorcist
  • Blue Exorcist: Kuro's Trip Away From Home
  • Blue Exorcist Specials
Noriko Paku is a student of the True Cross Academy and former student of it's cram school. She is soft-spoken, considerate, and has a benevolent nature. She is not confident in her capabilities as an Exorcist. She has a strong sense of justice when it comes to friendship.
