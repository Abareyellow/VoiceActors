Satsuki Yukino

Yuki Inoue or Satsuki Yukino is a Japanese voice actress. She was apart of Ken Production until 2016. She voiced Kagome Higurashi from InuYasha series, Kaname Chidori in Full Metal Panic series and Milly Thompson in Trigun.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 25, 1970
  • Birth Name: Yuki Inoue
  • Hometown: Shiga, Japan
