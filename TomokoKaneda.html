Tomoko Kaneda

Tomoko Kaneda is a Japanese voice actress and radio personality. She is affiliated with Aoni Production until she switched Across Entertainment. She graduated from Department of Architecture, Kanto Gakuin University. She generally voiced young child due to her high-pitched voice. She is marries to Wataru Mori and had her first child in July 13, 2017. She voiced Renge Inui in Air Master, Alice Twins in Eternal Alice, and Ebi in Girls Bravo.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 29, 1973
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Favorite Color: Blue
