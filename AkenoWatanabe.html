Akeno Watanabe

Akeno Watanabe is a japanese actress and voice actress affiliated with Office Osawa. She did Japanese Dubbing for Vida Rocca from Power Rangers Mystic Forces. She worked in animes such as Tales of Rebirth, Negima, Super Robot Wars, Witch Hunter Robin and Code Geass.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 18, 1982
  • Hometown: Funabashi, Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading and Writing
  • Likes: Games and Screenplays
