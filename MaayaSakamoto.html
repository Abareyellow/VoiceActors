Maaya Sakamoto

Maaya Sakamoto is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Sakamoto grew up with her parents and her brother. She started working as a voice actress in 1993 OVA Little Twins. Her single debut released on April 24, 1996 in The Vision of Escaflowne. In October 2016, Sakamoto debuted as a radio host presenting her own program, Sakamoto Maaya no Naisho-banashi, on radio station Nock 5. She has a Bachelor's degress in Sociology. On August 8, 2011, she married Kenichi Suzumura. A couple of Maaya's roles are Aura in .hack series, Motoko Kusanagi in Ghost in the Shell and Ciel Phantomhive in Black Butler series.
