Misato Fukuen

Misato Fukuen or Misato Sakuragi is a Japanese voice actress and actress. she is affiliated with Sigma Seven. She is also a singer who goes by the named MIRI. Her first role was Eve from Black Cat.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 10, 1982
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
