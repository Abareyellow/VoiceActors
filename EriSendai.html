Eri Sendai

Eri Sendai is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with Amuleto. Some her major roles were in Yes! Pretty 5, Yu-Gi-Oh! GX and Rocket Girls

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 30, 1981
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Jazz Dancing and Soft Tennis
