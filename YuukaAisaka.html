Yuuka Aisaka

Yuuka Aisaka is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Apte Pro. She voiced in animes such as Recovery of MMO Junkie and Sakura Trick.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 5, 1990
  • Birthplace: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Yuuka Aisaka

Lilac
  • Recovery of an MMO Junkie
  • Recovery of an MMO Junkie Special
Lilac is a little maniac sorcerer who loves cat ears. She is very open and leaks secrets easily. She is an university student in real life.
