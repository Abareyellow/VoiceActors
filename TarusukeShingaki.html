Tarusuke Shingaki

Tarusuke Shingaki is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Mausu Production. He voiced Kariya Mato in Fate/Zero series and Togusa in Ghost in the Shell series.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 18, 1976
  • Hometown: Okinawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Singing and Skiing
  • Skill: Guitar
