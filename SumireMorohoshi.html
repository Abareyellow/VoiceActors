Sumire Morohoshi

Sumire Morohoshi is a Japanese voice actress and actress. She is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group since the age of 3. She made her debut role in Red Garden as the voice of Carrie Sheedy. Sumire voiced the younger Ana in the Japanese dub of Frozen.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 23, 1999
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Skills: Reading and Illustration
