Sumire Morohoshi

Sumire Morohoshi is a Japanese voice actress and actress. She is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group since the age of 3. She made her debut role in Red Garden as the voice of Carrie Sheedy. Sumire voiced the younger Ana in the Japanese dub of Frozen.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 23, 1999
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Skills: Reading and Illustration

Characters Voiced by Sumire Morohoshi

Nina Tucker Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood Nina Tucker was the daughter of Shou Trucker. She was happy, cheerful and excitable young girl.
Stella Barklem The Ancient Magus' Bride Stella Barklem is young friend of Chise Hatori and older sister of Ethan Barklem. She is very knowledgable and mature for her young age and is talented at baking sweets.
