Masako Nozawa

Masako Nozawa is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She has been affiliated Production, 81 Produce, and Office Nozawa(founder). She is currently affiliated with Aoni Production. Nozawa made her movie debut as a child actor at the age of 3. She has performed many male roles such as Son Goku in Dragon Ball. She has achieved two Guinnes World Records for her role of Son Goku. She was the origial voice of Doraemon from Doraemon. She was married to Masaaki Tsukada until Tsukada death.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 25, 1936
  • Birthplace: Arakawa, Tokyo, Japan
  • Husband: Masaaki Tsukada
