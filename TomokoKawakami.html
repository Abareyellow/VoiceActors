Tomoko Kawakami

Tomoko Kwawkami was a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Production Baobab before her death. Kawakami was diagnosed with ovarican cancer, which required surgery. After a three year battle with cancer, she died on June 9, 2011 at the age of 41. Her debut was a boy in Metal Fighter Miku. Some of her major her major are Utena Tenjou in Revolutionary Girl Utena, and Misuzu Kamio in Air.
