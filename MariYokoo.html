Mari Yokoo

Mari Yokoo is a Japanese voice actress.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 21, 1952
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Mari Yokoo

Fumio Daimidou
  • Food Wars!
  • Food Wars! The Second Plate
  • Food Wars! The Third Plate
  • Food Wars! The Third Plate: Totsuki Train Arc
Fumio Daimidou is the Dorm Manager of Polar Star Dormitory. She care gently for the students that lives in her dormitory. She is quite benevolent and patient but exasperated by the mayhem that residents cause. She has a great pride over the Polar Star.
Katie
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
Katie is an older woman who makes medicines by using the electric powers of her her Mareep. She runs a pharmacy with the help of her granddaughter, Mariah, and Mariah's friend, McCauley. She does not sell medicine not made right.
