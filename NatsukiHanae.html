Natsuki Hanae

Natsuki Hanae is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Across Entertainment. When he was high school, he disliked studying so he did not go to college. He thought about taking his hobby of singing into a career, but aspired to be a voice actor after watching Ouran High School Host Club on TV. He sent voice sample to Across Entertaniment through their website and then joined November 2009. He made his debut as a voice actor in 2011. He got married on August 27, 2016.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 26, 1991
  • Hometown: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke and Gaming
