Houko Kuwashima

Houko Kuwashima is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is currently affiliated with Aoni Production. She plays a variety of characters. Her major role is Yurika Misumaru in Martain Successor Nadesico. She started her career in the Mid - 90s. She has qute the pairing together with Kotono Mitasuishi. She is good friends with Haruna Ikezawa.
