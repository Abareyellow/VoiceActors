Yuko Miyamura

Yuko Miyamura or Yuko Seki is a Japanese voice actress, singer and sound director. Miyamura graduated from the theater division of the Toho Gakuen College of Drama and Music. Yuko was diagnosed with Graves' disease in May 2007 after suffering exophthalmos. She married to manga artist Nakatani D. and stuntman Takaya Seki until she serepated divorce. She has two from her marriage with Seki: One daughter and one son. She is well known from the role of Asuka Langley Soryu from Neon Genesis Evangelion.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 4, 1972
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
