Shinnosuke Tachibana

Shinnosuke Tachibana is a Japanese voice actor, singer and narrator. He voiced Tomeo from Kamisama Kiss, Yoshitsune Hiiragi from Big Order and Shinichi Tamaki from Yamada-kun and the Seven Witches.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 26, 1978
  • Hometown: Gifu, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cycling, Darts, Karaoke, and Personal Computer
  • Skills: Miming, and writing acrostic peom
  • Wife: Ao Takahashi
