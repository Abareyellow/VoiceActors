Mei Nagano

Mei Nagano is a Japanese actress. She was scouted her third year of elementary school. Her debut roles was in Hard Revenge Milly Bloody Battle. Her roles relate to live action version of the manga are Rinko Yamato in My Love Story! and Sae Kashiwagi in Peach Girl.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 24, 1999
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Guitar, Running and Taking Pictures
  • Skills: Impersonations and Sports

Character Voiced by Mei Nagano

Astrid Ni no Kuni Astrid is the princess of Evermore and is Kotona Takashina's counterpart in Haru's world. She is a kind down to Earth girl, who wants to have real friends. She has a strong sense of justice and cares for her kingdom.
Kotona Takashima Ni no Kuni Kotona Takashima is popular and innocent high school student living in Tokyo. Her boyfriend is Haruto Ichihara and friends with Yusuke Ninomiya. She is sometimes jealous of Haru's fangirls.
