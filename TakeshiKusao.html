Takeshi Kusao

Takeshi Kusao is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and singer. He is afffiliated with Aoni Production. He is married to Yuka Saito. His major roles are Trunks in Dragon Ball Z and Mao in Code Geass.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 20, 1965
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Volleyball and Swimming
