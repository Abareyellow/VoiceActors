Tetsuya Kakihara

Tetsuya Kakihara is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He was previously affiliated with 81 Produce then became a freelancer. He created the agency called Zynchro. He was fascinated by its entertainment culture that he attended Amusement Media Academy. His major roles are Yutaka Mikoto in Princess Princess, and Natsu Dragneel from Fairy Tail.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 24, 1982
  • Hometown: Dusseldorf, Germany
  • Languages: German, English, and Japanese
  • Nickname: Kakkii
  • Hobbies: Snowboarding
  • Skills: Magic Tricks and Social Dancing
