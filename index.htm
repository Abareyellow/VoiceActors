Welcome to JVoiceActor!

This website is dedicated to learning about your favorite seiyu in the world of anime. Each voice actor has a page dedicated to them with description, Small facts about them, links to their Twitters, Blogs and Webpage. Their is a list of some characters that they voiced or currently voicing. Some people have one, two or four characters on their page. These are the author of this website favorite characters.
