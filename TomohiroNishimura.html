Tomohiro Nishimura

Tomohiro Nishimura is a Japanese voice actor, actor, and singer-songwriter. He is affiliated with 81 Produce, but now freelancer. He voiced Arumajiro in Silk Road Kids, and IR in Corrector Yui.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 2, 1961
  • Hometown: Asahikawa, Hokkaido, Japan

Character Voiced by Tomohiro Nishimura

Michio Tsukui Juuni Taisen Michiro Tsukui or Dotsuku is the warrior of the Dog. He kills by biting his enemies. He works as a daycare teacher.
