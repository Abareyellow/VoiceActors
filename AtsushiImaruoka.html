Atsushi Imaruoka

He did dubbing for American TV shows and movies such as Soos from Gravity Falls, Antonio Graiz Power Rangers Samurai and Luiz from Rio. He has been in Tokusatsu such as Engine Sentai Go-Onger, Tensou Sentai Goseiger, Kamen Rider OOO, Resha Sentai ToQger and Shuriken Sentai Ninniger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 23, 1972
  • Birthplace: Isesaki, Gunma, Japan
  • Hobbies: Boxing
