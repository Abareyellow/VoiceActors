Chie Satou

Chie Satou is a Japanese voice actress. She has does various characters on Pokemon and Bo-chan from Crayon Shin-chan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 1, 1964
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Diving, Japanese dance and Skiing
