Saori Oonishi

Saori Oonishi is a Japanese voice actress. Oonishi affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She began to have inspiration from Maaya Sakamoto and Sanae Kobayashi. Saori is a graduate of Nihon Narration Engi Kenyuujo In high school, she prefer to attend a voice acting training school. Her debut role was La Folia Rihavein in Strike the Blood. A couple others are Aiz Warenstein in Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? and Kuro in Restaurant to Another World.
