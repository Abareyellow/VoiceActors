Taku Yashiro

Taku Yashiro is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with VIMS agency. He voiced Brown Bressin The Thousand Noble Musketeers and Natsuo Fujii in Domestic Girlfriend.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 3, 1993
  • Hometown: Iwate, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball, Piano, and Badminton
