Kazuhiro Yamaji

Kazuhiro Yamaji is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with the Seinenza Theater Company. He is famous for official dubbing roles for several people across the world. As of 2020, he is currently married to Romi Park. He voiced Kenny Ackerman from Attack on Titan, Jabberwock from Beelzebub, and Uranus Hankai from Gintamia.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 4, 1954
  • Hometown: Mie, Japan
  • Wife: Romi Park
