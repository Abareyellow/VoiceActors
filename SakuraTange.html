Sakura Tange

Sakura Tange is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She worked for Aoni Production and Konami. Tange retired from voice acting for a while since 2000, but came back as a voice actress in September 2009. She released music under several artist names, including Little Seraph, Angelic Alice, Angel and Sakura. Her most notable voice is Sakura Kinomoto in Cardcaptor Sakura.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 24, 1973
  • Birthplace: Ichinomiya, Aichi Prefecture, Japan
