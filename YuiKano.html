Yui Kano

Yui Kano is a Japanese voice actess. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She starred in the live-action film Cat Girl Kiki. She voiced Dalshena Che Matelna in Chrome Shelled Regios and Phantoma in Hells.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 22, 1983
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Flower Arrangement, Koto Performance, Poetry, and Tea Ceremony

Character Voiced by Yui Kano

Cecile Lafitte Gosick Cecile Lafitte is a teacher in St. Marguerite Academy and the homeroom teacher for Kazuya, Victorique and Avril. she possesses a childish, friendly attitude and an interest in the supernatural. She is not strict and very friendly.
