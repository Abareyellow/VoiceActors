Aya Hirano

Aya Hirano is a japanese voice actress and singer. She spents her early years in America before coming to Japan. She appeared in commericals before she got her first role in Angel Tales. After gradulating high school, she begins her voice acting and solo singing career. Her popularity later confirmed her to the first Seiyu Award, Hirano won "Best Newcomer." November 2010, she posted that she had a pituitary gland tumor. She went into surgery, which prohibited her from getting new roles. She returned to music in 2012. In 2016, she become a stint in a musical.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 8, 1987
  • Birthplace: Aichi, Japan
