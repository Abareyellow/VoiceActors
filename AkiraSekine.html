Akira Sekine

Akira Sekine is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aptrpro.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 16
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Akira Sekine

Aa How to Keep a Mummy Aa is an inugami. It is good friends with Uu.
Tazuki Kamiya How to Keep a Mummy Tazuki Kamiya is Sora Kashiwagi's best friend and takes care of Conny. On the outside, he seems to be a cool and organized person. He tends to lose his head when the people he cares about are in danger. He not good with his emotions and he becomes very defensive when he is dealing with new people. He can be clever at times and often thinks and analyzes situation before acting. Despite his appearance, he is very loyal friend and cares about his friends.
