Yui Fukuo

Yui Fukuo is a Japanese voice actress and basketball player. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She was a member of AOP when they won the Special Award at the 10th Seiyu Awards in 2016. In early 2018, she graduate from the group due to her devotion to the voice acting industry. In August 2018, she announced as a member of the Seiyu Jr. Basuke 3x3 SJ3 LEAGUE, a basketball league for voice actors, and played for the Cherubiacchi team. She voiced Linse Silhoueska from In Another World With My Smartphone, Yuika Akitsuki from Minami Kamakura High School Girls Cycling Club, and Rika Hayase from Time Travel Girl.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 25, 1994
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Programming and Hyakunin Isshu
  • Skill: Kansai Dialect

Character Voiced by Yui Fukuo

Cilia By the Grace of the Gods Cilia is a Rank B adventurer rabbit tribe Beastman and friend to Ryoma Takebayashi.
