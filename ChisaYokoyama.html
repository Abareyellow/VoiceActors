Chisa Yokoyama

Chisa Yokoyama is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She affiliated with Art Vision for a while until she created her voice company named Banbina. When she was in high school, she was an assistant of Jump Broadcasting Station. She married musician Chaka from the band Tripolysm. She co - hosts an ongoing radio show with Kappei Yamaguchi and Ouji Hiroi.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 20, 1969
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
