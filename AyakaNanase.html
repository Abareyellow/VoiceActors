Ayaka Nanase

Ayaka Nanase is a Japanese voice actress. She completed her professional training from Axl-Zero and graduated there. She has voiced Rikka Isurugi in Black Fox and Mahiru Shirota in Servamp.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 11, 1994
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
