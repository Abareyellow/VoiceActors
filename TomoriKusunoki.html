Tomori Kusunoki

Tomori Kusunoki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with the Sony Music Artists. Kusunoki became interested in entertainment at an early age: when she was three, she started taking piano lessons. She becames interest in anime in her second year of junior high. She was a fan of Kana Hanazawa's work in Kobato. This gave her idea of becoming a voice actress. Her debut was a background character in Eromanga Sensei.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 22, 1999
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
