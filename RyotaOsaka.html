Ryota Osaka

Ryota Osaka is a Japanese voice actor and radio personality. He is affiliated with Early Wing. He hosts a radio show with Natsuki Hanae called OHana. He voiced Macro Bobt in Attack on Titans and Ryouta Watari in Your Lie in April.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 2, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokushima, Japan
  • Hobbies: Softball, Bowling, Walking and Games

Character Voiced by Ryota Osaka

Ryouta Watari
  • Your Lie in April
  • Your Lie in April OVA
Ryouta Watari is Kousei and Tsubaki's childhood friend and captain of soccer team at their school. He is extremely popular with girls and often adopting a frivolous attitude. Ryouta is a tiny bit of a play-boy.
