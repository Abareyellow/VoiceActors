Ryota Osaka

Ryota Osaka is a Japanese voice actor and radio personality. He is affiliated with Early Wing. He hosts a radio show with Natsuki Hanae called OHana. He voiced Macro Bobt in Attack on Titans and Ryouta Watari in Your Lie in April.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 2, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokushima, Japan
  • Hobbies: Softball, Bowling, Walking and Games

Characters Voiced by Ryota Osaka

Cross
  • Pokemon Movie 20: I Choose You!
Cross is a boy who aims to be the strongest trainer. He is a rival of Ash. He believes in strength is everything and aims to become the ultimate Trainer.
Fugil Arcadia
  • Undefeated Bahamut Chronicles
Fugil Arcadia is believed to be the first prince of the former Old Empire and Lux Arcadia's older half-brother from a different mother. He used to put on the facade of a cheerful young man who cared a lot about his brother Lux. In truth, Fugil is driven by the goal of finding a king who will lead the world correctly. He is a very insecure person. Before meeting Arshalia, he was an empty man who did not care about anything.
Ryouta Watari
  • Your Lie in April
  • Your Lie in April OVA
Ryouta Watari is Kousei and Tsubaki's childhood friend and captain of soccer team at their school. He is extremely popular with girls and often adopting a frivolous attitude. Ryouta is a tiny bit of a play-boy.
