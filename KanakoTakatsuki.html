Kanako Takatsuki

Kanako Takatsuki is a Japanese singer and voice actress. She is affiliated with Amuse. After graduating from middle school, Takatsuki went to Himawari Theater Group where she studied acting. She debuted as asinger in September 2012 with the release of the song "Bonjo Cinderella no Shiruke Aru Onegai." In 2015, Takatsuki began her career as a voice actress in the multimedia project Love Live! Sunshine!! as Hanamaru Kunikida.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 25, 1993
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
