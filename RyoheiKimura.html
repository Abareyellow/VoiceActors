Ryohei Kimura

Ryohei Kimura is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group. He voiced Ryouta Kise in Kuroko's Basketball, Akira Takizawa in Eden of the Eden, and Touji Ato in Tokyo Ravens.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 30, 1984
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
