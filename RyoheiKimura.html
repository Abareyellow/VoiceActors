Ryohei Kimura

Ryohei Kimura is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group. He voiced Ryouta Kise in Kuroko's Basketball, Akira Takizawa in Eden of the Eden, and Touji Ato in Tokyo Ravens.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 30, 1984
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ryohei Kimura

Howzer
  • The Seven Deadly Sins
  • The Seven Deadly Sins OVA
  • The Seven Deadly Sins: Signs of Holy War
  • The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments
Howzer is a Holy Knight, a colleague and partner of Gilthunder. He is very optimistic and has a lot of self confidence. He has easy-going personality but serious when fighting.
Shinjiro Hayata
  • Ultraman
Shinjiro Hayata is the son of Shin Hayata. Due to his father's merge with Ultraman, Shinjiro possesses Ultra Genes that he inherited from his father. He is a somewhat lonely boy with a good heart. He wants to carry his father's legacy of Ultraman.
