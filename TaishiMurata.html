Taishi Murata

Taishi Murata is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Across Entertainment. He voiced Eiichiro Maruo in Baby Steps, and Magee in Gundam Build Drivers.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 13, 1982
  • Hometown: Kochi, Japan
