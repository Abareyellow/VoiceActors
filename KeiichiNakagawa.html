Keiichi Nakagawa

Keiichi Nakagawa is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aksent.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 31, 1980
  • Hometown: Shiga, Japan

Character Voiced by Keiichi Nakagawa

Professor Kukui Pokemon Sun and Moon Professor Kukui is a Pokemon Professor in the Alola region and a teacher at the Pokemon School on Melemele Island. His alter ego is The Masked Royal. He is fairly serious, but friendly and upbeat character. He likes to add the names of moves into conversations. He enjoys watching the television show Alalon Dectective Laki. Kukui is a skilled Pokemon Trainer. He is able to handle battling multiple Pokemon.
