Yukari Tamura

Yukari Tamura is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Amuleto. Her debut as a singer and voice actress in 1997. Her major roles are Nanoha Takamachi in Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha, Mei Sunohara in Clannad and Suzuha Amane in Steins;Gate. Tamura voiced Navi from Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 27, 1976
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Making Ribbons Bows and Sweets, Writing Poems, and Playing Phantasy Star Online
