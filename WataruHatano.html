Wataru Hatano

Wataru Hatano is a a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. Hatano won the Best Male Rookie Award for his roles of Sam Houston in Toward the Terra and Tenshi Yuri in Saint Beast: Kouin Jojishi Tenshitan. He also voiced Gajeel from Fairy Tail. He announced marriage with fellow seiyuu and stage actress Mai Hashimoto on April 2, 2018
