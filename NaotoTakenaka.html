Naoto Takenaka

Naoto Takenaka is a Japanese actor, comedian, singer, and director. He is actor veteran of almost a hundred films, he has been nominated for ten Japanese Academy Awards.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 20, 1956
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Listening to Music and Watching Movies
  • Skills: Art and Singing

Characters Voiced by Naoto Takenaka

Entei Pokemom Movie 3: Spell of the Unknown Entei is a legendary Pokemon from the Johto. It is a member of the Legendary beasts resurrected by Ho-oh. It represent the flames that burned the Brass Tower.
Spencer Hale Pokemom Movie 3: Spell of the Unknown Spencer Hale is a researcher from the town of Greenfield. He has a daughter named Molly. He is good friends with Delia Ketchum and Professor Oak.
