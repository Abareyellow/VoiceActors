Nobuo Tobita

Nobuo Tobita is a voice actor. He affiliated with Arts Vision. He is a graduate of the Department of Literature of Komazawa University where he majored in history. His debut role is Bob from Cybot Robotchi. Tobita is most knoen for the roles of Kamille Bidan in Mobile Suit Zeta Gundam and Albert Heinrich/004 in Cyborg 009.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 6, 1959
  • Hometown: Ibaraki, Japan

Character Voiced by Nobuo Tobita

Brycen Pokemon Black and White Brycen is a Gym Leader of Icirrus City's Gym. He is a famous actor in the Unova region. He specializes in Ice-types Pokemon.
