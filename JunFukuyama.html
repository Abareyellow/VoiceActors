Jun Fukuyama

Jun Fukuyama is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Axlone. He voiced Baron Nero from Ressha Sentai ToQger. Some of his major roles are Lelouch from Code Geass and Koro-sensei from Assassination Classroom.
