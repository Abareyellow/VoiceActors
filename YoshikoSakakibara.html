Yoshiko Sakakibara

Yoshiko Sakakibara is a Japanese voice actress and actress. She is well known for her roles as Queen Nehellenia from Sailor Moon, Dyspear from Go! Princess Precure and Arion Rosemarine from The Peom of Wind and Trees.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 31, 1956
  • Birthplace: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Yoshiko Sakakibara

Kate Valentine Chrono Crusade Kate Valentine is the head mistress of the NY branch of the Magdalan Order. She is a first-class milita and is a strong woman. Valentine lose her patience with Rosette. she is very distrusted of Chrono due to her hatred of devils.
