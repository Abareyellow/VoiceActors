Junko Minagawa

Junko Minagawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. During her childhood, she was actively participated in school plays. Minagwawa played the role of a duck in The Ugly Ducking. During junior high and highschool, Minagawa devoted to volleyball. She quitted due to strict coaching and undermining her social life. After trying to a flight attendant and actress, she became a voice actor because of her interest of manga and anime. Her debut role was Ryoma Echizen in Prince of Tennis.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 22, 1975
