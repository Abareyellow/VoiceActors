Kazuo Hayashi

Kazuo Hayashi is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Theater Echo. He plays multiple recurring villians in the Super Sentai series. He was the voice of Riderman in Super Hero Taisen.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 17, 1945
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Kazuo Hayashi

Captain Aidan
  • Pokemon
Captain Aidan is a firefighter commander from Ascorbia Island. He leads Team Wartortle in rescue missions. He enjoy his line of work because he gets to help people. Aidan is very competitive and his ambition leads him to push his Pokemon to perform their personal bests.
Clow Reed
  • Cardcaptor Sakura
  • Cardcaptor Sakura Movie 1
Clow Reed is the magican who originally created the Clow Cars. He is the master of Keroberos and Yue. He created his own magic by blending both Eastern and Western magical elements.
