Shouta Aoi

Shouta Aoi is a Japanese singer, actor, and voice actor. From elementary to high school, he played tennis and won many trophies. When he was young, he watched a lot of animee and played videos games. His interest in voice acting through Akira Ishida's portrayal of Fish Eye from Sailor Moon SuperS. In school, he was bullied for his high pitched feminine voice. His friends helped him regain his confidence after performancing a song during a karaoke outing. In 2004, he competed in the Yamaha Teens' Music Festival under the name "Noboru Yanagawa" and won the Grand Prixfor the Ibaraki region. He was a finalist at the national competition, along with Ataru Nakamura. He debuted as a voice actor in 2011, voicing Kanshiki in the game Black Robinia. His first major in the anime universe was Ai Mikaze in Uta no Prince series. His first leading role was Ayumu Tamari in the OVA This Suffers From Crystallization. Along with voice acting, he has sung multiple songs for anime like You and Me, The Royal Tutor and Ace of Diamond.
