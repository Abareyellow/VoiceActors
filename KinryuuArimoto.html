Kinryuu Arimoto

Kinryuu Arimoto is a Japanese voice actor. He formerly employed by Production Baobab. He works in animes such as Dr. Knox in Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood, Mei's Granfather in Citrus and Szilard Quates in Baccano!

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 11, 1940
  • Died: February 1, 2019
