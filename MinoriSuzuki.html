Minori Suzuki

Minori Suzuki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with E-Stone Music. She was aspired to be involved in voice work and singing since her childhood. Suzuki would stay up until midnight in order to watch anime. Her mother is a big fan of Macross Frontier. Her influences are Maaya Sakamoto and Megumi Nakajima. Her debut role is Freyja Wion in Macross Delta.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 1, 1997
  • Birthplace: Aichi Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobby: Collecting Moomin Merchandise
