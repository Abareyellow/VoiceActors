Ryoko Shiraishi

Ryoko Shiraishi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. Shiraishi married on September 6, 2012, but divorced in 2013. She voiced Kaeda Nagase from Negima!, and Rina Nishio from Fafner.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 7, 1982
  • Birthplace: Nara, Japan
