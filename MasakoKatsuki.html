Masako Katsuki

Masako Katsuki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. Masako's major roles are Maria von Trapp in Trapp Family Story, Michiru Kaiou/Sailor Saturn in Sailor Moon, and Tsunade in Naruto. She voiced NeziYellow in Denji Sentai Megaranger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 15, 1958
  • Hometown: Hachinohe, Aomori, Japan
  • Birth Name: Masako Shiono
  • Hobbies: golf, knitting, skiing
  • Favorite Colors: pink and black
