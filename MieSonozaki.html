Mie Sonozaki

Mie Sonozaki is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Remax. She is well known for her role of Arle Nadja from the video game series Puyo Puyo.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 7, 1973
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Mie Sonozaki

Hiroko Seto
  • Your Lie in April
  • Your Lie in April OVA
Hiroko Seto is a close family friend of Arima. She was one of Arima Mother's classmates year ago. She is a talented pianist. She is a mother to Koharu Seto.
