Miho Arakawa

Miho Arakawa is a voice actress. She is affiliated Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society. She attended Haikyo Voice Actors Studio and a part of 36th gradulating class in 2009 then started working at Haikyo in 2010. Arakawa was a nurse working in the hospital until 2011. She voiced in Meiko in Digimon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 4, 1987
  • Hometown: Sendai, Miyagi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Swimming

Character Voiced by Miho Arakawa

Concordia Pokemon Black and White: Adventure in Unova Concordia is N's allies and have similar abilities for hearing the voices of Pokemon.
