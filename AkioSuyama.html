Akio Suyama

Akio Suyama is a Japanese voice actor. He affiliated with Sigma Seven. He voices Roger Klotz in the Japanese dub of Doug. He has voiced in Attack on Titan and Detective Conan.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 8, 1968

Character Voiced by Akio Suyama

Petruchio Romeo x Juliet Petruchio is a weak young man who works in one of the Montague's mines and is saved from being crushed by Romeo. He was sent to the mines for stealing bread for his poor family. He is desperate to return to Neo Verona to see his younger siblings.
