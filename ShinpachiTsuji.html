Shinpachi Tsuji

Shinpachi Tsuji is a voice actor. He is affiliated with Liberta. He voiced Daniel O'Connel in Master Keaton and Bason in Shaman King.

  • Birthday: October 26, 1956
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Scuda Driving and Soccer
  • Skill: Kendo
