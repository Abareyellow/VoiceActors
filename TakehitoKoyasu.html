Takehito Koyasu

Takehito Koyasu is a Japanese voice actor. He founded T's Factory in 1998. He is part of the Seiyu group, Weiss. His most notable roles are Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo in Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo, Gastro in Assassination Classroom, and Gin Dojima in Food Wars! Shokugeki no Soma.
