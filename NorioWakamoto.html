Norio Wakamoto

Norio Wakamoto is a Japanese actor, voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated Sigma Seven. After graduating from Waseda University, he employs as a police officer assigned to the Tokyo Metropoliton Police Department Ant-Riot Squad before becoming a voice actor. As a voice actor, he voiced Johnny in Guilty Gears, Cell in Dragon Ball and Charles zi Britannia in Code Geass.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 18, 1945
  • Hometown: Shimonoseki, Yamaguchi, Japan
  • Skills: Kendo and Shorinji Kempo
