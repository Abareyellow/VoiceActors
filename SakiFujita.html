Saki Fujita

Saki Fujita is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is represnted by Arts Vision. She has song Opening and Ending theme with Yuki Makishima, Yukako Yoshikawa, Kana Asumi and Eri Kitamura. She is best known for sampling her voice for Crypton Future Media's Vocaloid, Hatsune Miku.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 19, 1984
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birth Name: Sakiko Fujita
  • Hobbies: Drawing and Playing with Dogs
  • Skills: Calligraphy
