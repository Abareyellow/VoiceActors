Katsuji Mori

Katsuji Mori is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and narrator. He is the founder of Office Mori. He voiced Ken the Eagle from Science Ninja Team Gatchaman, and Go Mifune from Mach GoGoGo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 10, 1945
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobby: Computing

Characters Voiced by Katsuji Mori

Nephrite

Nephrite is the second of the Shitennou from the Dark Kingdom. He is very cocky and very emotional. His alter ego is Masato Sanjourin, a wealthy business man.
