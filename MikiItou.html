Miki Itou

Miki Itou is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. She voiced Cyborg 18 in Dragon Ball GT, Monica in Glass Maiden, and Toko Fujiwara in Natsume's Book of Friends.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 21, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
