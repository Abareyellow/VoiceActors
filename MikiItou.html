Miki Itou

Miki Itou is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. She voiced Cyborg 18 in Dragon Ball GT, Monica in Glass Maiden, and Toko Fujiwara in Natsume's Book of Friends.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 21, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Miki Itou

Aya Yukishiro
  • Pretty Cure
  • Pretty Cure Max Heart
Aya Yukishiro is Honoka's Mother. With her husband Yukishiro Taro, they work overseas as traders who buy and sell jewelry.
Sonomi Daidouji
  • Cardcaptor Sakura
  • Cardcapter Sakura: Clear Card
Sonomi Daidouji is the mother of Sakura's best friend Tomoyo Daidouji, and the first cousin of Sakura's mother Nadeshiko Kinomoto. She is a wealthy woman and runs a large toy corporation. She is very athletic and loves her cousin.
