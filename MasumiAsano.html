Masumi Asano

Masumi Asano is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. Her manga series Seiyu's Life!, with art by Kenjiro Hata, has an anime adaptation. She is a member of the singing group Aice with Yui Horie, Akemi Kanda, Chiaki Takahashi and Madoka Kimura. She is married to Kenjiro Hata.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 25, 1977
  • Hometown: Akita, Japan
  • Husband: Kenjiro Hata

Characters Voiced by Masumi Asano

Claire Pokemon Advanced Generations Claire is a woman that Ash and Co. met on their journey. She currently engaged with a guy named Kain.
Forsythia Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Forsythia is a Pokemon Trainer with a Roserade. She teaches a classes to make Pokemon Poffin with the berries she grows in her orchard.
