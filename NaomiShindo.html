Naomi Shindo

Naomi Shindo is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She is best known for her voice roles as Shizuru Fujino from Mai-hime and Cagalli Yula Athha from Modile Suit Gundam Seed.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 9, 1972
  • Hometown: Kyoto, Japan
  • Hobby: Tennis
