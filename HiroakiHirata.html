Hiroaki Hirata

Hiroaki Hirata is a Japanese voice actor. He is the founder Hirata Production. He is famous for his roles from One Piece, Saiyuki and Tiger & Bunny. He is official dub - over artist for Johnny Depp. He won at the Seiyu Awards for his work on Air Gear, One Piece and Tiger & Bunny.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 7, 1963
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Computers, Fishing, Gardening, Photography, Skiing
