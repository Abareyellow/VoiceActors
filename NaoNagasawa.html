Nao Nagasawa

Nao Nagasawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Kekke Corporation. She was married to Ryotaro Okiayu but now divorced. She voiced Sumeragi Daichi in Beyblade Series and Majorika in Ojamajo Doremi series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 12, 1971
  • Hometown: Hyojo, Japan
  • Hobby: Skuba Diving
  • Skills: Martial Arts, Traditional Japanese Dancing, and Trumpet

Character Voiced by Nao Nagasawa

Samantha Baccano! Samantha is a longstanding servant of Genoard Family. She is vivacious and spirited woman who is far less society reserved.
