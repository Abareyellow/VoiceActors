Ami Koshimizu

Ami Koshimizu is a Japanese voice actress and singer affiliated Axlone Management. She perivously worked with Production Baobab until 2011. She voiced in animes such as Sailor Moon, Spice and Wolf, Code Geass and Dog Days. At the 2014 Anime Expo, Koshimizu was a guest of honor to promote Kill la Kill. She voiced in video games such as Bravely Default and Persona 4.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: Feb 15, 1986
  • Birthplace: Kokubunji, Japan
