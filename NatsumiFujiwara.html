Natsumi Fujiwara

Natsumi Fujwara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Art Vision.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 2
  • Hometown: Shizuka, Japan

Characters Voiced by Natsumi Fujiwara

August Fairy Tail: Final Season August is a general and the strongest male member of the Alvarez Empire's Spriggan 12, the protection guard of Emperior Spriggan. He is well known as the "Wizard King." He rarely expressing strong emotions and offen questioning about parent love. He is very gentle with Brandish and very loyal to Zeref. He takes his position very seriously. Natsumi Fujiwara voiced younger August.
Dimeria Yesta Fairy Tail: Final Season Dimaria Yesta is a member of the Alvarez Empire and served under the Emperor Spriggan as part of his persoanl guard, the Spriggan 12. She is a descendant of the people of Mildian and the chosen vessel of the God of Time, Chronos. A supreme warrior has won several battle named her "Warrior Queen", a goddess of war. She has a relaxed and cynical personality. She shows very little concern for her colleagues' welling being. She is good friend with Brandish.
Keel The Rising of the Shield Hero Keel is a resident of Raphtalia's home village and one of Raphtalia's childhood friends. She can be whiny and bratty sometimes. Despite how she acts, Keel helps bring together the slaves as a leader.
