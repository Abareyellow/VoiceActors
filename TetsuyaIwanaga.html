Tetsuya Iwanaga

Tetsuya Iwanga is a Japanese voice actor. He is a freelance. He voiced Makoto Mizuhara in El Hazard-The Magnificent World and Tomohisa Kaname in Puella Magi Madoka Magica.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 24, 1970
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking and Driving
