Kyouko Hikami

Kyouko Hikami is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. Her major role was the title character Momoko Hanasaki in the anime Wedding Peach. Hikami is a part of voice actress tro Furil which consists of the other main voice mates Yuko Miyamura and Yukana Nogami.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 11, 1969
  • Hometown: Kobe, Hyougo, Japan
  • Skills: Kansai Dialect and Piano
