Eriko Matsui

Eriko Matsui is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with IAM Agency. She voiced Isuzu in Log Horizon and Yurino in Million Doll.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 18, 1989
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan

Character Voiced by Eriko Matsui

Tailor The Rising of the Shield Hero Tailor is the owner of the tailor store. She has a geeky personality.
