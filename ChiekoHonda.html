Chieko Honda

Chieko Honda was a Japanese actress and voice actress. She was attached to Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society, Mix Max and Aoni Production. On February 18, 2013, Honda died of colon cancer while undergo treatment.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 28, 1963
  • Death: February 18, 2013
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Chieko Honda

Gardenia Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Gardenia is a gym leader of the Sinnoh region. She is the gym leader of Eterna City. She battles using Grass-type Pokemon. She loves Grass-type so much that she will hug them when they are in sight.
Marie Mjolnir Soul Eater Marie Mjolnir is the death scythe who works over seas. Her weapon form is a hammer. She is carefree and absent-minded. She is also lovelorn and desperately wishes to get married. She has feelings for Stein.
