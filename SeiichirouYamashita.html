Seiichirou Yamashita

Seiichirou Yamashita is a Japanese voice actor. He ia affiliated with Office Osawa. Yamashita graduated from Department of Voice Acting Talent of Amusement Media Academy. His major roles are Kakeru Naruse in Orange, Takahiro Mizuno in Walhure Romanze, and Rekka Yukimura in Battle Spirits: Burning Soul.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 21, 1992
  • Hometown: Hiroshima, Japan

Characters Voiced by Seiichirou Yamashita

Clay Sizemore
  • Soul Eater Not!
Clay Sizemore is demon weapon and Akane Hoshi's weapon partner. He works part time at the Cafe shop with Akane. He is laid back than Akane and very confident in his abilities. Clay speaks formal to his peers and seniors.
Daichi Tatsuaki
  • How to Keep a Mummy
Daichi Tatsuaki is one Sora's classmates. He takes care of a Baku named Mukumuku. At first, he was a delinquent who is bad-tempered and impatient. This is due to his long-term sleeping problem. He is actually a very well-mannered, kind, and caring person who constanly worry bothering others. He is timid when dealing with supernatural entities.
Edward Midford
  • Black Butler: Book of the Altantic
Edward Midford is the son of Alexis Leon and Francis Midford and older brother of Elizabeth Midford. He is the current Prefect of the Green House at Weston College. He is a very dignfied and brave man. He has a strong sense of courage, pride, and honor. Edward is exetremely protective and possessive of his younger sister, Elizabeth.
