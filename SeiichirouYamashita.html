Seiichirou Yamashita

Seiichirou Yamashita is a Japanese voice actor. He ia affiliated with Office Osawa. Yamashita graduated from Department of Voice Acting Talent of Amusement Media Academy. His major roles are Kakeru Naruse in Orange, Takahiro Mizuno in Walhure Romanze, and Rekka Yukimura in Battle Spirits: Burning Soul.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 21, 1992
  • Hometown: Hiroshima, Japan

Characters Voiced by Seiichirou Yamashita

Clay Sizemore
  • Soul Eater Not!
Clay Sizemore is demon weapon and Akane Hoshi's weapon partner. He works part time at the Cafe shop with Akane. He is laid back than Akane and very confident in his abilities. Clay speaks formal to his peers and seniors.
Kakeru Naruse
  • Orange
  • Orange: Mirai
Kakeru Naruse is one of Naho Takamiya's classmate and love interest. He has issue with his family and he is lonely. Kakeru is very reserved and quiet until Suwa invited him into the group.
