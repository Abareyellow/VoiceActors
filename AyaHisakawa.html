Aya Hisakawa

Aya Hisakawa is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Her major roles are Sailor Mercury from Sailor Moon and Cure Moonlight in HeartCatch Pretty Cure!. She is affiliated with Aoni Production.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 12, 1968
  • Hometown: Kaizuka, Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dog, Games, Dog and Cat Foster Society Volunteer
