Aya Hisakawa

Aya Hisakawa is a japanese voice actress and singer. Hisakawa is well known for her roles. Her major roles are Sailor Mercury from Sailor Moon and Cure Moonlight in HeartCatch Pretty Cure!. She is affiliated with Aoni Production.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 12, 1968
  • Hometown: Kaizuka, Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dog, Games, Dog and Cat Foster Society Volunteer
