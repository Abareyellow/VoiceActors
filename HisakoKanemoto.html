Hisako Kanemoto

Hisako Kanemoto or Juri Aikawa is a voice actress and singer. She is represented by Production Baobab. Kanemoto attend and graduated Kamagata High School. She enrolled in voic acting course at the Vocational School of Art College Kobe. After completed her education in 2008. She moved Tokyo and joined Production Baobab. Her debut was 2009, under the name Juri Aikawa. She got the lead role for her debut in Sora no Manimari. After following year, she used her real name. She was named Best New Actress at the 5th Seiyu Awards in 2010. Kanemoto made her debut as a solo musical artist in 2014 under Victor Entertainment.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 16, 1987
  • Birthplace: Okayama Precture, Japan
