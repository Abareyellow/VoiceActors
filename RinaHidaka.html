Rina Hidaka

Rina Hidaka is a Japanese voice and actress. She is previouly worked with AT Production but now she is affiliated with Office Osawa. She voiced Keiko Ayano from Sword Art Online, Last Order from A Certain Magical Index and Yuniko Kouzuki from Accel World.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 15, 1994
  • Birthplace: Chiba, Japan
  • Skills: Jazz Dance
