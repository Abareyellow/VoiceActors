Yoshitaka Yamaya

Yoshitaka Yamaya is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with With Line. He voiced Tomohito Sugino in Assassination Classroom and Itsuki Kawasumi in The Rising of The Shield Hero.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: Feburary 15, 1992
  • Hometown: Miyagi, Japan
  • Hobby: Watch Wrestling Matches
  • Skills: River Fishing, Snowboarding, and Soccer

Character Voiced by Yoshitaka Yamaya

Itsuki Kawasumi The Rising of the Shield Hero Itsuki Kawasumi is the Bow Hero, one of the four Legendary Heros summoned in Melromarc. He called himself an ally of justice from his childhood. He can be gullible and unobservant. He is extremely petty and not intelligent. In short, he is suck up, gullible and wants to be praised by others.
