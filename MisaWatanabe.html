Misa Watanabe

Misa Watanabe is a Japanese voice actress. She is currently affiliated with Aoni Production. She is well known for the role of Vivi Nefertari in One Piece. Watanabe voiced Phantom Spy Vancuria in Mahou Sentai Magiranger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 30, 1964
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Misa Watanabe

Ursula
  • Dinosaur King
  • Dinosaur King D - Kids
Ursula is a member of the Alpha Gang. She works with Zander and Ed to obtain dinosaur cards. She is very conceited and acts like a queen. She is very smart, sneaky, opportunistic, manipulative, proud, charming but snobbish, arrogant, and mostly cares about herself.
