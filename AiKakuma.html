Ai Kakuma

Ai Kakuma is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. Ai Kakuma worked as an office lady for four years after graduating from high school. She enteredthe training school of Mausu Promotion in 2011. Her anime debut was Listen to Me Girls, I am Your Father! as a minor character. She voiced Mage in Bikini Warriors, RyuZu in Clockwork Planet and Primrose Shelly in Dragonar Academy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 9, 1988
  • Birthplace: Fukuoka, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ai Kakuma

Aima
  • Children of the Whales
Aima is a mysterious person who is connected to the Mud Whale and the twin of Neri. She is the opposite of her twin sister, being more forward and choatic. She is much more willing to reveal the secrets of the Mud Whale and showing off her powers.
Neri
  • Children of the Whales
  • Children of the Whales Specials
Neri is Unmarked child serving the Commitee of Elders who has special connection to the Mud Whale. Her personality is kind and soft-spoken. Sometimes she become more like her twin sister, Aima and more knowledgeable about the Mud Whale.
