Mamoru Miyano

Mamoru Miyano is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. Miyano joined THe Himawari Theatre Group at the of 7. His debut as an actor is Riku from the Kingdom Heart video game series. His first main character anime role is Kida from Wolf's Rain. From the Ultraman Universe, he voiced the character of Ultraman Zero. Mamoru got married 2008 and later had a kid.
