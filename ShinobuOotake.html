Shinobu Ootake

Shinobu Ootake is a Japanese actress. She has won three academy for her acting. Ootake has married twice in her lifetime. She currently has a son named Hattori, and a daughter named Imalu.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 17, 1957
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Shinobu Ootake

Homily The Secret World of Arrietty Homily is Arrietty's mother. She worries so much about her husband's and daughter's safety. She loves her house so much.
