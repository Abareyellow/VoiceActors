Kimiko Saito

Kimiko Saito is a Japanese voice actress. She affiliated with Aoni Production. Some major roles of her's are Rem from Death Note and Chieko in Princess Jellyfish.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 12, 1977
  • Hometown: Nagano, Japan
  • Hobbies: Ceramics and Watercolor Painting
  • Skill: Teaching
