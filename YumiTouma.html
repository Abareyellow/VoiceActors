Yumi Touma

Yumi Touma or Yumi Yoshida is a Japanese voice actress, narrator and singer. She was part of singing group Goddess Family Club. She once worked for Aoni Promotion before she founded her own agency ALLURE&Y. With her mature voice, she voices strong and beautiful women. She is currently married to Toei Company producer Tatsuya Yoshida.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 20, 1966
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Watching Young Magical Taruruuto
