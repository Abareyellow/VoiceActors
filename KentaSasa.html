Kenta Sasa

Kenta Sasa is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced in Gintama, Food Wars! and Fairy Tail (2014).

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 3
  • Birthplace: Akita Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Kenta Sasa

Grandfather Tadokoro Food Wars! Grandfather Tadokoro is the Megumi Tadokoro's grandfather.
