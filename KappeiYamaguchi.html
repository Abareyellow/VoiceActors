Kappei Yamaguchi

Kappei Yamaguchi or Mitsuo Yamaguchi is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He affiliated with Goku and 21st Century Fox. He is know to voice character of ongoing series. He is the voice of L from Death Note and Inuyasha from Inuyasha. He voiced Ticket and ToQger Equipment Voice in Ressha Sentai ToQger.

  • Birthday: May 23, 1965
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Real Name: Mitsuo Yamaguchi
