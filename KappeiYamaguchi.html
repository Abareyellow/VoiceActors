Kappei Yamaguchi

Kappei Yamaguchi or Mitsuo Yamaguchi is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He affiliated with Goku and 21st Century Fox. He is know for voicing the mani character, Conan Edogawa, of ongoing series Detective Conan. He is the voice of L from Death Note and Inuyasha from Inuyasha. He voiced Ticket and ToQger Equipment Voice in Ressha Sentai ToQger.

  • Birthday: May 23, 1965
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
