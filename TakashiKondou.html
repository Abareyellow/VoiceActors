Takashi Kondou

Takashi Kondou is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Vi-Vo. He voiced JC from Red Garden, Prince Romeo from Lady Jewelpet, and Lolaus from Heroic Age.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 12, 1979
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Darts, Futsal, Mahjong, Playing Music and Reading
  • Skill: Archery
  • Nickname: Kon-chan
