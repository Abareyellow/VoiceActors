Yui Makino

Yui Makino is a Japanese actress, voice actress, singer and pianist. Makino took up piano when she was 4 years old. Her talent was discovered by the film director Shunji Iwai when she was 7 to 18. She gradulated from Tokyo College of Music as a piano major. She did ending and inserted songs in anime. Along with singing, she voiced Sakura from Tsubasa Chronicle, Yusa from Angel Beats!, and Francesca from Francesca.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 19, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobby: Swimming
  • Skills: Piano
