Karin Takahashi

Karin Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. At a young age, she had an interested songs in anime. She would listen to music from Inuyasha and Cardcaptor Sakura. In Elementary school, she wanted become a lawyer because she enjoyed watching crime drama series. At age 17, she won an audition from Pony Canyon. In 2013, she debuted as a voice actor as Yui Natsukawa from Ace Of Diamond. She graduated from Rikikyo University snd voiced Nono Morikubo in The Idolmaster Cinderella Girls.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 9, 1994
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
