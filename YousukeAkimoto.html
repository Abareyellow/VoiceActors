Yousuke Arimoto

Yousuke Arimoto is a Japanese voice actor and actor. He is affiliated with Office PAC. Akimoto is a graduate of Hosei University business school. He voiced in Lomien Man as Quancao, and Maho Girls Precure! as Dokuroxy.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 5, 1944
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birth Name: Yukio Tajika
