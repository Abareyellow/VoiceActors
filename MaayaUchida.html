Maaya Uchida

Maaya Uchida is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She works for I'm Enterprise. Uchida completed her studies in narration school during 2009. Upon gradition, she made her debut as a voice actress in an OVA. Uchida has a younger brother in same career named Yuuma Uchida. She made an appearance in Unofficial Sentai Akibaranger.
