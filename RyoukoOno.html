Ryouko Ono

Ryouko Ono is a Japanese voice actress. Ono gave birth to her daughter on October 26, 2015. She is well known for her role of Mirajane Strauss.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 22, 1977
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Art of Flower Arrangement and Playing Musical Instruments
