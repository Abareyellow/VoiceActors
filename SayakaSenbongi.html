Sayaka Senbongi

Sayaka Senbongi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. Sayaka is a graduate of Nihon Narration Engi Kenkyuujo. Her debut role was Claire Kokonoe in Chronicles of the Going Home Club.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 24, 1995
  • Birthplace: Saitama Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Painting, Playing Games, Reading Manga, and Singing
  • Skills: Playing Clarinet

Characters Voiced by Sayaka Senbongi

Cilla Food Wars! The Third Plate: Totsuki Train Arc Cilla is the staff member under the Research Divison of Nakiri International. She and her sister Berta are Leonora Nakiri's subordinates.
