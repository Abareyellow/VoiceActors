Rokurou Naya

Rokurou Naya was a Japanese voice actor. He was affiliated with Mausu Promotion until his death. He is the brother of voice actor Goro Naya. He voiced are Jimmy Murray in Emily in New Moon and Papa in Fairy Princess Minky Momo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 20, 1932
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Death: November 17, 2014
  • Brother: Goro Naya

Character Voiced by Rokuro Naya

Grumman Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood Grumman is the commander of the eastern headquarters and grandmother of Riza Hawkeye. He is one of Colonel Mustang's closet allies and a mentor. He is kind and wry abundant sense of humor.
