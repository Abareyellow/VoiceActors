Takahiro Sakurai

Takahiro Sakurai is a Japanese voice actor. He was a member of 81 Produce 81 but now he is a member of INTENTION. He and Kenichi Suzumura are good friends. Many of his roles are handsome man, but the occasional roles of reluctant heros, villian and arrogant young men. He provided the voices of Edward Cullen in the Japanese dub of the Twilight films and Nick Russell in the Japanese dub of Power Ranger Mystic Force. Sakurai is voice of the infamous Jealousto from Kaizoku Sentai Gokairanger.
