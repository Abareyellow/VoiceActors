Omi Minami

Omi Minami is a Japanese voice actress, essayist, and columnist. She is the wife of Yuji Ueda. Her starring roles are Hyatt in Excel Saga, and Ruri Hoshino in Martian Successor Nadesico.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 13, 1968
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Husband: Yuji Ueda

Character Voiced by Omi Minami

Eriote Borchard Scrapped Princess Eriote Borchard is Second in command of the organization Scarlet. She is constantly by Senes' side. She feels that she has to protect her even though Eriote is weak.
