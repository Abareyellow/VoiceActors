Megumi Matsumoto

Megumi Matsumoto is a Japanese actress. She is affiliated with Ken Production. Some of her roles are Max Taylor in Dinosaur King and Sam N'Dool in D.I.C.E.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 6, 1977
  • Hometown: Kumamoto, Japan

Character Voiced by Megumi Matsumoto

Max Taylor
  • Dinosaur King
  • Dinosaur King D - Kids
Max Taylor is the partner of Chomp. Max is happy-go-lucky boy who loves dinosaurs. He can be quite clumsy. Caring for the dinosaurs, Max has devoted his life to save them.
