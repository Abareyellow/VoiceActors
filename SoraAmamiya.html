Sora Amamiya

Sora Amamiya is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Music Ray'n. She was introduced to voice acting in high school by one of her friends anime recommendation. After seeing the collection voice roles of Miyuki Sawashiro, she decided to be an voice actress. Amamiya, together with Momo Asakura and Shiina Natsukawa, passed an audition held by Music Ray'n in 2011. She did minored roles in Aikatsu!, Shinsekai Yori, and Tonari no Kaibutsu-kun. In 2014, she landed her first leading role as Koari Fujimiya from Isshuukan Friends.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 28, 1993
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke
