Yuusuke Kobayashi

Yuusuke Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Yu-rin Pro. He lived in Newbury, New England between the ages of 5 - 10. Kobayashi's notable roles are Ren Homura in Monster Strike series, Shouta Yabe in Killing Bites, and Arslan in The Heroic Legend of Arslan.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 25, 1985
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Darts, Driving, Games, and Karaoke
  • Skills: Acrobatics, Karate, and Singing
