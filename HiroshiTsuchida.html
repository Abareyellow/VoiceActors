Hiroshi Tsuchida

Hiroshi Tsuchida is a Japanese actoe and video actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. He dubs over characters from English films. Hiroshi Tsuchida is best known for his role as Saizo/NinjaBlue from Ninja Sentai Kakuranger. He is Lightning McQueen in the Japanese dub of Cars Movies.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 8, 1972
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
