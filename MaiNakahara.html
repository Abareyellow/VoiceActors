Mai Nakahara

Mai Nakahara is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is associated with I'm Enterprise. She voiced Mai Tokiha from My-Hime, Juvia Lockser from Fairy Tail and Nagisa Furukawa drom Clannad. She has sung theme songs for anime shows.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 23, 1981
  • Birthplace: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking and Watching Movies
  • Skills: Kendo
