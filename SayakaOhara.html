Sayaka Ohara

Sayaka Ohara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. She is the older sister of Takashi Ohara. Her well known roles are Layla Hamilton in Kaleido Star, Erza Scarlet if Fairy Tail and Alicia Florence in Aria.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 6, 1975
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skill: Playing Cello
  • Brother: Takashi Ohara
