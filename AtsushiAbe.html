Atsushi Abe

Atsushi Abe is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Ken Production. Some of his major roles are Toma Kamijo in A Certain Magical Index/A Certain Scientific Raligun series and Kukai Soma in Shugo Chara.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 25, 1981
  • Hometown: Tochigi, Japan
  • Skills: Piano

Characters Voiced by Atsushi Abe
