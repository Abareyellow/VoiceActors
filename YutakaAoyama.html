Yutaka Aoyama

Yutaka Aoyama is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He voiced Lebuty from Black Clover and The Earl of Millennium from D.Gray-man Hallow.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 30, 1965
  • Hometown: Ehime, Japan

Characters Voiced by Yutaka Aoyama

Jura Nekis
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
  • Fairy Tail: Final Season
Jura Nekis is a former Mage of Lamia Scale and one of the Ten Wizard Saints. He soon was elected to reform the Magic Council tthat was destroyed by Tartaros Guild. He is very modest about his immense power, never boasting about his power. He is an extremely upright, honor-bound individual, believing that it is unjust to arrest a good person.
