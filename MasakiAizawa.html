Masaki Aizawa

Masaki Aizawa is a Japanese voice actor. He is currently a freelancer. He voiced in Pokemon as Ghetsis and Keanan, Gintama as Ochi-san, and One Piece as Wiper and Ideo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 19, 1965
  • Hometown: Hokkaido, Japan

Characters Voiced by Masaki Aizawa

Ghetsis Harmonia
  • Pokemon Black and White: Adventure in Unova
Ghetsis Harmonia is the boss of Team Plasma. He is one of the Seven Sage behind Team Plasma. His goal is to free Pokemon from humans.
Keanan
  • Pokemon XY
  • Pokemon XYZ
Keanan is a warden eho watches over a swamp located between Lumiose City and Laverre City. He takes care of all the Pokemon living in the swamp land.
Professor Telesu
  • Pokemon
Professor Telesu is an archaeologist who is studying hieroglyphics near a lake full of Slowpoke.
Proprietor
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
The Proprietor is a server at a Sunyshore City cafe. He was once was a Pokemon Poacher and knew Volkner and Flint when they were kids.
Shouryuu
  • The Twelve Kingdom
Shouryuu is the King of En for five hundred years. He is a taika and considered a genius of adminstration. He believes that all kings have the potential to be great. He is supportive and protective of new Kings or Queen in the Twelve Kingdom. He originally was the young master of the Komatsu clan during feudal Japan.
