Kazuo Oka

Kazuo Oka is a Japanese voice actor. He is employed with Art Vision. He voiced minor villians in Denji Sentai Megaranger and KyuKyu Sentai GoGoFive.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 15, 1948

Old Man Romeo x Juliet Old Man is a mysterious and sage men who acts as Romeo's spiritual adviser.
