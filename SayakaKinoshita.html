Sayaka Kinoshita

Sayaka Kinoshita is a Japanese voice actress. She is voiced Bella Swan in the Japanese dub of Twilight(live action).

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 21, 1981
  • Hobbies: Listening to Music and Reading
  • Skills: English, French and Piano

Character Voiced by Sayaka Kinoshita

Lusamine Pokemon Sun and Moon Lusamine is Lillie's and Gladion's mother and the president of the Aether Foundation. She is a genuine Pokemon preservationist and a loving mother to both Lillie and Gladion. She is distracted with her work, she often to make decisions without considering Lillie. She is deeply ashamed of herself after choosing her work over her children. When one of her children is in danger, she is fiercely protective of them. She is strong obsessed with Ultra Beasts.
