Ryou Hirohashi

Ryou Hirohashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated Aoni Production. she went to Ryokoku University in Kyoto. She remembered in high school and became interested in voice actors in her second year. She then went to Blue II juku school in parallel with the university. She graduated from Blue School of Osaka. In 2002, she debuted in Aquarian Age: Sign for Evolution.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 5, 1977
  • Hometown: Niigata, Japan
  • Skills: Certified Junior and Senior High School Teacher
