Yuri Shiratori

Yuri Shiratori is a Japanese voice actress and J-pop singer. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Angelique Limoges in Angelique, and Sayo Aisaka in Negima.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 20, 1968
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skill: French Language

Characters Voiced by Yuri Shiratori

Alyssa Pokemon Advanced Generations Alyssa is the older sister of Thatcher. She lives in Misty Village with her family. She and her brother have the duty of protecting the light stone.
Lara Laramie Pokemon Lara Laramie is a girl with a Ponyta. She and her family own a farm outside of Kanto Safari Zone.
Nancy Pokemon Advanced Generations Nancy is a little girl with a Clamperl. Nancy lives on Island C, one of the A-B-c Islands. She attends school over on Island A. She has a rivalry with Keith.
Roxanne Pokemon Advanced Generations Roxanne is a gym leader in the Hoenn region. She is an expert of rock-types Pokemon. She is not only a gym leader, she is a teacher as well.
