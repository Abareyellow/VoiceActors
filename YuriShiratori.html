Yuri Shiratori

Yuri Shiratori is a Japanese voice actress and J-pop singer. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Angelique Limoges in Angelique, and Sayo Aisaka in Negima.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 20, 1968
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skill: French Language

Character Voiced by Yuri Shiratori

Roxanne Pokemon Advanced Generations Roxanne is a gym leader in the Hoenn region. She is an expert of rock-types Pokemon. She is not only a gym leader, she is a teacher as well.
