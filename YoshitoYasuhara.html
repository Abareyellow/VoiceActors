Yoshito Yasuhara

Yoshito Yasuhara is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Theatre Echo, a talent management firm known for its comedy sketches. He voiced Dr. Tokio Taimu in The Flying House, Louis XVI in The Rose of Versailles, and August from Fairy Tail: Fianl Season.

  • Birthday: November 17, 1949
  • Hometown: Aioi, Hyogo, Japan
