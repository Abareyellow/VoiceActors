Anri Katsu

Anri Katsu is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Ken Production. He voiced Nile Dok in Attack on Titans, Motoharu Tsuchimikado in A Certain Magicial Index and Hiroto Tonomachi in Date A Live. He is voice of Michelanglo in the Japanese dub of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 3, 1977
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Singing. Movies, and Ramen
  • Skills: Composing Songs, Playing Musical Instruments, and Fishing

Character Voiced by Anri Katsu

Tierno
  • Pokemon XY
  • Pokemon XYZ
Tierno is a Pokemon Trainer from the Kalos region and one of Ash's rivals in Kalos. He is a dancer and uses it as a battla tactic. He teaches his Pokemon dance moves so they can evade attacks. He is very friendly, supportive, and always encouraging his friends to do their best. He has feelings for Serena.
