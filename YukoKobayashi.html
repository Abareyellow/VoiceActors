Yuko Kobayashi

Yuko Kobayashi is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and narrator. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She is currently married to Mitsuaki Hoshino. She voiced YU Hazuki in Attack You! and Rapier in Maze.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 6, 1961
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
