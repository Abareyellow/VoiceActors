Yasuaki Takumi

Yasuaki Takumi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Haikyo. He voiced Gakuto Kase in Gamers! and Edgar Blanche in Knight's & Magic.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 23, 1982
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan

Character Voiced by Yasuaki Takumi

Masoh
  • Children of the Whales
  • Children of the Whales Specials
Masoh is an older Marked citizen on the Mud Whale. He is the captain on any watch party or military battle.
