Kanae Itou

Kanae Itou is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production and music publisher Lantis. After graduating from high school, Kanae enrolled at the Amusement Media Academy, a vocational school for actor and artist. She gradulated the Amusement Media Academy in 2007. She and Aki Toyosaki are good friend who worked in Shugo Chara!, To Love-Ru and A Certain Scientific Railgun.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 26, 1986
  • Birthplace: Nagano, Japan
