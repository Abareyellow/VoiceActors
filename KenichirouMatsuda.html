Kenishirou Matsuda

Kenichirou Matsuda is a Japanese voice actor for animes, video games and dubbing. He is the voice of Lloyd from Fire Emblem Heros. Along with Lloyd, he voiced Raphael in Japanese dub of Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 22, 1978
  • Birthplace: Saitama, Japan
