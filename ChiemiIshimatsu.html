Chiemi Ishimatsu

Chiemi Ishimatsu is a Japanese voice actress. She is voiced Tooth in the Japanese dub of Rise of the Guardians.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 5, 1976
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
