Banjou Ginga

Banjou Ginga or Takashi Tanaka is a Japanese voice actress. Ginga is affiliated with Aoni Production. He is most well known in Mobile Suit Gundam and Fist of the North Star. He is married to Gara Takashima.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 12, 1948
  • Birthplace: Kofu, Yamanashi, Japan
  • Wife: Gara Takashima
