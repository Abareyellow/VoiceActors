Yuuki Kuwahara

Yuuki Kuwahara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. In 2012, she graduated from the Tokyo Announcement College Voice Academy, and entered the Mausu Promotion Actor Training School. Before entering vocational school, she used self-taught books of voice actors at the library and participated in Romi Park's workshop. She voiced Rattle in Freezing Vibration, Sora Narukkami in The Day I Became a God, and Eliaria in By the Grace of the Gods.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 24, 1991
  • Hometown: Nagasaki, Japan
  • Favorite Foods: Sweet Food and Spicy Food
