Kaito Ishikawa

Kaito Ishikawa is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Pro-Fit. He voiced Fau in Last Hope, Genos in One Punch Man and Tenya Iida in My Hero Academia.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 13, 1993
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Kaito Ishikawa

Naofumi Iwantani The Rising of the Shield Hero Naofumi Iwantani is an university student before being summoned to another world as one of the 4 heros summoned the Shield Hero. He is an extremely trusting and positive person before becoming the Shield Hero. Now he is becoming distrustful, cynical and spiteful towards the world. Despite distrust everyone, he is a caring side to him. He is very observant. Naofumi have villianous attitude and evil ways of solving things, he gets the job done effective.
