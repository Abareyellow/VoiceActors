Rie Hikisaka

Rie Hikisaka is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced March in To Your Eternity, Osushi in Burn the Witch, and Hana Nono/Cure Yell in Hugtto! Precure.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 11
  • Hometown: Kagoshima, Japan

Character Voiced by Rie Hikisaka

Osushi Burn the Witch Osushi is a Dragon who masquerading as Balgo Parks's pet dog in London. In his dog form, Osushi is generally quiet and complacent with whatever Balgo tasks him to do.
