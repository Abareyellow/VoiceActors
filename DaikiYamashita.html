Daiki Yamashita

Daiki Yamashita is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He voiced Genta Doi in Hi Score Girl, Toya in Log Horizon, and Izuku Midoriya in My Hero Academia.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 7, 1989
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birthplace: Shizuka, Japan

Character Voiced by Daiki Yamashita

Jun Sword Art Online II Jun is a close-range tank-build swordsman member of the Sleeping Knight guild. In real life, he is affliacted with cancer. Despite having cancer, he is an easy going person who always wears a smile on his face.
