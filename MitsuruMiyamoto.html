Mitsuru Miyamoto

Mitsuru Miyamoto is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and narrator. He is affiliated with Theatre Company Subaru. His major roles are Roger Smith from The Big O, Ougai Mori from Bungo Stray Dogs, and Gaku Yashiro from Erased. He voiced the adult Simba from the Japanese dub of The Lion King. He also voiced Dogranio Yaboon from Kaitou Sentai Lupinranger VS Keisatsu Sentai Patranger

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 8, 1958
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball and Tennis

Character Voiced by Mitsuru Miyomoto

Maiza Avaro
  • Baccano!
  • Baccano! Specials
Maiza Avaro is one of the 1711 Advena Avis immortals and the contaiuolo of the Martillo Family. He is the only immortal who knows the summoning method. Maiza is soft-spoken and polite to friend and foe alike. He prefers violences and when it comes to it he does not hesitate. He is rebellious, quick-tempered, bitter, brash and belligerent. He is only scornful towards whatever displeases of offends him. He is gentle towards young children. He is cautious when in control of his temper and prefers to avoid violence.
