Misaki Kuno

Misaki Kuno is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Office Osawa. She is known for voices of Hawk in The Seven Deadly Sin and Serara in Log Horizon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 19, 1993
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Singing Song
  • Skills: Modern Ballet
