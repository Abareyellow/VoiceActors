Kanon Tani

Kanon Tani is a Japanese voice actress. She played the role of young Meiko Honma in Fuji Television's live-action adaptation of Ano Hi Mita Hana no Namae wo Bokutachi wa Mada Shiranai. She has affiliated with Theatre Academy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 4, 2004
  • Birthplace: Saitama, Japan

Character Voiced by Kanon Tani

Yotsuha Miyamizu Your Name Yotsuha Miyamizu is Mitsuha Miyamizu's younger sister, who lives with Mitsuha and their grandmother. She thinks her sister is somewhat crazy but loves her despite the situation.
