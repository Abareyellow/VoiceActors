Yuuko Kaida

Yuuko Kaida is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Ken Production. She voiced Angelica Varley in The Ancient Magus' Bride, Elizabeth Barly in Freezing, and Tsuyuko in Gintama.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 14, 1980
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
