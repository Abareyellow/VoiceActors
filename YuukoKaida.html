Yuuko Kaida

Yuuko Kaida is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Ken Production. She voiced Angelica Varley in The Ancient Magus' Bride, Elizabeth Barly in Freezing, and Tsuyuko in Gintama.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 14, 1980
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan

Character Voiced by Yuuko Kaida

Angelica Barley
  • The Ancient Magus' Bride: Those Awaiting a Star
  • The Ancient Magus' Bride
Angelica Barley is an acquaintance of Elias Ainsworth. She is a mage who mostly works with minerals such as gems and crystals. She is wife to David Barley and mother of Althea Barley.
