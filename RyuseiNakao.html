Ryusei Nakao

Ryusei Nakao or Tomoharu Takeo is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. He is cerrently married and has 2 children. He voiced Rainian Agent Abrella in Tokusou Sentai Dekaranger. In anime, he voiced Baikinman from Soreike! Anpanman, Frieza from Dragon Ball, and Mayuri Kurotsuchi as Bleach.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 5, 1951
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Diving, Horseback Riding, Skating, Skiing
