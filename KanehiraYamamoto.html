Kanehira Yamamoto

Kanehira Yamamoto is a Japanese voice actor.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 6, 1977
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Kanehira Yamamoto

Rigurdo That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Rigurdo is the goblin vilage elder and Rimuru Tempest's very first subordinate. He is the classic "only seen when there's trouble" character. He was the most grateful out of all goblins.
Yakov Nikitin Dr. Stone Yakov Nikitn is the doctor on board the International Space Station alongside his wife Darya Nikitina. He witnessed the petrification of humanity on Earth's surface from the ISS. He is a quiet and timid man who thought with logic.
