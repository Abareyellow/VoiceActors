Toru Furuya

Toru Furuya isa Japanese actor, voice actor and narrator. He worked for Aoni Production. He was best friends with Hirotaka Suzuoki. He protrayed AkaRed in Sentai Super. He voiced Mamoru Chiba/Tuxedo Mask in Sailor Moon, Amuno Ray in Mobile Suit Gundam and Yamcha in Dragon Ball.

  • Birthday: July 31, 1953
  • Hometown: Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Wife: Satomi Majima
