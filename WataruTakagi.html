Wataru Takagi

Wataru Takagi is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He voiced Bunbee from Yes! PreCure 5, Eikichi Onizuka from Great Teacher Onizuka and Genta Kojima from Detective Conan.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 25, 1966
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Wataru Takagi

Uraganos

Uraganos is a large and dim-witted minion of the Dotsuku Zone. He is clumsy and tends to destroy things by accident due to his size and strength. Uraganos nevers seem to hear people finish their sentences, especially Circulas.
