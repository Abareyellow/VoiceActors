Kikuko Inoue

Kikuko Inoue is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She found and manage her voice-acting company, Office Anemone. She plays female characters that are reserved, beautiful, kind, mature or domestic personality. She has a title of Big Sister in real life. Inoue is married and has a daughter named Honoka. She and Atsuko Tanaka are best friends. Inoue voiced Insarn in Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger. She voiced Belldandy from Aa Megumi-sama, Uraha from Air, and Miria from Claymore.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 25, 1964
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
