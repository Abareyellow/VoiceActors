Ayane Sakura

Ayane Sakura is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. Some of her roles are in Tokyo Ravens, My Hero Academia and Charlotte. She called Ayaneru because she sleeps on her spare time.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: Jan 29, 1994
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobby: Listening to the Radio
  • Skills: Gymnastic, Painting, Singing
