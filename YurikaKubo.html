Yurika Kubo

Yurika Kubo is a Japanese voice actress, singer and model. She is affiliated with Stay Luck. She started voice acting while working as a gravure model for JMO, and started obtaining voice acting roles in 2012. She started as a gravure model under the name Yurika Narahara. She played Hanayo Koizumi in the idol group, Muse, which is part of the Love Live! franchise. Some of her roles are Himeko Momokino in Citrus, Kikyo Kushida in Classroom of the Elite, and Peko in Mewkledreamy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 19, 1989
  • Hometown: Nara, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke, Long Distance Walking, and Watching Anime
  • Skill: Calisthenics

Character Voiced by Yurika Kubo

Philuffy Aingram Undefeated Bahamut Chronicles Philuffy Aingram is the second daughter of the Aingram Financial Conglomerate, the younger sister of Relie Aingram, and childhood friend of Lux Arcadia. She is the user of the Divine Drag-Ride called Typhon. She is an easy-going girl, but she is not good at expressing her emotions. Despite her stoic behavior, she has an honest personality and she exudes out an absent-minded air around her most of the time. She possess a strong will and will not back down once she decides on something. Combined with her sharp senses and her easy-going personality, Philuffy is very difficult to argue with. She has a very close relationship with Lux Arcadia.
