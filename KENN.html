Kenchiro Ohashi

Kenchiro Ohashi or KENN is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and singer. He made his debut as a voice actor was Yu-Gi-Oh! GX as Jaden Yuki. He also voiced Yuta Fuji in Prince of Tennis, and Futo in Brothers Conflict.
