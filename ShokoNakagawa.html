Shoko Nakagawa

Shoko Nakagawa is a Japanese voice actress, actress, illustrator and singer. She is the daughter of Katsuhiko Nakagawa, a celebrity in Japan. She is a fan of Super Sentai when she was young. She was a fan of Yuri/Time Pink of Mirai Sentai Timeranger. She tries to get a role of the pink sentai warrior but was rejected because of athletic ability. In 2013. she wrote an autobiographical book about her experience with Pokemon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 5, 1985
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Father: Katsuhiko Nakagawa
  • Nickname: Shokotan
