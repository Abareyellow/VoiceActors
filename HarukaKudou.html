Haruka Kudou

Haruka Kudou is a Japanese model and voice actress. She voices Sayu Yagami from Death Note and Hagumi Hanamoto from Honey and Clover. She was an model fro Seventeen Japanese magazine from 2003 to 2006.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 16, 1989
  • Birthplace: Osaka, Japan
