Chinami Nishimura

Chinami Nishimura is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced in amines such as Pokemon as several different roles, Precure Smile as Reika Aoki/Cure Beauty, Aria as Aria Pokoteng and Bleach as Kiyone Kotetsu.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 18, 1970
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
