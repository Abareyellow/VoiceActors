Yuka Imai

Yuka Imai is a former Japanese voice actress. She used to represented Gekidan Moonlight and Atomic Monkey. She retired on August 31, 2018 due to her life going crazy. She voiced Jinto Lin from Banner of the Star, Ai and Marie Ange from Dokidoki! Precure, and Wakaba Shinohara from Revolutionary Girl Utena.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 19, 1970
  • Hometown: Atami, Shizuoka, Japan
  • Retired: August 31, 2018

Character Voiced by Yuka Imai

Kaoru Kiryuu Pretty Cure Splash Star Kaoru Kiryuu was one of the two mysterious girls that Saki and Mai at the Sky Tree. At first she was rude and cold, but as the series progresses she befriended Saki and Mai along with her sister, Michiru. Shets along with Saki's younger sister, Minori.
