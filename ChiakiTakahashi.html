Chiaki Takahashi

Chiaki Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress, singer, and gravure idol. In 2010, she changed her kanji lettering in her name. She was a member of J-pop group Aice. She made her solo musical debut in 2011.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 8, 1977
  • Hometown: Yokohama, Japan
