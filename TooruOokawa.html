Tooru Ookawa

Tooru Ookawa is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and narrator. He is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. He went to a College af Art, Nihon University. His major roles are James Ironside in Blood+, Professor Carlton in Earl and Fairy, and Roy Mustang in Fullmetal Alchemist.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 28, 1960
  • Hometown: Kagoshima, Japan
