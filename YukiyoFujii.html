Yukiyo Fujii

Yukiyo Fujii is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. Before she was a voice actress, she was a stage lighting technician. She participated in a Miss International Japan beauty pageant. Fujii was originally affiliated with Aoi Corporation but as of April 2011, she was apart of Aoni Production.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 8, 1985
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
