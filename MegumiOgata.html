Megumi Ogata

Megumi Ogata is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She attended Tokai University, but left due to lack of interest. She started out as a musical actress and attended the same classes as Rumi Kasahara. Ogata is affiliated with Aoni Production. She has voiced in Sailor Uranus in Sailor Moon, Yukito/Yue in Cardcaptor Sakura, and Tier Harribel from Bleach.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 8, 1965
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Ceramic Art, Dancing, Driving, Swimming, and Traveling
  • Skills: Drawing Portraits and Sleeping
