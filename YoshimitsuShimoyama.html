Yoshimitsu Shimoyama

Yoshimitsu Shimoyama is a Japanese voice actor. He has been Satomi Arai's husband since 2008. He and Satomi had a son in 2010.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 6, 1976
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Wife: Satomi Arai
