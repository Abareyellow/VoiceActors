Tomoaki Maeno

Tomoaki Maeno is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He voiced Graham Bell in Time Travel Girl, and Reiji Kiaki in World Trigger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 26, 1982
  • Birthplace: Ibaraki, Japan
