Unshou Ishizuka

Unshou Ishizuka was a Japanese actor, voice actor, narrator and theatre director. He was affiliated with Aoni Production until his death. On August 17, 2018, Ishizuka died on August 13 to esophogeal cancer. He voiced Professor Oak in Pokemon and Mr. Satan in Dragon Ball.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 16, 1951
  • Hometown: Fukui, Japan
  • Death: August 13, 2018
