Hiroki Takahashi

Hiroki Takahashi is a Japanese actor and singer. Some of his major roles are in Prince of Tennis, School Rumble and Hunter x Hunter. He provides the voice of Ryu in the Street Fighter games. He is married to Akio Aihashi.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 7, 1974
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball, Guitar
  • Skills: Traditional Japanese dance

Character Voiced by Hiroki Takahashi

Tobias Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Tobias is a trainer that Ash battled in the Sinnoh Pokemon League. He is a mysterious trainer that is not known for his appearance. He is a strong trainer.
