Hiroki Takahashi

Hiroki Takahashi is a Japanese actor and singer. Some of his major roles are in Prince of Tennis as Eiji Kikamaru, School Rumble as Kenji Harima and Hunter x Hunter as Hisoka Morow. He provides the voice of Ryu in the Street Fighter games. He is married to Akio Aihashi.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 7, 1974
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball, Guitar
  • Skills: Traditional Japanese dance
