Yuuko Mita

Yuuko Mita is a Japanese voice actress. She is well known for voicing young boys. She likes to play tennis and skiing. She is employed with Aoni Production.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 14, 1954
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
