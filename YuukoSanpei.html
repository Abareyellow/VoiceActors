Yuuko Sanpei

Yuuko Sanpei is a Japanese voice actress. Some of roles she voices in anime are from Eureka Seven and Boruto: Naruto the Movie. In May 2007, she left the agency Gekidan Wakakusa. She transfer in October 2011 to AXL-One.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: Febuary 28, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
