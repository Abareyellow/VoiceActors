Shinsuke Sugawara

Shinsuke Sugawara is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced Kei Kuramoto in Flying Witch, and Archid Olter in Knight's & Magic.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 28
  • Hometown: Ibaraki, Japan
