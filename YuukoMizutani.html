Yuuko Mizutani

Yuuko Mizutani is a Japanese actress, voice actress, narrator and singer. She was affiliated with Production Baobab but up until her death she was affiliated with Aoni Production. She graduated from Shimamato High School. Her debut role is Sarah Zabiarov in Kidou Senshi Z Gundam. She is the wife of Mizuho Nishikudo. She paased away from Beast Cancer on May 17, 2016.
