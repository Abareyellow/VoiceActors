Yuuichi Shigeru

Yuuichi Shigeru is a Japanese voice actor. He is a gradulate of the Nishogakusha University Department of Literature. He recieved training at Bungoruza's research establishment. He is affiliated with Production Baobab. He voiced Galileo in Marie & Gali, and Brook in One Piece.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 15, 1957
  • Hometown: Kounosu, Saitama, Japan
  • Skills: Licensed Calligraphy Teacher

Characters Voiced by Yuuichi Nagashima

Galein Musica Rave Master Galein Musica was a blacksmith before his family was killed. He is related to Hamrio Musica.
Sumihiko Tsujiie Juuni Taisen Sumihiko Tsujiie is the warrior of the sheep. He kills his enemies with trickery.
