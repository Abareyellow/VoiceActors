Kumiko Nishihara

Kumiko Nishihara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. While a student at Wako University, Nishihara attended voice acting classes run by Kaneta Kimotsuki. She voiced by Diana from Sailor Moon series, and Iris Chateaubriand from Sakura Wars series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 27, 1965
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skills: Koto
