Ryuuichi Kijima

Ryuuichi Kijima is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced Brawnz Ni and Sage Ayana in the U.S. ONA RWBY. He did several voices in Naruto.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 29, 1985
  • Hometwon: Hokkaido, Japan
  • Hobby: Traveling
