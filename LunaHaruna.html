Luna Haruna

Luna Haruna or Aya Itou is a Japanese singer, fashion model and voice actress. At a young age, Haruna was interested in anime and music. In her first year of junior high school, she was obsessed with gothic lolita manga characters and started collecting Western clothes. In her 3rd year of junior high, she auditioned for the internet radio programnamed Rental Magica and was selected to perform some opening theme. She gained popularity when she became a finalist at a songing competiton. She has sing end theme songs for Sword Art Online, Fate/Zero 2 and Urahara. Her debut as a seiyu was Rito Sudou from Urahara.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 11, 1991
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
