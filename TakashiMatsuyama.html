Takashi Matsuyama

Takashi Matsuyama is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is well known for playing Takeo Saeki in The Grudge and Mamoru Satake in Zipang. He voiced Fuzzy Lumpkins in Powerpuff Girl Japanese dub.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: Apr 2, 1960
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Takashi Matsuyama

Atlas Flame Fairy Tail (2014) Atlas Flame is a dragon from 400 years ago. He is a close friend of Igneel.
