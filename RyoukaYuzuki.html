Ryouka Yuzuki

Ryouka Yuzuki or Ayumi Nagashii is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She did a lot of live action when she was a teen. Later, She became an idol under the name Kanori Kadomatsu, appearing in men's magazines and V-Cinema. After break away from live action , she became a voice actress.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 10, 1974
  • Hometown: Anjo, Aichi, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ryouka Yuzuki

Misdreavus
  • Pokemon: Raikou - The Legend of Thunder
Misdreavus is a Pokemon from the Johto region. It spends it's days sleeping in darkness and it nights startling people with mischievous tricks. Misdreavus is known to yank and bite at people's hair. Ryouka Yuzuki voiced Marina's Misdreavus.
Ruby Moon
  • Cardcaptor Sakura
  • Cardcaptor Sakura: Clear Card Prologue
  • Cardcapter Sakura: Clear Card
Ruby Moon or Nakuru Aikzuki is one of two magical guardians created by Eriol Hiiragizawa. Ruby Moon is genderless but loves girl clothes and acting like a female. She has a crush on Toya Kinomoto, Sakura's brother.
