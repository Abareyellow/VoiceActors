Yumiko Kobayashi

Yumiko Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is currently a freelance seiyu, but was apart of Art Vision. She and Mikako Takashi are knon as the duo, The Excel Girls. She is also apart of the seiyu quartlet Prits. She voiced Excel Kobayashi from Excel Saga and Mamoru from Sister Princess.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 18, 1979
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Driving and Drinking
