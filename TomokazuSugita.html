Tomokazu Sugita

Tomokazu Sugita is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Atomic Monkey. His major roles are Gintoki Sakata in Gintama, Yusuke Kitagawa in Persona 5, and Akutsu Akuru in Aho-Girl.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 11, 1980
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan

Characters Voiced by Tomokazu Sugita

Brock
  • Pokemon: The Origin
Brock is the former gym leader of Pewter City. He is aspires to become a top class breeder. He is experienced cook and caretaker by nature. Brock is a womanizer, but he is mature and wise.
Escanor
  • The Seven Deadly Sins: Revival of The Commandments
  • The Seven Deadly Sins Movie: Prisoners of the Sky
Escanor is a member of the Seven Deadly Sins and the Lion's sin of Pride. He gains immense power during the day and becomes weaker in the night. At night, he is skittish, submissive and unfailingly polite. In the sun, he is arrogant, boastful and domineering.
