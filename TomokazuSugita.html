Tomokazu Sugita

Tomokazu Sugita is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Atomic Monkey. His major roles are Gintoki Sakata in Gintama, Yusuke Kitagawa in Persona 5, and Akutsu Akuru in Aho-Girl.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 11, 1980
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan

Character Voiced by Tomokazu Sugita

Brock Pokemon: The Origin Brock is the former gym leader of Pewter City. He is aspires to become a top class breeder. He is experienced cook and caretaker by nature. Brock is a womanizer, but he is mature and wise.
