Ayumu Murase

Ayumu Murase is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with VIMS. He voiced Cartaphilus in The Ancient Magus' Bride amd Yu Kusakable in Wolf Girl & Black Prince.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 14, 1988
  • Birthplace: United States
  • Hobbies: One Person Duet and English Conversation

Characters Voiced by Ayumu Murase

Ginro Dr. Stone Ginro is Kinro's Brother and one of the village guards. He is more cowardly than his brother. He can be very full of himself and selfish. He is distrated by shiny things and Senku's inventions. He is more sincere in his cowardice. Out of the two brothers, he is kinder, gentler for his family and friends. He may be a coward but he will protect and help in dangerous situations. He can be lazy as he slack off on his guard duties.
Kenjiro Minami Yuri!!! on Ice Kenjiro Minami is a Japanese figure skater. He has a bright and innocent personality. He is very energetic and can get nervous in stressful circumstances. He is very supportive of Yuri Katsuki.
