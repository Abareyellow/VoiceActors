Yoko Asada

Yoko Asada is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She has voiced minor characters in Aria The Animation and Pokemon. Her notable role is Juri Katou from Digimon Tamers.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 23, 1969
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan

Character Voiced by Yoko Asada

Lily
  • Pokemon
  • Pokemon Chronicles
Lily is one of Misty's older sisters. She is the third oldest out of the four sisters. She and Violet tends to tease Misty more than Daisy. She encourages Misty to be a better trainer. She plays boyish roles for their performances.
