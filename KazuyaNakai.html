Kazuya Nakai

Kazuya Nakai is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. His notable role is Roronoa Zoro in One Piece. Other major voice roles include Toshiro Hijikata from Gintama, Mugen from Samurai Champloo, and Ultraman Max from Ultrman Max. Nakai voiced Garu/Ookami Blue from Uchu Sentai Kyuranger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 25, 1967
  • Hometown: Kobe, Hyogo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Archery, Driving, and Suverying
