Fuyuka Ooura

Fuyuka Oura is a Japanese voice actress. She has a high pitched voice that uses for younger boys of older characters. She been married since 2011. She change her surname from Oura to Ono.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 13, 1978
  • Birthplace: Hakkaido, Japan
  • Qualification: Registered Nurse
  • Likes: Cats
