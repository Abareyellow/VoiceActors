Tomo Muranaka

Tomo Muranaka is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. Tomo voiced Aoi Tategami/Cute Gelato, and Yuma Kuga in World Trigger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 15, 1989
  • Birthplace: Ibaraki Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Tomo Muranaka

Mrs. Tadokoro

  • Food Wars! The Second Plate
  • Food Wars! The Fifth Plate
Mrs. Tadokoro is the mother of Megumi Tadokoro.
