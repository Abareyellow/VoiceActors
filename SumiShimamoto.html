Sumi Shimamoto

Sumi Shimamoto is a Japanese voice actress of film, anime and video games. She gradulated the Toho Gauken College of Drama and Music. She is affiliated with Gekidan Seinenza. She is currently married to Daisuke Koshikawa since 1984 and has a daughter named Shiori Koshikawa. Her best known voice roles including Nausicaa in Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind and Kyoko Otonashi in Maison Ikkoku.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 8, 1954
  • Hometown: Kochi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Playing RPG Games
