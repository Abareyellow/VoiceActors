Akiko Yajima

Akiko Yajima, formally known as Akiko Ogasawara, is a Japanese voice actress from Niigata, Japan. She voiced in anime such as Gundam, .hack, Air, Sailor Moon and InuYasha. She was freelance voice previously affiliated with Production Baobab until April 2010.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 14, 1967
  • Birthplace: Kashiwazaki, Niigata
