Katsuyuki Konishi

Katsuyuki Konishi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Ken Production. His debut role was Volfogg/ Big Volfogg in GaoGaiGar. He has won Best Suppoerting Actor Award in the 9th Annual Seiyu Awards. Konishi was Gosei Knight from Tensou Sentai Goseiger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 21, 1973
  • Hometown: Wakayama, Japan
