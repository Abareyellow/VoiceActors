Yasuhiro Takato

Yasuhiro Takato is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Artemis from Sailor Moon series and Russia from Hetalia Series.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 23, 1968
  • Hometown: Okayama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Calligraphy, Illustration, Singing, and Writing Lyrics
