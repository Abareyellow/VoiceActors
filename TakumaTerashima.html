Takuma Terashima

Takuma Terashima is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Axlone but was once apart of Holypeak and Production Baobab. He is married to Satomi Sato. His debut role was Apollo from Genesis of Aquarion. He voiced Kojiro Kageyama from Baby Steps, Shiroe form Log Horizon and Usagi from PriPara.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 20, 1983
  • Birthplace: Ishikawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Billard, Drawing, Remaking Clothes and Singing
  • Skills: Waking Up Early
  • Wife: Satomi Sato
