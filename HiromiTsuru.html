Hitomi Tsuru

Hiromi Tsuru is a Japanese voice actress and actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced anime characters from many different animes for 31 years. She was married to Keiichi Nanba but divorced in 1990. Unfornuately, Tsuru died of Aortic dissection on November 16, 2017. She has voiced Bulma from 1986 to 2017. She has also voiced Dokin-chan in Anpanman, Lily in Heavy, and Miyuki Kajima in Miyuki.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 29, 1960
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Died: November 16, 2017
  • Hobbies: Golf and Tennis
