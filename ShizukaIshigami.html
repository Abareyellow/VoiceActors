Shizuka Ishigami

Shizuka Ishigami is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Pro-Fit but was formerly affiliated with 81 Produce. Ishigami wanted to become a manga artist, but became a voice actress after trying out a video game voicing role. She voiced Ikuno in DARLING in the FRANXX, Syr Flover in Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon?, and Ikumi Mito in Food Wars.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 14, 1988
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
