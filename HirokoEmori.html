Hiroko Emori

Hiroko Emori is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She is a Japanese voice actress who has played numerous guest roles throughout the Sailor Moon anime.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 27, 1961
  • Hometown: Kumagaya, Saitama, Japan
