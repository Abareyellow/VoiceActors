Minami Takahashi

Minami Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. Her major roles include Megumi Tadakoro in Food Wars, Midoriko in Selectotor Infected Wixoss and Ojuo in Please Tell Me! Gako-chan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 20, 1990
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Rythmic Gymnastics
  • Skills: German and English Language

Megumi Tadakoro is Tootsuki Culinary Academy student from a small port town in Japan. Megumi's poor skills on her extreme demophobia get the best of her sometimes. She is one of clumsy and shy student who try to do her best to become a chef. Despite easily nervous and sensitive, Megumi is an honest and innocent student who does not lie about anything. Megumi's style of cooking is mostly based on cutting the ingredients using neat style.
