Atsushi Kisaichi

Atsushi Kisaichi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Meta Knight in Kirby: Right Back At Ya! and Timka in Angelique series.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: Feburary 23, 1972
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball and Tennis
