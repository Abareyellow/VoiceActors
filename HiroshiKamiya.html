Hiroshi Kamiya

Hiroshi Kamiya is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He went to Aoni Juku to pursue voice acting. He now affiliated with Aoni Production from his debut in 1992 to today. He hosts several radio shows. He and Daisuke Ono has hosts Dear Girl Stories and are vocalists for Masochistic Ono BanD. He voices Shou Ronpo/Ryu Commander in Uchu Sentai Kyuranger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 28, 1975
  • Birthplace: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Hiroshi Kamiya

N Pokemon Black and White 2: Introduction Movie N appeared as a recurring character in the Pokemon anime. He is a mysterious man with the ability to hear Pokemon voices.
