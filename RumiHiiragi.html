Rumi Hiiragi

Rumi Hiiragi is a Japanes actress. Hiiragi began her career as a child actress at the age of 6. In 2001, she worked on Hayao Miyazaki's award-winning anime film Spirited Away, in which she voiced the film's protagonist, Chihiro, for which her performance was praised.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 1, 1987
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Rumi Hiiragi

Chihiro Ogino Spirited Away Chihiro Ogino is a shy, pessimistic, 10 year-old girl. After her parents turn into pigs, she became more independent and working. Her and Haku become good friends.
