Motoko Kumai

Motoko Kumai is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. From December 2006 to October 2007, she left her voice acting careerdue to medical treatment for an illness. Her most notable roles are Takao Kinomiya in Beyblade and Syaoran Li in Cardcaptor Sakura. Kumai is the voice of Gibby Gibson in the Japanese dub of iCarly.
