Asami Sanada

Asami Sanada is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Amuleto. Her debut role was Deijiko in Di Gi Charat. Some of her best known roles are Vita in the Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha series and Jun Sakurado in the Rozen Maiden series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 8, 1977
  • Hometown: Nagano, Japan

Character Voiced by Asami Sanada

Rani VIII Fate/Extra Last Encore Rani VIII is a Demi-Servant of the Berserker-class. Rani is an intelligent, clam, polite, yet emotionless young girl. She often speaks in riddles.
