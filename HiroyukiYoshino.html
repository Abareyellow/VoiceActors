Hiroyuki Yoshino

Hiroyuki Yoshino is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He voiced in Space Dandy, Inazuma Eleven, The Prince of Tennis, and Kill La Kill.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 6, 1974
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Favorite Anime: Berserk
  • Hobbies: Computer games and Watching Soccer
