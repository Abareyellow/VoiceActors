Hiroyuki Yoshino

Hiroyuki Yoshino is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He voiced in Space Dandy as Meow, Inazuma Eleven as Yuuto Kidou, The Prince of Tennis as Rin Hirakoba, and Kill La Kill as Houka Inumuta.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 6, 1974
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Favorite Anime: Berserk
  • Hobbies: Computer games and Watching Soccer

Characters Voiced by Hiroyuki Yoshino

Sylvester Pokemon Sylvester is a Pokemon Trainer who has a Farfetch'd. His Farfetch'd does not listen to him. He is making Charcoal from trees that Farfetch'd cuts down.
Treize Bain Allison and Lillia Treize Bain is the son of Carr Benedict and Fiona. He is the prince of Ixtova. He has a twin sister named Merielle. He is thoughtful and calm by nature. He is also somewhat dorky.
