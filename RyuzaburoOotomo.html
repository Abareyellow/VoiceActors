Ryuzaburo Ootomo

Ryuzaburo Ootomo is a Japanese voice actor, actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Aoni Production since 2006. He was previously apart of 81 Produce and Haikyo. He taken over the ongoing roles from seiyu wh have deaths. He voiced Crocodile from One Piece and Zero from Montana Jones.

  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
