Mayumi Iizuka

Mayumi Iizuka is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and singer. She is a member of Gekidan Wakakusa and Tokuma Japan Communications. Her major roles are Misty in Pokemon and Sakuya Kumashiro in Tenichi in Tokyo.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 3, 1977
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking and Swimming
  • Skills: Baton and Chinese
