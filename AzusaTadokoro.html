Azusa Tadokoro

Azusa Tadokoro is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Horipro and signed to Lantis. She was inspired to became a voice actress after hearing the perfermance of Akiko Yajima as Kohaku in Inuyasha. She participated in the 36th Horipro Talent Scout Caravan and declared the winner. Later, she joins Horipro. Her notable roles are Aoi Kiriya in Aikatsu!, and Chtholly Nota Seniorious in WorldEnd.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 10, 1993
  • Birthplace: Ibaraki Prefecture
  • Hobbies: Impersonator, Karaoke, Making Albums, and Sewing
  • Skills: Staying Healthy, and Tennis

Character Voiced by Azusa Tadokoro
