Mewtwo
  • Pokemon: Mewtwo-Prologue to Awakening
  • Pokemon Movie 16: Genesect and The Legend Awakened
Mewtwo is a Pokemon cloned from Mew. It was created by scientists through manipulation of Mew's DNA. Its creator did not imbue him with compassion, making it one of the most savage Pokemon. Its combat abilities and psychic powers developed to be of the highest level. In order to always fight at full power, it remains motionless to conserve energy.
