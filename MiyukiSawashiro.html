Miyuki Sawashiro

Miyuki Sawashiro is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production but was apart of Mausu Promotion. During her time in high school, she stayed in Pennsylvania. In Pennsylvania, Sawashiro learned English with a homestay program. When she is not voice acting, Miyuki practices English. She has a younger brother named Chiharu Sawashiro. She participated in voice actor auditions of Di Gi Charat on May 2, 1999. She voiced Puchiko in the English dub of Leave it to Piyoko but could not continued due to scheduling conflict. Sawashiro announced her marriage in 2014. Some of her major roles are Sinon in Sword Art Online, Shinku in Rozen Maiden, Jun Sasada in Natsume's Book of Friends, and Beelzebub in Beelzebub.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 2, 1985
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Piano
