Marika Matsumoto

Marika Matsumoto is a Japanese actress. She is the voice of Rikku in Fianl Fantasy X and X-2. She was known as "Mini Stop-chan", acting as the mascot for the Mini Stop convenience store chain commercials in Japan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 12, 1984
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Japanese Dancing, Photography, and Tap Dancing
  • Skills: Karate and Skiing

Characters Voiced by Marika Matsumoto
