Sanae Kobayashi

Sanae Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is currently affiliated with Sigma Seven. Some of her major roles Lucy/Nyu in Elfen Lied, Allen Walker in D.Gray-man, and Reiko Natsume in Natsume's Book of Friend.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 26, 1980
  • Hometown: Shizuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Calligraphy, Dancing, Drawing and Scuba Driving
  • Skill: Lincensed to Scuba Dive

Characters Voiced by Sanae Kobayashi

Greta Pokemon Advanced Generations Greta is Hoenn frontier Brain of the Battle Arena. She is a spirited, tomboyish young girl who is very passionate about Pokemon battles. She specializes in Fighting-Type Pokemon.

Pokemon Voiced by Sanae Kobayashi

Squirtle Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Team Go-Getters Out of the Gate! Squirtle is a Pokemon from the Kanto region. Squirtle is the water type starter that a trainer can choose in the Kanto region. It can spray foamy water from its mouth with great accuracy. Squirtle's shell is powerful tool for its protection.
