Azumi Waki

Azumi Waki is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. Although she had been interested in anime since she was young, she initially dreamed of becoming a flight attendant. She pursue this dream until the end of junior high school. However, she gave up of that dream because her English language skill. Her interest in anime grew, so she instead decided to pursue a voice acting career. Waki enrolled at the Tokyo Anime Voice Acting College after her third year of high school, where she work as a radio assistant personality while studying. After graduating from the college, she became affiliated with Haikyo. Her roles included Gaiko in Please Tell Me! Gaiko-chan, Marika Sakuranomiya in Blend S, and Rem Galeu in How Not to Summon a Demon Lord.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 8, 1994
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Azumi Waki

Kusue Hakozaki I'm Standing on a Million Lives Kusue Hakozaki is a middle student who is whisk away to another world. Due to being born with a weak constitution from a chronic disease, Kusue is very self-conscious about herself. When the time is right, she will display courage in the face of imminent danger, but right after she became scared and did run away. She hope to be a doctor one day.
