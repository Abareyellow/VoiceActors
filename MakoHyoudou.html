Mako Hyoudou

Mako Hyoudou is a Japanese voice actress. She is a member of 81 Produce company. She is in Pokemon and Peach Wedding.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 7, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Mako Hyoudou

Lola
  • Pokemon Chronicles
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Specials
Lola is a Water-type Pokemon specialist. She is the mother of Brock and Brock's siblings. She married to Flint. One of Lola's hobbies is decorating.
