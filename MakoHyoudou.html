Mako Hyoudou

Mako Hyoudou is a Japanese voice actress. She is a member of 81 Produce company. She is in Pokemon and Peach Wedding.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 7, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
