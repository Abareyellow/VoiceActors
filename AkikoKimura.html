Akiko Kimura

Akiko Kimura is a Japanese voice actress for the Pokemon anime and more. Her famous role is the role of Anabel. She has voice in animes such as Gintama, Hunter x Hunter, Prince of Tennis and Trinity Blood. Apart from voice acting, she is a certified elementary school teacher.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 28, 1971
  • Hometown: Fukui, Japan
  • Hobbies: Illustration
  • Skills: Aikido (2-dan) and teaching

Characters Voiced by Akiko Kimura

Anabel Pokemon Advanced Generations Anabel is a member of the Kanto Battle Frontier. She runs the battle tower. She has the power to sense Pokemon feelings and communicates with them. She calls every Pokemon her friend
Mick Pokemon: Black and White Mick is a young boy who met on his journey in Unova. He was a shy boy didn't have to much conifidence.
