Akiko Kimura

Akiko Kimura is a Japanese voice actress for the Pokemon anime and more. Her famous role is the role of Anabel from Pokemon. She has voice in animes such as Gintama as Kirie Rokkaku, Hunter x Hunteras Mito Freecss and Biscuit Kruger, Prince of Tennis as Ana Tachibana and Trinity Blood as Vanessa Walsh. Apart from voice acting, she is a certified elementary school teacher.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 28, 1971
  • Hometown: Fukui, Japan
  • Hobbies: Illustration
  • Skills: Aikido (2-dan) and teaching
