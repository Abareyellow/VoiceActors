Juurouta Kosugi

Juurouta Kosugi is a Japanese voice actor. His major roles are Arlong in One Piece and Akio Ohtori in Revolutionary Girl Utena. He provides voice for Jame Bond in Japanese dub in films and games.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 19, 1957
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
