Yoshitsugu Matsuoka

Yoshitsugu Matsuoka is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. He voiced Sora from No Game No Life, Soma Yukihara from Food Wars! Shokugeki no Soma, and Kirito in Sword Art Online.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 17, 1986
  • Hometown: Hokkaido, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Guitar, Jogging, Singing, Snowboarding, and Taking Walks
