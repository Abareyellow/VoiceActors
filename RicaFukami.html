Rica Fukami

Rica Fukami is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Vi-Vo. She and Emi Shinohara share the same birthday. Her debut is in Tokimeki Tonight. Her major roles are Minako Aino/Sailor Venus in Sailor Moon and Daiko Hayami in New Cutey Honey.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 8, 1963
  • Hometwon: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobby: Traveling
  • Skill: US-Certified Hypnotherapist
  • Husband: Eisuke Tsuda
