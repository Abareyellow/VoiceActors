Yumi Uchiyama

Yumi Uchiyama is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Office Osawa.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 30, 1987
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Yumi Uchiyama

Erika Chiba
  • The Irregular at Magic High School
  • The Irregular at Magic High School: Get to Know Magic Studies!
  • The Irregular at Magic High School The Movie - The Girl Who Summons The Stars
Erika Chiba is a Course 2 student and Tatsuya's 1-E classmate. One of Tatsuya first friends and close friends with Mikihiko, Leo and Mizuki. She has an outgoing personality. she befriends people easily and garners attention from those around her. She is a master of swordsmanship.
Siduri
  • Fate/Grand Order: Absolute Demonic Front - Babylonia
Siduri is the assistant of Gilgamesh. She is an elegant and intelligent lady who cares greatly for Uruk and its people. While having tremendous respect and trust for king, she is not afraid aganist him when she must. She is beloved by Uruk's citizeny and , is known to alaways have their best interests at heart.
Sophie
  • Sword Art Online Alternative: Gun Gale Online
Sophie is a player in Gun Gale Online. Her real name is Kana Fujisawa. She and her friends are part of their school gymnastic team.
