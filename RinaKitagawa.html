Rina Kitagawa

Rina Kitagawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Ken-On. Her debut anime role in Gatchaman Crowds!!. She has voiced Yuuko Oomori from Happiness Charge Precure! and Brianne de Chateau from Dragon Ball Super.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 6, 1993
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking
  • Skills: Calligraphy, Flute, and Violin

Character Voiced by Rina Kitagawa

Yumi Hinode A Whisker Away Yumi Hinode is the older sister of Kento Hinode. She is high school graduate who spends her days time lazily.
