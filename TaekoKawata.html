Taeko Kawata

Taeko Kawata is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She is best known in for her voice of Amy Rose from the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 20, 1965
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birth Name: Taeko Yamada
