Yui Sakakibara

Yui Sakakibara is a Japanese voice actress, choregrapher, dancer, and singer. Sakakibara is a multitalented lady who signed LOVETRAX OFFICE. She currently voiced Aoi and Akane Kotonoha.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 13, 1980
  • Birthplace: Hyogo, Japan

Character Voiced by Yui Sakakibara

Marie Dies Irae Marie or Marguerite is a cursed girl who lived France during the 18th Century. She is a mysterious woman in Ren Fujii's Dream.
