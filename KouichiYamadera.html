Kouichi Yamadera

Kouichi Yamadera is a Japanese actor, voice actor, narrator and singer. He is affiliated with Across Entertainment and graduated Tohoku Gakuin University's economics school. He was the manager of the Tagajo Senior High School basketball before he became famous. His debut role is the OVA Megazone 23 as Shinji Nakagawa. He was once married to Mika Kanai and Rie Tanaka, but divorced. He is the official Japanese dubbed voice of Donald Duck.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 17, 1961
  • Hometown: Shiogama, Miyagi, Japan
