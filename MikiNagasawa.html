Miki Nagasawa

Miki Nagasawa is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Haikyo, but now affiliated with Atomic Monkey. Some of Nagasawa's voice in anime are Wedy in Death Note, Ryo Hayakawa in Princess Nine, and Toki in Naruto.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 11, 1970
  • Hometown: Fukushima, Japan
