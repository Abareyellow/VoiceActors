Hirofumi Nojima

Hirofumi Nojima is a Japanese actor and narrator. Nojima is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He is the son of Akio Nojima and older brother of Kenji Nojima. He voices the boss villian Commandant Warz Gill in Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger. He well known for his role of Akatsuki Izumo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 16, 1973
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Father: Akio Nojima
  • Brother: Kenji Nojima
