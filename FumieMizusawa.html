Fumie Mizusawa

Fumie Mizusawa is a Japanese actress, voice actress and novelist. She is affiliated by Sigma Seven. Mizusawa got married on April 16, 2011 to someone outside of acting. Her notable work is Juliet from the anime Romeo and Juliet.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 9, 1980
  • Hometown: Tochigi, Japan

Character Voiced by Fumie Mizusawa

Juliet Fiammata Asto Capulet Romeo x Juliet Juliet Capulet is the last member of the Capulet family. For a long period of time, she had to dress like a boy to pervent harm to her from the Montague. She is pacifistic and kind by nature. She tries to find an alternative solution.
