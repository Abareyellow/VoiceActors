Masami Nagasawa

Masami Nagasawa is a Japanese actress and model. She is a Japan Academy Prize, a Mainichi Film Award and a Blue Ribbon Award. Masami Nagasawa is the Grand Prix winner of 5th Toho Cinderella audition.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 3, 1987
  • Birthplace: Iwata, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Masami Nagasawa

Miki Okudera Your Name Miki Okudera is an university student. She works in the same restaurant as Taki. She and Taki have a mutual crush on each other.
