Haruna Ikezawa

Haruna Ikezawa is a Japanese voice actress born in Athens, Greece. She was affiliated with 81 Produce but switch to Oscar Promotion until December 31, 2013. She is currently free right now. She is the oldest daughter of Akutagawa Prze winning author Natsuki Ikezawa and granddaughter to Takehito Fukunaga. She is a member of Seiyu group More Peach Summer Snow. She voices light - hearted or mysterious roles.
