Rumi Ookubo

Rumi Ookubo is a japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. Her roles included Ako Shirabe/Cure Muse in Suite PreCure, Suguri Kinoshita in Happy Kappy, and Mia Ageha in Pretty Rhythm.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 27, 1989
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Favorites: Bear Character and Digimon
  • Hobbies: Fashion and Shopping
  • Skills: Commit to Memory

Characters Voiced by Rumi Ookubo

Rider of Black Fate/Apocrypha Rider of Black is the Rider-class of Selenik Icecol Yggdmilennia as part of Black Faction in the Turifas Holy Grail War. Rider's true name is Astolfo, one of 12 Paladins of Charlemagne. He is very optimistic and completely lacking senses.
Souka That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Souka is Gabiru's younger sister and Abiru's daughter. She is one of Souei trainees. Souka is a dutiful and respectful person who thinks about other well being. She is good at pressuring others.
