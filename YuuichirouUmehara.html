Yuuichirou Umehara

Yuuichirou Umehara is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Art Vision. On May 10, 2018, it announced that Umehara was hosptialized due to an acute disseminated encephalomyelitis. He recovered and came back on July 30, 2018. He voiced En Yufuin in Cute High Earth Defense Club LOVE! and Jay Yun in Last Hope.
