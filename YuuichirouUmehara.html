Yuuishirou Umehara

Yuuichirou Umehara is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Art Vision. On May 10, 2018, it announced that Umehara was hosptialized due to an acute disseminated encephalomyelitis. He recovered and came back on July 30, 2018. He voiced En Yufuin in Cute High Earth Defense Club LOVE! and Jay Yun in Last Hope.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 8, 1991
  • Hometown: Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Classic Music Appreciation, Piano, Guitar and Singing

Characters Voiced by Yuuichirou Umehara

Eiji Kashii Juuni Taisen Eiji Kashii is the warrior of the Ox. He simply kills his enemies with his sword.
Shizu Kino's Journey: The Beautiful World - The Animated Series Shizu is a young man with a great talent for swordsmanship. He travels with his dog companions, Riku. He is a reasonable person and not greedy. Shizu has a soft spot for children.
