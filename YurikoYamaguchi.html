Yuriko Yamaguchi

Yuriko Yamaguchi is a Japanese voice actress. She was once affiliated with 81 Produce but now represents by Vi-Vo. Her voice acting coach was Hitoshi Takagi. She is best known for her roles of Nico Robin in One Piece and Nurse Joy in Pokemon.

  • Birthday: November 21, 1965
