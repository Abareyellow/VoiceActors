Shigeru Ushiyama

Shigeru Ushiyama is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is afffiliated with Gekidan Subaru. He is married to voice actress Keiko Aizawa. He voiced Doctor Hiluluk from One Piece and Robita from Phoenix.
