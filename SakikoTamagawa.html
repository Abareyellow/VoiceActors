Sakiko Tamagawa

Sakiko Tamagawa is a Japanese acress, voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Sigma Seven. She voices Tachikama in Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex, Natsumi Tsujimoto in You're Under Arrest, and Juiz in Eden of the East. She is currently married to Shuuichi Ikeda.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 20, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Husband: Shuuichi Ikeda
