Sakura Nakamura

Sakura Nakamura is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She debuted as a unnamed character in Mayo Chiki!. Along with that, she voiced Akebi Sasaki in Girls & Panzar and Hibari Takada in Two Car.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 25
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Sakura Nakamura

Callum Norad By the Grace of the Gods Callum Norad is an employee at Ryoma's Bamboo Forest Laundry Service and Carla's brother. He, like his sister, act like robots before meeting Ryoma. After working at Laundry Service, he and his sister stated to open up a bit and show their personality.
