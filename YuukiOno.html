Yuuki Ono

Yuuki Ono is a Japanese voice actor and singer. When he was in high school, Ono wanted to be a soccer player. He stopped due to a bone fracture. Ono is a big fan of Kisho Taniyama. After meeting Taniyama in person, he was inspired to pursuethe same career. He is currently marry to some outside the industry. Ono voiced Balance/Tenbin Gold from Uchuu Sentai Kyuranger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 22, 1984
  • Birthplace: Shizuoka, Japan
