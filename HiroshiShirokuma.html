Hiroshi Shirokuma

Hiroshi Shirokuma is a Japanese voice actor. He voices in iconic animes such as Fairy Tail, Baccano! and Gintama.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 4, 1979
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
