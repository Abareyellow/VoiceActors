Ryouta Takeuchi

Ryouta Takeuchi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He voiced Elias Ainsworth in The Ancient Magus' Bride, Shiro in Assassination Classroom, and Wakatoshi Ushijima in Haikyu.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 22, 1982
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ryouta Takeuchi

Elias Ainsworth
  • The Ancient Magus' Bride: Those Awaiting a Star
  • The Ancient Magus' Bride
Elias Ainsworth is the ancient magician with both human and fae characteristics. Despite his appearance, he is gentle and polite with human and shows a great interest in them. He understands human feelings and emotion but never experienced them.
