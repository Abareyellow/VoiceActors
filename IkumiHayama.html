Ikumi Hayama

Ikumi Hayama is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Clare Voice.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 17, 1984
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Birthname: Ikumi Fujiwara
  • Hobbies: Darts and Tennis
