Ayaka Fukuhara

Ayaka Fukuhara is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with VIMS. At one point of her life, she dreamed of becoming a veterinarian. After listening to a radio drama adaptation of manga series Hunter x Hunter. She became aware of the occupation of voice acting. She took correspondence training to improve her voice akills, while reading manga aloud in order to practice her skills. In high school, she was a member of her school's broading club and later participated in the national radio contest sponsered by NHK. While getting a degree in nutrition at a university, she attended the Japan Narration Acting Institute. Her debut role was Rin Shibuya in The Idolmaster Cinderella Cirls. She has voiced in Myoko in Arpeggio of Blue Steel and Hotaru Rindo in Qualidea Code.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 31, 1989
  • Hometown: Kagoshima, Japan
