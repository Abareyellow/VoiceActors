Youko Hikasa

Youko Hikasa is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. Hikasa was aspiredto become a voice actress after watching Sailor moon. She attended Nihon Narration Engi Kenkyujo, a voice actor training school. She is a member of Ro-Kyu-Bu!

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 16, 1985
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Sports: Softball and Basketball
  • Hobbies: Fashion
