Houchu Ootsuka

Houchu Ootsuka or Houchu Ohtsuka is a Japanese voice actor. He affiliated with talent management firm Crazy Box. He voices Signalman from Gekisou Sentai Carranger, Jiraiya from Naruto and Abuto from Gintama.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 19, 1954
  • Spouse: Kazuko Yanaga
  • Hometown: Okayama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball, Bicycling, Golf

Characters Voiced by Houchu Ootsuka

  • Pokemon

Koga was the gym leader of Fuchisa City's Gym. He is a student of ninjutsu who specializes in Poison-type Pokemon. He is a ninja who trains in a mansion.

  • Kino's Journey
Riku is a talking dog who travels with Shizu. He is large, white and always smiles. Riku is loyal to Shizu.
