Miyoko Asou

Miyoko Asou is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is best known for her role of Fune Isono from 1969-2015 in the longest-running Japanese anime series Sazae-san. She setted down from the role on September 18, 2015. She is the voice of Flora form the Japanese dub of Sleeping Beauty.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 7, 1926
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Scuba Diving
