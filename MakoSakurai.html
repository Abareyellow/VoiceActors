Mako Sakurai

Mako Sakurai or Mako is a Japanese voice actress. singer, and a member of the band Bon-Bon Blanco. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She has perfromed in a Japanese television drama called Meido in Akihabara. Her anime voice acting debut in Kamichu as Yurie Hitotsubashi. She has also voiced in Jewelpet series as Charotte and Hanamaru Kindergarten as Koume.

  • Birthday: October 7, 1986

  • Hobbies: Chatting Over the Phone and Fortune-Telling
