Fumiko Orikasa

Fumiko Orikasa is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer who works for Atomic Monkey. She is well known for her voices in Bleach and Street Fighter.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 27, 1974
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Shopping and Dancing
