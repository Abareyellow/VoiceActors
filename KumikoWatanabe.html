Kumiko Watanabe

Kumiko Watanabe is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Art Vision. She has done voices for Pretty Cure series, Sailor Moon series and InuYasha.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 7, 1965
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Watching Professional Wrestling
  • Husband: Kouji Tsujitani
