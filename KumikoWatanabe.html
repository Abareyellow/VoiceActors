Kumiko Watanabe

Kumiko Watanabe is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Art Vision. She has done voices for Pretty Cure series, Sailor Moon series and InuYasha.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 7, 1965
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Watching Professional Wrestling
  • Husband: Kouji Tsujitani

Characters Voiced by Kumiko Watanabe

Jeffrey
  • Fresh Pretty Cure!
Jeffrey is the Prince of a royal family.
JunJun
  • Sailor Moon Super S
  • Sailor Moon Super S Memorial
JunJun is one of the members of the Amazoness Quartlet who serves for Zirconia. She is athletic and somewhat impulsive. She is an acrobat in the Circus, referred to as the Magician of Acrobatic Feats. Her Senshi counterpart is Sailor Jupiter. JunJun uses very informal male and vuglar speech.
Yu Natsuki
  • Yes! Pretty Cure 5
  • Yes! Pretty Cure 5 GoGo!
Yu Natsuki is a younger brother of Rin Natsuki and twin of Ai Natsuki. Both him and his sister are so naughty that they always get in trouble. Yu is normal boy despite his older sister being a magical girl.
