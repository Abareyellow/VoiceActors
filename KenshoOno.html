Kensho Ono

Kensho Ono is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He was affiliated by Sunaoka Office but now represents Animo Produce. He has appeared in numerous anime and Japanese TV drama series. He is known for role in the Prince of Tennis musical. He is not related to Daisuke Ono or Yuki Ono. He currently has a relationship with Kana Hanazawa.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 5, 1989
  • Birthplace: Fukuroka Prefecture, Japan
