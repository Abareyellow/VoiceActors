Kazuhiro Nakata

Kazuhiro Nakata is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Ken Production. He voiced Bear in .hack series and Fei-Wong Reed in Tsubasa Chronicle.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 19, 1958
  • Hometown: Nagasaki, Japan

Character Voiced by Kazuhiro Nakata

Victor Pokemon Victor is a surfer who owned a Pikachu. His Pikachu can sense waves when they are coming. He is trying to surf Humungadunga, a huge wave.
