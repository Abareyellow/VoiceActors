Satomi Sato

Satomi Sato is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated Aoni Production. Sato is a graduate of Tokyo Announce Gakuin Performing Arts College. Ahe is best known for her roles as Ritsu Tainaka in K-On! and Wendy Marvell in Fairy Tail. Satomi is married to Takuma Terashima since July 6, 2017.
