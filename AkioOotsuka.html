Akio Ootsuka

Akio Ootsuka is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is with Mausu Promotion. His voice landed him several roles in films, dubbing, animation and video games. He has voiced in many animes such as Black Jack, Bleach, One Piece, Detective Conan and Gudnam series. Most notable video games voicing roles is Metal Gear and Kingdom Hearts. He has done for Super Sentai sinces 1995's Ohranger. Akio Ootsuka most notable Super Sentai role is Champ/Oushi Black from Kyuranger.

  • Birthday: November 24, 1959
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
