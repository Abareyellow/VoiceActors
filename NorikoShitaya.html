Noriko Shitaya

Noriko Shitaya is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Sakura Matou form Fate/stay Night and Lailah from Tales of Zestiria the X.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 22, 1982
  • Birthplace: Chiba Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Making Pizza, Skiing, Swimming, and Volleyball
