Shizuka Itou

Shizuka Itou is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Ken Production. Her and Hitomi Nabatame formed a voice acting unit called Hitoshizuka. On 32nd birthday, she got married. She voiced Hinagiku Katsura in Hayate the Combat Butler!, Akeno Himejima in High School DxD, and Meiko Shiraki in Prison School.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 5, 1980
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
