Aki Toyosaki

Her carrer in music began with her performance for the opening and ending theme for the anime K-On in April 2009. She and three other voice actresses create a music group called Spheres. Their debuted in April 2009 with their Future Stream. She was given Animation Kobe's "Best Song" and Best Musical Performance awards.
