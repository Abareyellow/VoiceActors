Aki Toyosaki

Aki Toyosaki is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Her carrer in music began with her performance for the opening and ending theme for the anime K-On in April 2009. She and three other voice actresses create a music group called Spheres. Their debuted in April 2009 with their Future Stream. She was given Animation Kobe's "Best Song" and Best Musical Performance awards.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 28, 1986
  • Hometown: Tokushima

Characters Voiced by Aki Toyosaki

Homura
  • Dr. Stone
Homura is the right-hand woman of Hyoga in Tsukasa's army. She is loyal to Hyoga and has romantic feelings for him. She is cautious, observant, and intelligent. She manages to remember names of all the people in Ishigami Village. She maintains a cool composure. She has great speed and gymnastic ability. She will complete her task regardless the situation.
Olivinis
  • Children of the Whales
Olivinis is a mysterious being living the Mud Whale.
Seilah

Seilah is a member of the Dark Tartarus and the Nine Demon Gates Team.
Tetsudo Hisakawa
  • Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day
  • Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day Movie

He hides his guilt for Menma's death undernearth his happy-go-lucky personality. Since he dropped out of school, he travels the world with the money he has earn by taking various part time jobs.
