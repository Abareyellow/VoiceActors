Natsuko Kuwatani

Natsuko Kuwatani is a Japanese voice actress. She works for I'm Enterprise. Her major roles are Yue Ayasw in Negima, Fiore in Chrono Crusade and Suiseiseki in Rozen Maiden.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 8, 1978
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke and Swimming

Character Voiced by Natsuko Kuwatani

Fiore Harvenheit Chrono Crusade Fiore is Joshua's guardian who is very protective of him. She is a powerful jewel summoner. She appears to be cold and emotionless but, She is a very sweet and caring girl. She cooks and capable of being a maid.
