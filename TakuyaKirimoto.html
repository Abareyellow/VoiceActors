Takuya Kirimoto

Takuya Kirimoto is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Ivan Glaziev in Gangsta. and Taira in Freedom.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 27, 1967
  • Hometown: Gifu, Japan

Character Voiced by Takuya Kirimoto

Belserion Fairy Tail: Final Season Belserion is the Sage Dragon who allied himself with humans during Dragon King Festival. He is loyal and righteous, wanting to serve and protect his queen, Irene. He cares for humans, due to them saving his life in his past.
