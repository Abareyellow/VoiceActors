Keiko Takeshita

Keiko Takeshita is a Japanese voice actress. She starred in the Japanese version of From Up on Poppy Hill as Hana Matsuzaki.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 15, 1953
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan
  • Husband: Teruo Sekiguchi
