Harumi Sakurai

Harumi Sakurai is a Japanese voice actress. Some of her prominentt roles in Angel Beats!, Little Busters!, Ecstasy, and Fairy Tail. Jun Maeda recommended her to be Yuri's voice actress in Angel Beats! because he believes her voices her voice fits the character.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 21, 1982
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

  • Fairy Tail OVA (2016)

Lisanna Strauss is the younger sister of both Elfman and Mirajane. She is a member of Fairy Tail. She, like her siblings, can use Take Over magic. She loves animals but hates studying. She can easily adjust and be accepted by anyone.
