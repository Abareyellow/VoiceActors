Rintarou Nishi

Rintarou Nishi or Kazuaki Kurita is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Office Osawa. He voiced Dokoku Chimatsu in Samurai Sentai Shinkenger. Nishi's most popular roles are Barta Liones from The Seven Deadly Sins and Jason from Tokyo Ghoul.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 15, 1965
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
