Yuuki Takada

Yuuki Takada is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She is a graduate of the Fukuoka branch of Yoyogi Animation Gakuin. Takada was accepted into the training school of Mausu Promotion following graduation in 2013 and joined the agency in 2015. She debuted as a seiyuu in Bayonetta: Bloody Fate as some extra characters. She has voiced Rin Kurosawa in Aikatsu!, Aoba Suzukaze in New Game!, and Babylon in Infinite Dendrogram.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 16, 1992
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Mentaiko Eating Comparsion, Singing and Sock Collecting
