Kenta Miyake

Kenta Miyake is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. After the death of Hirotaka Suzuoki death, he took over the role of Giovanni in the Pokemon anime series. He voiced Furious Demon Good Gai from GoGo Sentai Boukenger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 23, 1977
  • Hometown: Okinawa Prefecture, Japan
  • Skills: Judo
