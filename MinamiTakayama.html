Minami Takayama

Minami Takayama or Izumi Arai is a Japanese voice actress, singer, and composer. She is apart of DoCo when it was active. Minami is part of a pop group Two-Mix with Shiina Nagano. She was married to manga artist Gosho Aoyama in 2005, but divored on December 10, 2007. She is best known for roles in Kiki's Delivery Service as Kiki and Ursula, Ranma 1/2 as Nabiki Tendo, Yaiba as Yaiba Kurogane, and Detective Conan as Conan Edogawa.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 5, 1964
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Real Name: Izumi Arai
