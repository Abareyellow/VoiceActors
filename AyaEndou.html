Aya Endou

Aya Endou is a Japanese voice actress. At the 2nd Seiyu Awards, she won Best Song. In the 3rd Seiyu Awards, she won Best Supporting Actress. She is currently the official Japanese dub for Minnie Mouse. She is currently married.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 17, 1980
  • Birthplace: Yamagata, Japan
