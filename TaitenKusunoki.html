Taiten Kusunoki

Taiten Kusunoki is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Amuleto. He is the besting knowing dubbing roles for Vin Diesel and Dwayne Johnson. He voiced Liogre in Beast Saga, Ryujin Zanba in All Out!! and Black King in Drifters.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 18, 1967
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Real Name: Daisuke Fukuda
  • Hobby: Biking

Character Voiced by Taiten Kusunoki

Elean Duga
  • Baccano!
  • Baccano! Specials
Elean Duga is an information broker at the Daily Days and the copy editor of the newspaper's Chinese edition. He is well known for episodes and his used of deprecatory language. He comes across of well-meaning and good-hearted. He would rather give a client good news than bad news.
