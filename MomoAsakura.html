Momo Asakura

Momo Asakura is a Japanese voice actess and singer. She is affiliated with Ray'n Agency. Asakura's interest in becoming a voice actress began in middle school and continued through high school. She got her break in 2011 when, together with Sora Amamiya and Shiina Natsukawa held by Sony Music Entertainmant Japan's sub-label Music Ray'n. Her debut role is Yumi in My Little Monster. She has voiced in Ayumi Otoska in Charlotte and Sumi Otokawa in Sakura Trick.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 25, 1994
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan

Character Voiced by Momo Asakura

Nadja Liones The Seven Deadly Sins: Wrath of the Gods Nadja Liones was a princess of the Kingdom of Liones, the older of Barta and Denzel Linones. She is a kind girl who loves to read, her favorite books being adventure stories. Her frail body stops her from going outside the castle walls. Along with being kind, she is very caring and accepting.She accept Gowther and cares for him.
