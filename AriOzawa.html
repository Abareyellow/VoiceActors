Ari Ozawa

Ari Ozawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. Ozawa decided to become a voice actress after becoming a fan of Negima! when she was in the Seventh Grade. Her first leading role in an anime is Chiyo Sakura in Monthly Girls' Nozaki-kun. She has alos voiced Airi Arcadia in Undeafeated Bahamut Chronicle, Al in Flying Witch, and Ayame Seki in Magic of Stella.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 10, 1992
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ari Ozawa

Airi Arcadia Undefeated Bahamut Chronicles Airi Arcadia is Lux Arcadia's younger sister and a student of the Royal Officer Academy. She is a survivor of the Old Arcadia Empire's Imperial Family, making her a former princess. She has a very calm temperant. She is critical of her brother, but at the same time is confident in his strength. She becomes flustered when other people tease her about her relationship with Lux.
Conny The Promised Neverland Conny was an orphan at the Grace Field House before she was shipped off. She was a kind, soft-spoken, and gentle girl who loved her family deeply, especially Don. She was compassionate and caring, as aspired to become a mother. She was a little absent-minded person and not the smartest out her siblings.
