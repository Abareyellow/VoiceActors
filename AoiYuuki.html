Aoi Yuuki

Aoi Yabasaki or better known Aoi Yuuki is japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She is represent by Pro-Fit agency. She been in the entertainment industry at he age of four. In her childhood, she acted in films and dramas. By the time Aoi was in fifth grade, she made her debut role as a voice actress. Her notable roles are Iris from Pokemon and Victorique de Blois from Gosick. In 2010, she graduated from high school. In 2012, she was awarded Best Lead Actress Award in 6th Seiyu Awards. Yuuki graduated college in 2014.
