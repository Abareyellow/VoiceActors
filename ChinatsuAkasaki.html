Chinatsu Akasaki

Chinatsu Akasaki is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She was aspired to be a voice actor in fifth and sixth grade. She was in her middle school basketball club and broadcasting club. She has two sisters. She gradulated from Tokyo Gakugei University. She is affiliated with 81 Produce.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 10, 1987
  • Birthplace: Kagostima, Japan
