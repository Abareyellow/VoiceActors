Aya Suzaki

Aya Suzaki is a Japanese voice actress, singer and narrator. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She recieved one of the Best Female Newcomers at the 9th Seiyu Awards in March 2015. She is known for her role of Tamako Kitashirakawa in Tamako Market.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 25, 1986
  • Hometown: Ishikawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Scuda Driving, Singing, and Soft Tennis
  • Skill: Flower Arrangement

Character Voiced by Aya Suzaki

Selina Sherlock
  • Trinity Seven
  • Trinity Seven OVA
  • Trinity Seven Movie 1: Eternity Library & Alchemic Girl
  • Trinity Seven Movie 2: Heavens Library to Crimson Lord
Selina Sherlock is the younger twin sister of Lieselotte and student reporter in Royal Biblia Academy. She is more kinder than her sister. She has an energetic personality and a great photographer. Selina is able to use healing magic with her camera.
