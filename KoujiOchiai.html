Kouji Ochiai

Kouji Ochiai is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He voiced Joe Gibson in Major and Trudge/Tetsu Ushio in Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 4, 1966
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
