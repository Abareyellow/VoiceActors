Aimi Tanaka

Aimi Tanaka is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. Her most notable work is Umaru Dome in Himouto! Umaru-chan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 28, 1992
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Aimi Tanaka

Berta Food Wars! The Third Plate: Totsuki Train Arc Berta is the staff member under the Research Divison of Nakiri International. She and her sister Cilla are Leonora Nakiri's subordinates.
