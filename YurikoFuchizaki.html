Yuriko Fuchizaki

Yuriko Fuchizaki is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is afiliated with Re-max. She is a graduate of the College of Fine Arts at Nihon University. Fuchizaki began voicing in Japanese dub of American films. Her anime debut was the role of Kishin Shinokawa in Persia, The Magic Fairy when she was in 10th grade. She is the middle child out 3 kids. She got married in 2005 and is the mother of twins son.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 5, 1968
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
