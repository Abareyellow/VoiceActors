Konami Yoshida

Konami Yoshida is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced Umi Ryuuzaki in Rayearth and Reki in Orphen.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 6, 1967
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
