Mitsuki Saiga

Mitsuki Saiga is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated Ken Production. She has a deep voice that is used to protray male or masculine female characters. Her major roles are Tsukasa in .hack, Maria Ross in Fullmetal Alchemist, and Chie in My-Hime.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 23, 1973
  • Birthplace: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading, Singing and Tennis
