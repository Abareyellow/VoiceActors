Rie Kugimiya

Rie Kugimiya is a Japanese voice actor and singer. She is affiliated I'm Enterprise. Some of Rie's iconic roles Alphonse Elric from Fullmetal Alchemist series, Happy from Fairy Tail series and Kagura from Gintama series. Along with the her lead roles, she voices Cure Ace in Precure Dokidoki. In the Tomagotchi fandom, she is known for roles as Mametchi in anime world. She currently voices Jim Carter in Kaitou Sentai Lupinranger VS Keisatsu Sentai Patranger.
