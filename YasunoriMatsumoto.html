Yasunori Matsumoto

Yasunori Matsumoto is a Japanese voice actor and actor. He is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He is married to Junko Sakuma. He voiced Ryo Hibiki in Sonic Soldier Borgman and King Endon in Deltora Quest.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 7, 1960
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
