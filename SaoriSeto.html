Saori Seto

Saori Seto is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Mausu Promotion until 2014. She did the voiceover for Allison "Sonny" Monroe in the Japanese dub of Sonny With A Chance.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 3, 1982
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Horseback Riding and Skiing
  • Skills: Funeral MC
