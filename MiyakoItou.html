Miyako Itou

Miyako Itou is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Ken Production. She has voice multiple Pokemon Characters.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 23, 1969
  • Hometown: Morioka, Iwate, Japan

Characters Voiced by Miyako Itou

Martha
  • Pokemon Black and White: Rival Destinies
Martha was Iris's dorm adviser in Opelucid City's Academy when Iris was young.

Pokemon Voiced by Miyako Itou

Aipom
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Aipom is a Pokemon from Johto region. Aipom uses its powerful tail freely and cleverly from many purposes. Aipom has been known to wrap its tail around trees branches as it sleeps so it does not fall. Miyako Itou voiced Ash's/Dawn's Aipom.
Bulbasaur
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
  • Pokemon Chronicles
Bulbasaur is a Pokemon from the Kanto region. Bulbasaur is a starter Pokemon that a trainer can choose in Kanto. Bulbasaur are loyal to their trainers. It uses its vines to pick up young Pokemon. Miyako Itou voiced May's and Gilbert's Bulbasaurs.
Happiny
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
  • Pokemon Movie 10: The Rise of Darkrai
  • Pokemon Movie 11: Giratina and The Sky Warrior
  • Pokemon Movie 12: Arceus and the Jewel of Life
Happiny is a Pokemon from the Sinnoh region. Happiny's pouch is rubbery and flexible, enabling Happiny to put things into it with ease. Happinys are female only-species. Miyako Itou voiced Brock's Happiny.
Mime Jr.
  • Pokemon Movie 8: Lucario and The Mystery of Mew
Mime Jr. is a Pokemon from the Sinnoh region. Mime Jr. has a knack for imitating other. Once the foe is confused, it will quickly escape. Miyako Itou voiced Queen Ilene's Mime Jr.
Mismagius
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Mismagius is a Pokemon from the Sinnoh region. It is the evolved form of Misdreavus. People that near it s cries will usually receive headaches and halluciantions. Miyako Itou voiced Zoey's Mismagius.
Pikachu
  • Pokemon XY
Pikachu is the main Pokemon in all of Pokemon. Pikachu is from the Kanto region and the evolved form of Pichu. Pikachu can recharge each other with their build up energy. Miyako Itou voiced one of Frank's Pikachu.
Spoink
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
Spoink is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. The pearls amplifies its psychic power. Spoink will be weakened without a pearl.

Totodile
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Totodile is a Pokemon from the Johto region. Totodile is a starter that trainer can get in the Johto region. Totodile tends to be playful by nature. Miyako Itou voiced Khoury's Totodile.
Turtwig
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Turtwig is a Pokemon from the Sinnoh region. Turtwig is the grass starter in the Sinnoh region. It usually nourishes itself through photosynthesis. Miyako Itou voiced Gardenia's Turtwig.
