Kei Mizusawa

Kei Mizusawa is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with Yellow Tail. She is mostly known for her work in Visual Novels and voicing older sister characters.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 14
  • Hometown: Nagasaki, Japan

Character Voiced by Kei Mizusawa

Beatrice Waltrud von Kircheisen
  • Dies Irae: The Dawning Days
  • Dies Irae
Beatrice Waltrud von Kircheisen is a masterful combatant. She had trained for decades in an endless cycle of combat, death and reincarnation as a member of the Longinus Dreizehn Orden.
