Ayaka Saitou

Ayaka Saitou is a japanese actress and voice actress. She was formerly with Vocal, now transferring to Remax. She well known for her roles in Fruits Basket, Girls Bravo, Ouran High School Host Club and Jewelpet.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 2, 1988
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
