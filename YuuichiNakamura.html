Yuuichi Nakamura

Yuuichi Nakamura is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Sigma Seven. He voiced Gray Fullbuster in Fairy Tail, Gai Tsutsugami in Guilty Crown, and Hotaro Oreki in Hyoka. He also voiced Beet J. Stag/Stage Buster in Tokumei Sentai Go-Buster.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 20, 1980
  • Hometown: Kagawa, Japan
