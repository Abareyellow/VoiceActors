Yoshino Takamori

Yoshino Takamori is a Japanese voice actress. Takamori is a member of Art Vision.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 23, 1963
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Piano

Characters Voiced by Yoshino Takamori

Sloth
  • Fullmetal Alchemist
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Reflections
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Premium Collection
Sloth is a homunculus transmute from Trisha Elric. She has the power to control water.
Trisha Elric
  • Fullmetal Alchemist
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood
Trisha Elric is the mother of Edward and Alphonse Elric. She is also the wife of Van Hohenheim. She is a kind, sweet, gentle and loving woman.
