Kenyuu Horiuchi

Kenyuu Horiuchi is a Japanese voice actor. In 2002, he founded his own voice acting management office called Kenyu Office.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 30, 1957
  • Hometown: Shizuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Golf and Singing
