Chiwa Saito

Chiwa Saito is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She is half of a Seiyu duo called "Coopee". She worked in amines such as Yona of Dawn, Air, Aria, Baccano!, Berserk and Gintama. She is good friend of Kozuo Amano. Chiwa got married on July 29, 2013. Her daughter was born in 2015.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 12, 1981
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke
