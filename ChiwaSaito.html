Chiwa Saito

Chiwa Saito is a Japanese voice actress affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She is half of a Seiyu duo called "Coopee". She voiced in animes such as Yona of Dawn as Yona, Air as Ryuuya, Aria as Aika S. Granzchesta, Baccano! as Carol, Berserk as Schierke and Gintama as Kuriko Matsudaira. She is good friend of Kozuo Amano. Chiwa got married on July 29, 2013. Her daughter was born in 2015.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 12, 1981
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke
