Miyu Irino

Miyu Irino is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He's good friends with voice actor Mamoru Miyano though their primary years. He does not want consider himself as a seiyu. Irino voice the lead character Sora in the Kingdom Heart video game series. He voiced Daisuke Niwa in D.N.Angel and Ishida Shouya in A Slient Voice.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 18, 1988
  • Nick Names: Miyu-Miyu and Miyu-kun
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dancing, Playing Soccer, and Singing
  • Skills: Playing the Guitar, Dancing and Juggling
  • Favorite Food: Watermelon
