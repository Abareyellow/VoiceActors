Nobutoshi Canna

Nobutoshi Canna or Nobutoshi Hayashi is a Japanese voice actor and singer. Nobutoshi is affiliated in Aoni Production. He voiced in Knuckles the Echidna form Sonic X anime and Sonic the Hedgehog series games.
