Akihiro Miwa

Akihiro was born into a family who ran a small cafe. He was inspired to have a interest in music, after seeing the Boy Saprano at the age of 11 and had a dream about singing. He attended the Kunitachi College of Music High School at the age of 15. His career started at the age of 17 as a professional cabarent singer. He worked at various nightclubs singing his favorite French Chansons. He written many books about the government, social issues and war. He wrote a song about one of his childrenhood friends named "Yoitomake no Uta". The song was banned in Japan because he use several "discriminating" words until 2012. When he sang the song on stage he wears dark clothing and he has long yellow hair.
