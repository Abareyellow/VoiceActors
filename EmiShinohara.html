Emi Shinohara

Emi Shinohara is a Japanese voice actress and singer employed 81 Produce. She is most famous for her role of Sailor Jupiter from Sailor Moon. Her husband is Hiroshi Watari. She and Rica Fukami have the same birthday, August 8. She voices one of the main anatogist in Bakuryu Sentai Abaranger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 8, 1963
  • Hometown: Nagano, Japan
