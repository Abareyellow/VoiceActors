Yuu Kobayashi

Yuu Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Holy Peak. Before becoming a voice actress, Kobayashi worked as a model for magazines. In 2013, she auitioned for the role of Setsuna Sakurazaki in Negima! Magister Negi Magi. This role was the beginning of her career as a voice actress. She debut as singer in 2007. She created the musical group Crush Tears. On December 29, 2017, she announced her marriage on her Twitter account. Her major roles were Masaki Hijiri in Saint October, Sasha Braus in Attack on Titan and Dan Kuso in Bakugan Battle Brawlers.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 5, 1982
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
