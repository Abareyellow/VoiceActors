Eriko Hara

Eriko Hara is a Japanese voice actress. She was formerly apart of Arts Vision. She voiced Sailor Iron Mouse from Sailor Moon Super S and Ayako in Slam Dunk.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 1, 1959
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Eriko Hara

Unazuki Furuhata
  • Sailor Moon R
  • Sailor Moon S
Unazuki Furuhata is the sister of Motoki Furuhata. She works as a waitress at the Crown Fruit Parlor.
