Mitsuaki Hoshino

Mitsuaki Hoshino is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He is currently married to Yuko Kobayashi. He voiced Mayor in BEASTARS, Bunji Inukai in Monkey Turn, and Tetsuguro Mikijiro in Plastic Memories.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: Feburary 19, 1959
  • Hometown: Shizuoka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Movie and Soundtrack Story

Redford
  • Izetta: The Last Witch
Redford is the Britannian Foreign Secretary who is sympathetic to Eylstadt's cause.
