Chiaki Omigawa

Chiaki Omigawa is a Japanese actress and seiyu. She is affiliated with Hirata Office. Her debut role was Maka Albarn in Soul Eater.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 11, 1989
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
