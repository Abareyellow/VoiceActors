Akiko Nakagawa

Akiko Nakagawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Haikyo. She is married to InuYasha music composer, Kaoru Wada. She voiced Bomper from Engine Sentai Go-Onger.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 1, 1973
  • Hometown: Hokkaido, Japan
