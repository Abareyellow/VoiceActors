Nao Fujita

Nao Fujita is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated by VIMS. She has voiced Sister Krone from The Promised Neverland and Rakko from Nukko.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 14
  • Hometown: Nara, Japan

Character Voiced by Nao Fujita

Krone The Promised Neverland Krone is a caretaker who helped run the Grace Field House orphange with Isabella. She wants to be Mama for an orphanage and will stop at nothing to achieved her dream. She has learned to trust no one but herself. Under her affectionate, kind, and warm exterior, she is cunning, highly aggressive and exetremely manipulatively.She is a prideful woman with a strong hatred towards the farm system.
