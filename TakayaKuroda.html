Takaya Kuroda

Takaya Kuroda is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with AXL-One and formerly with Mausu Promotion and 81 Produce. He is best known for his villianous role of Yaiba in GoGo Sentai Boukenger. He replaced Hirotaka Suzuoki voices as Starscream in Transformers: Cybertron. He did not get the role of Giovanni from Pokemon(One of the other voices of Hirotaka Suzuoki).

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 17, 1965
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: fashion, cleaning, housing and laundry
  • Skills: basketball, baseball, and kickboxing
