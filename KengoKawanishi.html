Kengo Kawanishi

Kengo Kawanishi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. He voiced Rei Kiriyama in March Comes in Like a Lion, Hu Li in Voice of Fox, and Gen Asagi in Dr. Stone.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 18, 1985
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dance, Karaoke, and Games

Character Voiced by Kengo Kawanishi

Gen Asagiri Dr. Stone Gen Asagiri is an enemy turned ally of Senku and the Kingdom of Science. He is very full of himself and his abilities. He is a magician and a mentalist/psychologist with his knowledge of human psyche. He is outwardly stoic, rather quirky, tends to be lazy, self absorbed and perverted. He is perfectly capable of lying without a hint of stress or change in expression. He is capable of genuine kindness.
