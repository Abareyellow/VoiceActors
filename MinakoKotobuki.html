Minako Kotobuki

Minako Kotobuki is a Japanese voice actress, actress and singer. She is affiliated with Music Ray'n. She voiced Natsu Takasaki from Baby Steps, Vivian from The Seven Deadly Sins and most importantly Tsumugi Kotobuki from K-On.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 11, 1991
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
  • Favorite Animal: Ferret and Koala
  • Favorite Beverage: Orange Juice
  • Favorite Food: Spaghetti
  • Favorite Sport: Swimming
  • Favorite Subjects: P.E. and Music
  • Hobbies: Dancing, Listening to Music, Reading Books, and Watching Movies

Character Voiced by Minako Kotobuki

Toole Fate/Apocrypha Toole is a female homunculus that serves under Yggmillennia in Great Holy Grail War. She has a neat and lively personality.
