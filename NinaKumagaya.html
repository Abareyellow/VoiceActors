Nina Kumagaya

Nina Kumagaya is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Tink in Princess Knight, and Marin in Heli-Tako Pu-Chan.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 18, 1963
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Collecting Accessories, Diving, and Walking
  • Skill: Japanese Drum
