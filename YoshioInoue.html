Yoshio Inoue

Yoshio Inoue is a Japanese stage actor and voice actor. He is a graduate of Tokyo University of the Arts. He is currently married to actress Rina Chinen.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 6, 1979
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan

Character Voiced by Yoshio Inoue

Eiji Nochizawa
  • Sword Art Online Movie: Ordinal Scale
  • Sword Art Online: Alicization - War of Underworld: Part II
Eiji Nochizawa/Nautilus was one of the 10,00 players who were trapped in Sword Art Online. He was the childhood friend of Shigemura Yuuna(Yuna). He is former member of the Knights of the Blood Oath before Aincard was cleared.
