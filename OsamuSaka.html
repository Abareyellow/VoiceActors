Osamu Saka

Osamu Saka is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. Osamu's main roles are Shinsuke Tanba from Knights of Sidonia and Noah Van Helsing from Blade.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 11, 1970
  • Birthplace: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobby: Reciting Poems

Character Voiced by Osamu Saka

Masaki Amamiya Cardcaptor Sakura Masaki Amamiya is Sakura Kinomoto's grant-grandfather on her mother side. He is very rich and a kind old man.
