Tatsuhisa Suzuki

Tatsuhisa Suzuki is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with I'm Enterprise and Lantis. He is part of the band, OLDCODEX. His major roles are Yuuta Mishima in Ace of Diamond, Tsutamu Ishii in Hoop Days and Eishiro Sugata in Heavean's Lost Property. He voiced Usada Lettuce from Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 11, 1983
  • Birthplace: Ichikawa, Chiba Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Bicycling, Karaoke, Reading, Games and Shopping
  • Skills: Calligraphy, Drawing, and Pencil/Pen Writing
