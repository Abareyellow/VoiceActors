Keiichi Nanba

Keiichi Nanba is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Joa in Toriko and Zoisite in Sailor Moon.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 26, 1957
  • Hometown: Yamaguchi, Japan
  • Wife: Mayami Sho
