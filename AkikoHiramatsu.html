Akiko Hiramatsu

Akiko Hiramatsu is a Japanese voice actress. She works at Ken Production. She worked in animes such as .hack series, Sailor Moon, Bleach, Full Metal Panic and Nana.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 31, 1967
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Drum, Ice Skating, Tap Dancing

Character Voiced by Akiko Hiramatsu

Yaeko Nishimiya A Silent Voice Yaeko Nishimiya is the mother of both Shouko and Yuzuru Nishimiya. She caught a virus that left her first daughter, Shouko, deaf. She was originally married by then divorced.
