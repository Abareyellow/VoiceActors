Kae Araki

Kae Araki is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced Usagi Tsukino when Kotono Mitsuishi had appendicitis. She originated the role of Chibiusa in the same series. Araki has a relationship with Konami Yoshida after training at a voice acting school.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 6, 1966
  • Birthplace: Osaka, Japan

Characters Voiced by Kae Araki

Sabrina
  • Pokemon
Sabrina is the Gym Leader of Saffron City Gym in the Kanto region. She is a cold person who communicates through a doll. She got pyshic power from her father.
Usagi Tsukino
  • Sailor Moon
  • Sailor Moon R
Usagi Tsukino is a carefree schoolgirl with an enormous capacity for love. Her alter ego is Sailor Moon. She cares deeply about her friends and family. She is a bit selfish, very lazy, clusmy and a crybaby. She is very trusting and believes that everyone has a better nature.
