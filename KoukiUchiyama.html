Kouki Uchiyama

Kouki Uchiyama is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Gekidan Himawari. Kouki is well known for his role of Roxas in the Kingdom Heart video game series. In anime, he voiced Soul Evan in Soul Eater, Yoshino Takigawa in Blast of Tempest, and Yuu Otosaka in Charlotte. Uchiyama voiced Jake Long in the Japanese dub of American Dragon: Jake Long.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 16, 1990
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Skills: Piano, Swimming, and Typing
