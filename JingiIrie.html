Jingi Irie

Jinji Irie is a Japanese actor. He is affiliated with Ken-On. He gradulated from Horikoshi High School. He plays Zamigo Delma in Kaitou Sentai Lupinranger vs Keisatsu Sentai Patranger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 18, 1993
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Painting, Music, Basketball, Coma, Kendama, and Karate

Character Voiced by Jingi Irie

Saotome Colorful Saotome is a boy in Makoto's class. He becomes good friends with Makoto because they share the fact that they have bad grades.
