Kazuko Sugiyama

Kazuko Sugiyama is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She works for Aoni Production. She voiced Akane from Dr. Slump and Jun the Swan from Gatchaman.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 9, 1947
  • Hometown: Nagoya, Aichi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading, Writing and Stargazing

Characters Voiced by Kazuko Sugiyama
