Ayana Taketatsu

Ayana Taketatsu is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She attended a voicing training school before being affiliated with a company. She and Aoi Yuuki formed a singing group named Petit Milady. She voiced in anime such as Berserk, Citrus and K-On.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 23, 1989
  • Birthplace: Saitma Prefecture, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke, Reading, Shopping, Tennis, Bingo, Origami
