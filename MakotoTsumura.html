Makoto Tsumura

Makoto Tsumura is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Acsent but formerly affiliated with Mausu Promotion.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 22, 1965
  • Hometown: Sapporo, Hokkaido, Japan

Character Voiced by Makoto Tsumura

Selim Bradley Fullmetal Alchemist Selim Bradley is the son of Fuhrer King Bradley. He loves his father and asks him to tell stories of his work. He is a big fan of Edward Elric.

Pokemon Voiced by Makoto Tsumura

Whismur Pokemon Advanced Generations Whismur is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. Whismur's murmurs are barely audible, it lets out an ear splitting cry. It sometimes startled itself by it's cry. Makoto Tsumura voiced Alanna's Whismur.
