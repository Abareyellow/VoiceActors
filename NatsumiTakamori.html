Natsumi Takamori

Natsumi Takamori is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Pro-Fit. Some of her major roles are Mei Misaki in Another, Miku Maekawa in The Idolmaster Cinderella Girls, and Misaki Kamiigusa in ThePet Girl of Sakurasou.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 14, 1987
  • Hometown: Yamanashi, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cafe Tour and Illustrations
