Michiko Neya

Michiko Neya is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated Art Visions, but is now a freelance voice actress. Michiko's well know roles are Jun the Swan in Gatchaman 94, Riza Hawkeye in Fullmetal Alchemist and Amy Burklight in Tales of Phantasia.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 4, 1965
  • Hometown: Fukui, Japan
  • Hobbies: Eating, Going to the Gym and Sleeping
