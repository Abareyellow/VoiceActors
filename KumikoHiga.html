Kumiko Higa

Kumiko Higa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Produce 81. She is the voice of Lan Hikari from Mega Man series and Netto Hikari from Rockman.EXE.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 29, 1978
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan

Character Voiced by Kumiko Higa

Trisha Fellows Lord El-Melloi II Case Files: Rail Zeppelin Grace Note Trisha Fellows is Olga Marie Animusphere's governess. She greatly cared for Olga Marie after her father abandonment of his daughter.
