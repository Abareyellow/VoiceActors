Ayumi Nagao

Ayumi Nagao is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She voiced Buona from Arte.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 11
  • Hometwon: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Ayumi Nagao

Oleana Pokemon: Twilight Wings Oleana is the secretary of Chairman Rose. She serves as the vice president of his company, Macro Cosmos. Before working for Chairman Rose, she was a researcher and invented the Dynamax Band. She is extremely dedicated to her work. She is the only one who has made progress on the Dynamax phenomenon.
