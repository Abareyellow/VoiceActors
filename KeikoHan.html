Keiko Han

Keiko Han is a Japanese voice actress, actress and Western Astrologer of Taiwanese descent. Han is a fortune teller of Western horoscopes and has written several books on the subject. She is currently employed with Never Land Arts but was affiliated with Aoni Production & 81 Produce. She voiced Luna and Queen Baryl from Sailor Moon. Han has a daughter named Megumi Han.
