Tomoe Hanba

Tomoe Hanba is a Japanese voice actress. She is associated with Art Vision. She is the official Japanese dubbing voice for Velma Dinkley in the Scooby-Doo frnachise. She voiced in Pokemon, Sailor Moon and Scrapped Princess.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 9, 1972
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Swimming
  • Husband: Jin Yamanoi
