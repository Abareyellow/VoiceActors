Kaneto Shiozawa

Kaneto Shiozawa was a Japanese voice actor. He was affiliated with Aoni Production. Shiozawa wanted to be a performer since his childhood. He gradulated from Hihon University Second Senior High School, where learned to perform in its art department. He died of Cerebel Contusion on May 10, 2000. He voiced Gray Fox in Metal Gear Solid, Aries Mu in Saint Seyia and Ninzaburo Shiratori in Detective Conan.
