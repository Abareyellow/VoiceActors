Satomi Koorogi

Satomi Koorogi is a Japanese actress, voice actress, narrator. She is affiliated with Production Baobab. She is bilingual; the first being Japanese and the second is English. She voiced Misty's Togepi in the Japanese and English verisons of Pokemon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 14, 1962
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
