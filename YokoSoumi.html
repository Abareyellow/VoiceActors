Yoko Soumi

Yoko Soumi is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She married Akio Ootsuka in February 2005, but they divorced in 2009. Some of her roles are Buzam A. Calessa from Vandread and Takako Iwayama from Tokyo Majin.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 31, 1965
  • Hometown: Niigata, Japan
