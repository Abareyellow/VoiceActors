Mai Gotou

Mai Gotou is a Japanese voice actress. She is represented by Ken Production. Agumi Oto is an alias primarily for visual novels, some erotic games and anime. Her most notable role is May Chang from Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 22, 1982
  • Birthplace: Hokkaido, Japan

Character Voiced by Mai Gotou

May Chang
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood - 4-Koma Theater
May Chang is the seventeeth princess of Xing who travel west searching for the Philosopher's Stone. She travels with her pet panda, Xiao May. May is sweet girl with remarkable inner strength. She is independent, intelligent and Amazingly brave. She uses Alchanastry.
