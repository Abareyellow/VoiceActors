Kaori Fukuhara

Kaori Fukuhara is a Japanese voice actress. She and fellow voice actress Emiri Katou were in duo music group called Kato*Fuku. Kato*Furu is disband in 2016.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 11, 1986
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan

Character Voiced by Kaori Fukuhara

Ada Vessalius Pandora Hearts Ada Vessalius is Oz's little sister who adores him very much. She has a gentle nature. She loves her friends and family.
