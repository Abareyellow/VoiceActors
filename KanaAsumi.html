Kana Asumi

Kana Harada or Kana Asumi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She worked for Voice & Heart until 2007 and has worked for 81 Produce since 2008. On January 14, 2014, Asumi announced her marriage on her blog.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 12, 1983
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Skills: Kendo
  • Real Name: Kana Harada

Character Voiced by Kana Asumi

Emmy Pokemon Black and White Emmy is a dragon trainer that Ash & Co. met on their journey. She is currently having trouble with her Druddigon.
