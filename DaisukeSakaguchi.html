Daisuke Sakaguchi

Daisuke Sakaguchi is a Japanese voice affiliated with Aoni Production. He is known from his roles in Gintama as Shinpachi Shimura, Clannad as Youhei Sunohara, Baccano! as Jacuzzi Splot, and Smile Precure as Pop. He was in minor scandal after being spotted with Ryo Hirohashi watching a soccer game.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 11, 1973
  • Hometown: Kashiwazaki, Japan
