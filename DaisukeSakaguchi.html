Daisuke Sakaguchi

Daisuke Sakaguchi is a Japanese voice affiliated with Aoni Production. He is known from his roles in Gintama, Clannad, Baccano!, Smile Precure. He was in minor scandal after being spotted with Ryo Hirohashi watching a soccer game.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 11, 1973
  • Hometown: Kashiwazaki, Japan
