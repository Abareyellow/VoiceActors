Yuu Mizashima

Yuu Mizushima is a Japanese voice actor. He has voiced in Saint Seiya as Subaru Equuleu, Misty Lizard, and Bud Alcor. and Parasyte -the maxim- as Takeshi Hirokawa. He is the voice of Wakko in the japanese dubbing of Animamanics.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 18, 1956
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Real Name: Kenji Noda
