Nobunaga Shimazaki

Nobunaga Shimazaki is a Japanese voice ator. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Yuno from Black Clover, Shido Itsuka from Date A Live, and Hus from World Trigger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: December 6, 1988
  • Birthplace: Miyagi Prefecture, Japan
