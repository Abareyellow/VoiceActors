Tooru Nara

Tooru Nara is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Kenyu Office. He voiced Rikido Sato in My Hero Academia as Rikido Sato.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 7
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading, Traveling, and Singing in Bath

Characters Voiced by Tooru Nara

Flint Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Flint is a member of the elite four of Sinnoh region. He is friend with the gym leader Volkner.
Nico Pokemon XY Nico is Pokemon Trainer who visits the Battle Chateau to battle. He is good friends with Chester and his younger brother. He enjoys batting at the Battle Chateau.
