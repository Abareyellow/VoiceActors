Kenji Hamada

Kenji Hamada is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. He voiced Tigre in 91 Days, James Ron in Basquash!, and Haruaki Azuma in World Trigger.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 12, 1972
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
  • Wife: Junko Takeuchi

Character Voiced by Kenji Hamada

Takumi Nomiya
  • Honey and Clover
  • Honey and Clover Specials
  • Honey and Clover II
Takumi Nomiya is Mayama's former boss at Fujiwara Design. He has fallen in love with Yamada.
