Kanami Satou

Kanami Satou is a Japanese voice actress. She worked in animes such as Fairy Tail, Noragami and Log Horizon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 16
  • Birthplace: Iwate, Japan

Characters Voiced by Kanami Satou

Eclipse Mini
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Eclispe Mini is the Eclipse form of Celestial Spirit Mini. Her true desire is to grant the rogue Celestial King more power by stealing life force from the 12 Zodiac Spirit.
Mini
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Mini is the other half of Celestial Spirit Gemini and one of the 12 Golden Zodiac Keys. They possess the ability to mimic the appearances and abilities of others.
