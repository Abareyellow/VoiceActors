Mika Kikuchi

Mika Kikuchi is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Production Org. Her iconic role is Umeko/DekaPink from Super Sentai series, Tokusou Sentai Dekaranger. She was married to Yuki Kishi but they divorced in 2011. She is currently married to Tomokzau Yoshida(Tetsu/DekaBreak). She is voiced Mokona from Tsubasa Chronicle and xxxHoLiC.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 16, 1963
  • Birthplace: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Dancing and Singing
  • Husband: Tomokazu Yoshida
