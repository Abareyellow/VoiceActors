Takuma Takewaka

Takuma Takewaka is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Theater Echo. He was put in rehab after recovering from Cerebral Hemorrhage.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 22, 1971
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan

Character Voiced by Takuma Takewaka

Kaoru Morita
  • Honey and Clover
  • Honey and Clover II
Kaoru Morita is Shinobu Morita's older brother who seems to have a mysterious agenda in earning ridiculously large amounts of money. He is jealous of his younger brother but loves and respect him.
