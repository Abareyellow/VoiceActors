Rica Matsumoto

Rica Matsumoto is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She was one of the founders of Anison band JAM Project. The most popular role by Rica is Ash Ketchum from Pokemon. He has voice characters from Yu-Gi-Oh series. She has voice several roles in Super Sentai.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 30, 1968
  • Birthplace: Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan
  • Favorite Colors: Black, White and Red
