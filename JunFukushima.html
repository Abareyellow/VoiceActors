Jun Fukushima

Jun Fukushima, not Jun Fukuyama, is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. Fukushima is actually a very serious person at the recording studio that barely speaks according to Kana Hanazawa and Hiroki Yasumoto. He mainly freely during the broadcast. He voiced Kazuma Sato in KonoSuba and Yoshihisa Manabe in Kotura-san.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 4, 1976
  • Hometown: Ehime, Japan
  • Hobbies: Billiards
  • Skill: Guitars

Character Voiced by Jun Fukushima

Issei Miyata I Want to Eat Your Pancreas Issei Miyata is a student that goes to Haruki and Sakura's school. He tries to be nice to Haruki.
