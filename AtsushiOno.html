Atsushi Ono

Atsushi Ono is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Office Osawa. Before he was a voice actor, he narrated documentaries. He started voice acting in 2004. He voiced Giriko Kutsuzawa in Bleach, Joe Buttataki in Soul Eater, and Master in Hamatoma.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 9, 1961
  • Hometown: Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan

Character Voiced by Atsushi Ono

Kaijin That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Kaijin is a blacksmith of Dwarven Kingdom and the brother of Kaidou. He is one of Rimuru Tempest's subordinate. He is a builder, blacksmith, and craftman. He might look like a big softy, but actually an extremely prudent old man.
