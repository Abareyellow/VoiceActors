Hidekatsu Shibata

Hidekatsu Shibata is a Japanese voice actor, actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He was classmate of former prime minister Yasuo Fukuda. Shibata is currently married to seiyu actress Akiko Sekine. He voiced King Bradley from Fullmetal Alchemist.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 25, 1937
