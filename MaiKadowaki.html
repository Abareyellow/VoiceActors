Mai Kadowaki

Mai Kadowaki is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She gain popularity as a radio actress who wears glasses. She is known for protraying megankko characters on a regular basis. On February 26, 2013, she announced that she got married. In the same year, she gave birth to her first child.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 8, 1980
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
