Youko Matsuoka

Youko Matsuoka is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Huck in Huckleberry Finn Story and Karl Lichter von Randoll in Future GPX Cyber Formula.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 20, 1954
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Youko Matsuoka

Bertha Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Bertha is a Ground-type Trainer and member of the Sinnoh Elite Four. Bertha has a strong advocay for observant attitudes in everyday life. Her observation skills are an useful tool in battle and everyday life. She wants to keep her identity a secret because she does not like the expectations with her title.
