Kaori Mizuhashi

Kaori Mizuhashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is employed by Arts Vision. She debuted in 1966 in 1966 with the Game Legend of the Valkyrie. As a child she liked reading aloud books, especially lines of dialogue. This motivated her to become an voice actress. Her debut role was Nyako Mikenika in Eden's Bowy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 28, 1974
  • Hometown: Sapporo, Hokkaido, Japan

Character Voiced by Kaori Mizuhashi

Zephiris Scrapped Princess Zephiris is a powerful spiritual being who is a dragoon. She is an AI weapon created during the Genesis Wars. She is calm, solemn and quiet. She gives Cassulls vital information about assissins.
