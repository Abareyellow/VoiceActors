Tetsu Inada

Tetsu Inada is a Japanese voice actor. His affiliated with Tetsu Inada. He voiced Akira Maeda in Cromartie High School and Caster of Red in Fate/Apocrypha.

Character Voiced by Tetsu Inada

Caster of Red Fate/Apocrypha Caster of Red is the Caster-class Servant of Jean Rum of the Red Faction in the Great Holy Grail War. His true identity is William Shakespeare. Shakespeare is a legendary platwright of England, gentlemanly and soft-spoken. He will willingly employ deception, provaction or anything else for the sake of spinning the story.
