Mami Koyama

Mami Koyama is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She married Toru Furuya in 1976 but divorced in 1983. Some of her roles are Kaguya Otsutsuki in Naruto Shippuden, Balalaika in Black Lagoon and Talia Gladys in Gundam Seed.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 17, 1955
  • Hometown: Nishio, Aichi, Japan
  • Skills: Japanese Dance and Scuba Driving

Characters Voiced by Mami Koyama

Luana Pokemon Luana is the Gym Leader of Kumquat Island's Gym. She specializes in Double Battles. She cares deeply for Pokemon like their her own child.
Pinako Rockbell Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood Pinako Rockbell is Winry's grandmother. She is a resident of Risembool. She is a parental figure for Edward and Alphonse Elric, after lossing their mother. Mami Koyama voiced the younger Pinako Rockbell.
Towa Pokemon Movie 4: Pokemon 4Ever Towa is a villager from Arborville. she has a granddaughter named Diana
