Kouji Totani

Kouji Totani was a Japanese voice actor. He worked for Aoni Production. He voiced in Dragon Ball and Fullmetal Alchemist. On February 6, 2006, Totani died from acute heart failure. His last appearance was the Naruto 2006 New Year Special, where he voiced Oki.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 12, 1948
  • Death: February 6, 2006

Character Voiced by Kouji Totani

Tim Marcoh Fullmetal Alchemist Tim Marcoh was the leading researcher in creation od the Philosopher's Stone. He was called the Crystal Alchemist. He suffers from post traumatic stress disorder due to guilt. He is honest hardworking man who does not bluff.
