Takuya Kimura

Takuya Kimura is a Japanese actor, singer, and radio personality. He is a member of SMAP, an idol group. He is married to Shizuka Kudo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 13, 1972
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
