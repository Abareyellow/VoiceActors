Shinichiro Miki

Shinichiro Miki is a Japanese voice actor and singer. Miki is affiliated with 81 Produce. He landed the role of James in Japanese version of Pokemon. He mainly voices in video games, arcade games and anime. He is in a band called WeiB Kreuz with Takehito Koyasu, Tomokazu Seki and Hiro Yuuki.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 18, 1968
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Auto Racing
