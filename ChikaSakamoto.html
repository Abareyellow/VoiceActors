Chika Sakamoto

Chika Sakamoto is a voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. She voiced the puppet of Dino-kun in Zyuranger first video called Kyoryu Sentai Zyuranger Dino Video. As a seiyu, she well known for her role of Agumon in Digimon Adventure.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 17, 1959
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Pokemon Voiced by Chika Sakamoto

Pikachu Pokemon Pikachu is the main Pokemon in all of Pokemon. Pikachu is from the Kanto region and the evolved form of Pichu. Pikachu can recharge each other with their build up energy. Chika Sakamoto voiced Victor's Pikachu.
