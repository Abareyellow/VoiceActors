Youko Honna

Youko Honna is a Japanese voice actress. She works at Re-Max, although she was formerly worked at 81 Produce. She is best known as the voice of Nagisa Misumi from the original Pretty Cure series. She is married in 2014 and gave birth to a daughter in June 2015. In 2017, she announced her pregnancy with a second child.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 7, 1979
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
