Kouki Miyata

Kouki Miyata is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. He voiced Burstosaur Triceratops in Bakuryuu Sentai Abaranger. He also voiced Phineas in the Japanese dub of Phineas and Ferb. Some of his anime roles are Rhythm in Shugo Chara series and Amnesia in Ukyo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 9, 1972
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke and Strolling

Character Voiced by Kouki Miyata

Yukito
  • Tsubasa Chronicle
  • Tsubasa Chronicle 2nd Series
Yukito is the high priest of the Clow Kingdom and close friends with Touya. He possesses the ability to send others across dimesion and to see the future.
