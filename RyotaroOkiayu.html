Ryotaro Okiayu

Ryotaro Okiayu is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He married Ai Maeda in 2013. His major roles are Dark and D.N.Angel, Toriko in Toriko and Hisashi Mitsui in Slam Dunk.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 17, 1969
  • Hometown: Kitakyushu, Fukuoka, Japan
  • Favorite Colors: Bright Colors
  • Favorite Foods: Bananas and Curry
  • Skills: Eating fast and Kansai
  • Wife: Ai Maeda
