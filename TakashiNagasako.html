Takashi Nagasako

Takashi Nagasako is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Arts Vision. He has voiced in several games such as The Legend of Zelda as Ganondorf and Donkey Kong & Super Mario Series. In Super Sentai, He voiced Bakuryu Tyrannosaurs in Bakuryu Sentai Abaranger. He voiced Lewi in Blood+ and Enma Daio in Hozuki's Coolheadedness.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 24, 1964
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Characters Voiced by Takashi Nagasako

Mr. Moore Pokemon Advanced Generations Mr. Moore is the grandfather of Flannery. He is the former Lavaridge Gym Leader and used to be part the Elite Four. He is cheery individual with a fiery temperament in battle. He is concerned about his granddaughter taking control of the Gym.
Mr. Shellby Pokemon Mr. Shellby is a Pokemon Nesters: people who take care of Pokemon Eggs. He lives in Eggseter town with his wife. He and his wife owns Shellby Ranch. He helps out in the town's annual Extreme Pokemon competition.
