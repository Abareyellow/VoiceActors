Ayahi Takagaki

Ayahi Takagaki is a japanese voice actress and singer. She has done many diffferent types of Characters in anime. In 2007, she landed a major role on Deltora Quest. She gradulated from a Tokyo music college majoring in operatic singing. She is currently a member of a singing group named Sphere.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 25, 1985
