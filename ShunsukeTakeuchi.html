Shunsuke Takeuchi

Shunsuke Takeuchi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. His most notable roles are Hackmon in Digimon series and Yamato Godai in Nanbaka.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 12, 1997
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: DTM, Fashion, and Vocal Music

Character Voiced by Shunsuke Takeuchi

Archer of Black Fate/Apocrypha Archer of Black is the Archer-class servant of Fiore Forvedge Yggdmillennia of the Black Fraction in the Great Holy Grail War. He is noble and treats everyone with courtesy. He taught many heros and respected by his peers for his wisdom. He is really Chiron, the son of the Titan Cronus.
