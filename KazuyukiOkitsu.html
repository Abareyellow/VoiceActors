Kazuyuki Okitsu

Kazuyuki Okitsu is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. He is affiliated with Kekke Corporation. He graduated from the Osaka University of Arts. He voiced in Serpico from Berserk, Dietrich Knitz from Knight's & Magic and Saku Hagita from Orange.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 8, 1980
  • Birthplace: Awaji Island, Japan
