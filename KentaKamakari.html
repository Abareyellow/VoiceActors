Kenta Kamakari

Kenta Kamakari is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He is famous for his role as Ryo Shishido in the Prince of Tennis Musicals. Kamakari was hospitalized on April 21, 2007 and unable to play the ride of Minami Itsuki in the Air Gear Musical. He recovered in February 2008 and continue working again. He is the leader and lead vocals for the rock band Cocoa Otoko that debuted on 2010, yet disbanded again.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 17, 1984
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Billards, Darts, Karaoke, Soccer
  • Skill: Billards
