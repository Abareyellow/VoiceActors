Mikako Takahashi

Mikako Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress and J-pop singer. She is affiliated with I'm Enterprise. She is part of The Excel Girls with Yumiko Kobayashi.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 29, 1980
  • Birthplace: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Chatting, Darts, Guitar, Shopping, Skiing, Snowboarding, TV Games and Watching Movies
