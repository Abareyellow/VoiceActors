Aya Uchida

Aya Uchida is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Across Entertainment. She is known for her roles of Kotori Minami in Love Live! and Kaban in Kemono Friends.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: July 23, 1986
  • Hometown: Ota, Gunma, Japan
  • Favorite Seiyu: Ikue Ohtani
  • Hobbies: Making Sweets and Shopping
  • Skill: Making Things

Characters Voiced by Aya Uchida

Arin Kannazuki
  • Trinity Seven
  • Trinity Seven OVA
  • Trinity Seven Movie 1: Eternity Library & Alchemic Girl
  • Trinity Seven Movie 2: Heavens Library to Crimson Lord
Arin Kannazuki is one of the Trinity Seven of the Ira(Wrath) archive and an user of Chaotic Rune magc. She has a similar appearance to Hijiri, Arata's childhood friend. She is a student at Royal Biblia Academy. Arin rarely shows no emotion and cares about others a little. She has a stoic/blase personality.
