Kenjiro Tsuda

Kenjiro Tsuda is a Japanese actor, voice acotr and narrator. He works with Amuleto. He is best known for his roles of Seto Kaiba in Yu-Gi-Oh!, Hannes in Attack on Titans, and Mikoto Suoh in K.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 11, 1971
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
