Yui Ishikawa

Yui Ishikawa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Sunaoka Office. Before she was a voice actress, Ishikawa was a stage sctress. Her major roles are Mikasa Ackerman from Attack on Titans, Sayuri Haruno from Bonjour Sweet Love Patisserie, and Hinaki Shinjo from Aikatsu.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 30, 1989
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
