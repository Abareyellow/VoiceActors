Noriaki Sugiyama

Noriaki Sugiyama is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Stay-Luck. His notable roles are Sasuke Uchida in Naruto, Shiro Emiya in Fate/Stay Night and England in Axis Powers Hetalia.
