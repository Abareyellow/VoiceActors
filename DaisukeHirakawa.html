Daisuke Hirakawa

Daisuke Hirakawa is a Japanese voice actor, not to be mistaken for Daisuke Namikawa. He is the official Japanese voice actor for Orlando Bloom characters such as Will Turner from Pirates of Carribbean and Legolas from The Lord of the Rings. His major voice actor are Jerome form Berserk, and Koji Tanaka from Ajin.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 4, 1973
  • Hometown: Niigata, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Skiing, and Snowboarding

Character Voiced by Daisuke Hirakawa

Melvin Weins Lord El-Melloi II Case Files: Rail Zeppelin Grace Note Melvin Weins is a mage affiliated with the Creation department at the Clock and self-proclaim friend of Waver/Lord El-Melloi II. Melvin is constantly seeking excitment from the sidelines. He is interest in Waver's stories and events. Born with a feeble body, he often coughs up blood.
