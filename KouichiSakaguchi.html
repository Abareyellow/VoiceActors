Kouichi Sakaguchi

Kouichi Sakaguchi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. He voice in The Japanese and English language of Pokemon.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 10, 1968
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan

Pokemon Voiced by Kouichi Sakaguchi

  • Pokemon
Gengar is a Pokemon from the Kanto region. It the final form of Gastly and has the ability to mega evolve. Gengar enjoys playing prank practical jokes and casting curses on humans. Kouichi Sakaguchi voiced Drake's Gengar.
Pidgeot
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
Pidgeot is a Pokemon from the Kanto region. It the final form of Pidgey and can mega evolve. This Pokemon is capable of flying faster than the speed of sound. Kouichi Sakaguchi voiced Solidad's Pidgeot.
Swellow
  • Pokemon Advanced Generations
Swellow is a Pokemon from the Hoenn region. It is the evolved form of Taillow. An impressive dive-bomber, it will never miss its target. Kouichi Sakaguchi voiced Winona's Swellow.
