Kishio Taniyama

Kishio Taniyama is a Japanese voice actor, singer, and lyricist. He is affiliated with Ken Production. He provides the vocals and lyrics for the band Granrodeo under his stage name Kishow. He voiced Fred Jones in the Japanese dub of the Scooby-Doo franchise. He voiced Chuya Nakahara in Bungo Stray Dogs and Ryohei Tachibana in Sky Girls.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 11, 1975
  • Hometown: Yamaguchi, Japan

Character Voiced by Kishio Taniyama

Kinzo Shima
  • Blue Exorcist Movie
  • Blue Exorcist: Kyoto Saga
  • Blue Exorcist OVA
Kizo Shima is the fourth son of Shima Family, Renzo's older brother and a Middle Second-Class Exorcist. He is a simple guy who is ruled by his emotions. He is described as a idiot by Juzo, the eldest brother, but cares about his brothers.
