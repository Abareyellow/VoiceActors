Kokoro Kikuchi

Kokoro Kikuchi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Kenyuu Office. She is well known for her roles of Akito/Agito Wanjima in Air Gear and Sarada Uchiha in Boruto series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 9, 1982
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Gardening, Piano and Reading Manga

Character Voiced by Kokoro Kikuchi

Takeshi Fresh Pretty Cure! Takeshi is a young boy who is friends with Inori. He has a dog named Lucky.
