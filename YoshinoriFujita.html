Yoshinori Fujita

Yoshinori Fujita or Keisuke Oda is a Japanese voice actor. He voiced in animes such as Kaoru Hitachiin from Ouran High School Host Club and Alessandro XVIII from Trinity Blood.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 27, 1976
  • Hometown: Hirosaki, Aomori, Japan
  • Hobbies: Painting and Singing

Character Voiced by Yoshinori Fujita
