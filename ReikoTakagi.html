Reiko Takagi

Reiko Takagi is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Sigma Seven. She voiced Kaolla Su in Love Hina, Luzandra Frail in Divergence Eve, and Nobunaga Asakura in Haruka Nogizaka's Secret. In video games, she voices Cassandra Alexandra in the Soulcalibur series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 26, 1973
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan

Ryuji Suguro
  • Blue Exorcist
  • Blue Exorcist: Kyoto Saga
Ryuji "Bon" Suguro is an Exwire at the True Cross Academy. He is Rin Okumura's rival and the prodigy of the class. Despite his appearance, he is the exact opposite of his appearance. He is stern, hardworking, and taking things overly serious. He dislikes people who do not work hard and slack off. He is a very strong-willed and etermined individual. He dislikes being looking down upon by people.He can be cool and collected, but mainly baed-tempered, argumentative, obnoxious and violent. He cares deeply about working together as a team and teamwork. Reiko Takagi voiced younger Ryuji Takagi.
