Daisuke Kishio

Daisuke Kishio is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. Kishio joined Aoni Production on April 1, 2014. He has voicing in Super Sentai. He has been nominated for award in the Seiyu Awards.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 28, 1974
  • Hometown: Mie, Japan
  • Hobbies: Audio Equipment, Bowling, Feng Shui, Skiing

Characters Voiced by Daisuke Kishio

Eclipse Leo
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Eclipse Leo is the Eclipse form of Celestial Spirit Leo. His true desire is to grant the rogue Celestial King more power by stealing life force from the 12 Zodiac Spirit.
Loki
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail OVA
