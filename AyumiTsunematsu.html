Ayumi Tsunematsu

Ayumi Tsunematsu is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced Echidna in Uchuu Sentai Kyuranger. She worked in animes such as Re:Creaters as Aki Kikuchihara and Digimon as Beautymon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 26, 1981
  • Hometown: Hyogo, Japan
