Mao Ichimichi

Mao Ichimichi is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. Ichimichi made her debut under the stage name Rio Natsuki. She was part of the singing group HOP Club but then parted ways. She restart hewr career in Tokyo and signed with Yellow Cab Next. She was two Super Sentai Heros Luka Millfy/Gokai Yellow from Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger and Raptor 283/Washi Pink from Uchu Sentai Kyuranger.

  • Birthday: February 1, 1992
  • Birthplace: Osaka Prefecture, Japan
