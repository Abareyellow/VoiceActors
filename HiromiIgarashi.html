Hiromi Igarashi

Hiromi Igarashi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. She voiced anime characters in The Idolmaster Cinderella Girls and High School Fleet.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 13, 1986
  • Birthplace: Hokkiaido, Japan
  • Hobbies: Skiing, Swimming, Bowling

Characters Voiced by Hiromi Igarashi

Kanae Aira
  • Juuni Taisen
Kanae Aira or Tora is the warrior of the Tiger. She is an alcoholic.

Pokemon Voiced by Hiromi Igarashi

Espeon
  • Pokemon Black and White: Rival Destinies
  • Pokemon: Mewtwo-Prologue to Awakening
Espeon is a Pokemon from Johto region. Espeon is one of the Eevee evolve forms. The gem on Espeon's head boosts it's psychic powers to protects it's trainers. Hiromi Igarashi voices Vigil's Espeon.
