Kotono Mitsuishi

Kotono Mitsuishi is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is a freelancer. Before she was voice actress, she worked as a elevator girl and office lady. Mitsuishi graduated from high school in 1986. She entered Katsuta Seiyu Academy after high school. She is married and has a daughter. Her debut role is Tomoyo in Aim for the Ace!. Some of other roles are Usagi Tsukino from Sailor Moon and Misato Katsuragi from Neon Genesis Evangelion.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 8, 1967
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Shopping and Tennis
