Masafumi Kimura

Masafumi Kimura is a voice actor. He works at Production Baobab. He work in animes such as Fairy Tail and Soul Eater.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 3, 1977

Characters Voiced by Masafumi Kimura

Orga Nanagear
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
  • Fairy Tail: Final Season
Orga Nanagear is a Lighting God Slayer and one of the five strongest Mages of Sabertooth. Orga is one of the quieter members, rarely ever talking. He does have odd side of himself in regards to publicity.
Sid Barett
  • Soul Eater
  • Soul Eater NOT!
Sid Barrett is an undead instrctor of DWMA. He is a stubborn, well-respected, and friendly individuals. He is Black Star adoptive father.
Tadashi Yamabuki
  • Fresh Pretty Cure!
Tadashi Yamabuki is Inori's father. He owns and runs a personal clinic in Yotsuba Town. As a veterinarian, he has a bold, generous appearance and personality paying patient to animals.
