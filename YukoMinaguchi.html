Yuko Minaguchi

Yuko Minaguchi is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. In 2012, she study abroad to the United States. Tis resulted in Shino Kakinuma being cast as Videl for the final arc Dragon Ball Kai but she did reprise later. She voiced Sailor Saturn as well.
