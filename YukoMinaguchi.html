Yuko Minaguchi

Yuko Minaguchi is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. In 2012, she study abroad to the United States. This is because of the resulted in Shino Kakinuma being cast as Videl for the final arc Dragon Ball Kai but she did reprise later. Her main voice actoring role is Sailor Saturn from Sailor Moon S. Her other roles are Appleland Story as Frida Lenbach, Dragon Ball series as Pan, and Hayate the Combat Butler as Yukariko Sanzenin.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 26, 1966
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Tokyo, Japan
  • Birth Name: Tomoko Minaguchi
