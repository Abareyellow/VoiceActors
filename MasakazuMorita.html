Masakazu Morita

Masakazu Morita is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He works for Aoni Production. Morita is a host of a radio show called Bleach B-Station. His most notable roles are Chili from Pokemon, Ichigo Kurosaki from Bleach and Marco from One Piece. He protrays Tenjiro Banno in Karem Rider Drive. He voices Troy Bolton in Japanese dub of High School Musical and it's sequel.
