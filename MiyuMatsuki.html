Miyu Matsuki

Miyu Matsuki or Meiko Matsuki was a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with 81 Produce. Matsuki was a graduate of Hiroshima Jogakuin Junior & Senior High School and Keio University. She majored in Environment and Information Studies. Her parents were aganist her becoming a voice actress. She started pursuing her voice acting career. She attended the Aksent voice acting school. Her debut role was Hitomi Onodera from Shichinin no Nana. She was once with Aksent but switch over 81 Produce. Miyu had been under treatment for acute pneumonia since July 2015. She passed away on October 27th 2015 due to her illness.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 14, 1977
  • Died: October 27, 2015
  • Hometown: Kure, Hiroshima, Japan
