Hiro Shimono

Hiro Shimono is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with I'm Eneterprise. He voices characters from Dawn of Yona, Soul Eater, and Attack on Titans.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 21, 1980
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Karaoke, Sunbathing
  • Nickname: Shimocchi

Character Voiced by Hiro Shimono

Shou Fairy Tail Shou is a childhood friend of Erza. He was the youngest of the group. He is the most sensitive of the group.
