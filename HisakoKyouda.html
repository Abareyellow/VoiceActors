Hisako Kyouda

Hisako Kyouda is a Japanese actress and voice actress. Kyouda is affiliated with Haikyo. She has voice in Yu Yu Hakusho, Sore Ike! Anpanman and Madison Ikkoku. She voiced Slappy Squirrel in Japanese dub in Animaniacs and Fairy Godmother in Japanese dub of Cinderella.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 22, 1935

Character Voiced by Hisako Kyouda

Agatha Pokemon Generations Agatha is a trainer in Kanto. She is part of the Elite Four of Kanto.
