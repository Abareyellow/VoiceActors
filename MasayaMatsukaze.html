Masaya Matsukaze

Masaya Matsukaze is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and singer. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. His bebut to the entertainment industry is MegaBlue/Shun Namiki in Denji Sentai Megaranger. His major roles in the anime series are Miroku Fujima in Beyond the Boundary and Ren Ichimaku in Hell Girl.

  • Nick Name: Banchou
