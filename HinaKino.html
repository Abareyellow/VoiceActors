Hina Kino

Hina Kino is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Amuleto. She had been interested in anime and manga from an early age such as One Piece. She got more inspired in junior high by a gaming magazine Famitsu and an One Piece fil. Her first anime role is a waitress in A Good Libarian Like a Good Shephard. Her first main role is Sylvia Silkcut in Hybrid x Heart Magias Academy Ataraxia.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 12, 1997
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Watching Movies and Playing with Cats
