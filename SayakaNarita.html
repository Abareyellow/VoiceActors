Sayaka Narita

Sayaka Narita is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated 81 Produce. She has voiced in Christel Ketsuno from Gintama, Aries from Fairy Tail and Kusu Kusu from Shugo Chara!

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 26, 1980
  • Hometown: Chiba, Japan
  • Hobbies: Skate, Painting, Illustration

Characters Voiced by Sayaka Narita

Aries
  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail OVA
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Aries is the celestial spirit of the sheep. She is very timid female, very softspoken and shy. Along with that, she is very sweet and polite. She is quite easily frightened and very obedient.
Eclipse Aries
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
Eclipse Aries is the Eclipse form of Celestial Spirit Aries. Her true desire is to grant the rogue Celestial King more power by stealing life force from the 12 Zodiac Spirit.
Lyon Vastia
  • Fairy Tail
Lyon Vastia is a member of Lamia Scale. He is one of Gray Fullbuster's rival. He was a former student Ur as well. He ambitious and very pride of himself but cold and composed. Sayaka Narita voiced the younger Lyon Vastia.
