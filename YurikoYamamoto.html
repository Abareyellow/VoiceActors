Yuriko Yamamoto

Yuriko Yamamoto is a Japanese actress, voice actress, singer and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. Her debut role was Sandybell in Hello! Sandybell. Her major roles are Marin Eagle in Saint Seiya, Rumy in Gail Force and Souma in RG Vega.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 13, 1959
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Classic Ballet and Golf

Character Voiced by Yuriko Yamamoto

Maya Pokemon Maya is a Sea Priestess of the Whirl Islands. Maya is responsible for keeping the culture and tradition of the Whirl Cup alive. She oversees the Whirl Cup competition with the magical power of the Great Sea Spirit Sapphire. Maya can communicate with Water-type Pokemon when she evokes the Sea Spirit Union. Maya is very confident.
