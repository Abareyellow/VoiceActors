Ayako Kawasumi

Ayako Kawasumi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is a skilled pianist because she's played the piano since childhood. She composed and performed for the opening theme to piano. She has voiced in animes such as Queen Blade's and Nodame Catabile.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 30, 1976
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Astrological Sign: Aries
  • Favorite Baseball: Yakult Swallows
  • Hobbies: Cooking, Scuba driving, Yoga
