Tsubasa Yonaga

Tsubasa Yonaga is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Ken Production. His wife is Yuka Nishigaki. He voiced Ren Mihashi in Big Windup! and Prue in Valvrave the Liberator.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 15, 1984
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Wife: Yuka Nishigaki

  • Fairy Tail
  • Fairy Tail (2014)
