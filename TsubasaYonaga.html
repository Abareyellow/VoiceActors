Tsubasa Yonaga

Tsubasa Yonaga is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Ken Production. His wife is Yuka Nishigaki. He voiced Ren Mihashi in Big Windup! and Prue in Valvrave the Liberator.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 15, 1984
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Wife: Yuka Nishigaki

Character Voiced by Tsubasa Yonaga

Solaad The Seven Deadly Sins Movie: Prisoners of the Sky Solaad is a member of the Celestial Clan. He was trained to inherit the Winged Sword. He studies about legend of Great Oshiro and hates idea of fighting.
