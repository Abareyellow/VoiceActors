Madoka Yonezawa

Madoka Yonezawa is a Japanese voice actress and singer. On her 35th birthday, Yonezawa announced on her marriage and her first baby. She voiced Izami Akazawa in Another, Ui Hirasawa from K-On!, and Serena Jounouchi from the Pretty Rhythm series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 30, 1981
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan

Character Voiced by Madoka Yonezawa

Olga Marie Animusphere
  • Fate/Grand Order: First Order
  • Fate/Grand Order: Moonlight/Lostroom
Olga Marie Animusphere is the leader of the Chaldea and is monitoring the future and trying to prevent the extinction of humanity in 2016. She is dutiful in her role and takes full responsibility in Chaldea.
