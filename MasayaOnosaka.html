Masaya Onosaka

Masaya Onosaka is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He voiced Pierre Vieira in Aquarion and Isaac Dian in Baccano!

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 13, 1964
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
