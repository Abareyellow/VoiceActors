Yumi Kakazu

Yumi Kakazu is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Atomic Monkey. Kakazu most well known roles are Shizuka from Doraemon, Siliva de Alisia from Aquarion, and Haruka in Princess Sister.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 18, 1973
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Interest: Geography
