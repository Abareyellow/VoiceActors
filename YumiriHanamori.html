Yumiri Hanamori

Yumiri Hanamori is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with m&i. When Hanamori was in the second year of high school, her friend encouraged her to try out voiceover as a career choice. Her anime fil debut was Garakowo: Restore the World as Remo. She voiced Zero in Grimoire of Zero and Yuki Soleil in The Price of Smiles.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 29, 1997
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Skill: Tennis

Character Voiced by Yumiri Hanamori

Shizue Izawa That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Shizue Izawa is the companion of the previous Hero and a Japanese Summoned One. She was a teacher for Freedom Academy. She is kind and friendly. She is famous for her heroic deeds and achievements.
