Naomi Wakabayashi

Naomi Wakabayashi or Miyako Suzuta is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She got married on her 33rd birthday and gave birth to her child on February 15th, 2015. She is well known for her as Kudryavaka Noumi from Little Busters! series and Ritsuko Akizuti from The iDoLM@STER series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 2, 1975
  • Birthplace: Tottori, Japan
