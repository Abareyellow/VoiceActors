Yuu Shimamura

Yuu Shimamura is a Japanese voice actress. She was affiliated with Office Kaoru but now with Ken Production. She voiced in several animes such as Attack on Titan, Go! Princess Precure and Aldnoah.Zero. She is the voice of The Princess Zelda in Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of Wind.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 18, 1985
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Yuu Shimamura

Siune

  • Sword Art Online Movie: Ordinal Scale
Siune is a support-class member and the sub-leader of the Sleeping Knights guild.
