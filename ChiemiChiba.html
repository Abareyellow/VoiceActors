Chiemi Chiba

Chiemi Chiba is a voice actress, J-Pop singer and former gravure model. She is a member of the J-Pop group Aurora Gonin Musume. She voiced Yuzuriha Nekoi from Tsubasa Chronicle, and Hinako from Sister Princess.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 25, 1975
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan

Character Voiced by Chiemi Chiba

Yuzuriha Nekoi
  • Tsubasa Chronicle
  • Tsubasa Chronicle 2nd Series
Yuzuriha Nekoi is a demon hunter in the land of Oto. She is one of the seven fighters of Tower faction in Tokyo. She is exceeding energetic.
