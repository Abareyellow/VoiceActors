Hirotaka Suzuoki

Hirotaka Suzuoki was a Japanese actor and voice actor. He graduated from Tokyo Keizai University. His voice was one of his notable trait. His best friend was fellow Japanese voice actor, Toru Furuya. Furuya voiced with Suzuoki in different series. Suzuoki was a heavy smoker and drinker. In July 2006, he was diagnosed with lung cancer and undertook hospitalization and medical treatment. His health declined and died on August 6, 2006. His ongoing roles were replaced with other voice actors.
