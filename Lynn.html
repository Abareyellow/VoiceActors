Lynn

Lynn is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. Lynn was born to American and Japanese parents. During her early years, she began watching anime. Her favorite were Dectective Conan and InuYasha because Kappei Yamaguchi. After learning about characters, she began considering the career. She has keep her studies going after high school. Her debut role was Maya Kyoudou from Sabagebu!

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 1, 1992
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan

Characters Voiced by Lynn

Sakura Yamauchi I Want to Eat Your Pancreas Sakura Yamauchi is a teenager with a pancreas disease. She has a outgoing and excited personality due to her shortened life. She has a diary about her life with the disease.
Shishou Kino's Journey: The Beautiful World - The Animated Series Shishou is Kino's master who taught her marksmanship. She lives in the forest and does not travel.
