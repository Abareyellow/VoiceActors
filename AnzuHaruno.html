Anzu Haruno

Anzu Haruno is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. She debuted as a voice actress in 2015 and recieved a major role in 2017.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 30, 1994
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Watching Soccer, Singing Karaoke, Cooking, and Listening to Music

Character Voiced by Anzu Haruno

Ramiris That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Ramiris is one of the Eight-Star Demon Lords and Queen of Spirits. She is the Saints' Guide, bestowing divine protection on heros and preserves the balance of entire world. She is a ridiculously free-willed individual. Ramiris is a pleasant person.
