Megumi Hayashibara

Megumi Hayasibara was born on March 30, 1967 in Tokyo, Japan. She studied at a Catholic school. She participated in Badminton, Biology, Boardcasting, Drama and English club. She is a qualifying nurse but never has been qualified for a nursing position. She is married to Masaharu Amiya on March 30, 1998 and has a daughter who was born on Januray 10, 2004.
