Asuna Tomari

Asuna Tomari is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with VIMS.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 20, 1989
  • Hometown: Ibaraki, Japan

Characters Voiced by Asuna Tomari

Chrome Dr. Stone Chrome is the sorcerer/scientist of Ishigami Village and Senku's friend. He is gifted in chemical knowledge and crafting. He is a high energy person with passion and curosity. He has an interested and a deep love of science. He is loyal to his friends and cares little about age when it comes to friendship. While he lacks physical abilities, he is still able to think of clever solutions out of dangerous. He is inquistive and loves helping make inventions. Asuna Tomari voiced younger Chrome.
Gobuta That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Gobuta is a hobgoblin and is Rimuru Tempest's follower. He is considered a fool but proves to be a very competent individual. He is a natural airhead and an idiot but a hidden genius.
