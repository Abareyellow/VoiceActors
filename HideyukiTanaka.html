Hideyuki Tanaka

Hideyuki Tanaka is a Japanese voice actor, actor and narrator. He is attached to Aoni Production. He graduate from Toho Gakuen School of Music. His main roles are from Captain Tsubasa and One Piece.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 12, 1950

He teaches biology and studies the native language of the Tweleve Kingdom. Despite learning the language, he does not understand language and used writing to communicate.

Yaozo Shima
  • Blue Exorcist: Kyoto Saga
