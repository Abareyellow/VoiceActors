Masaaki Tsukada

Masaaki Tsukada was a Japanese voice actor. He worked for Mausu Promotion. He was marry to voice actress Masako Nozawa until his death. He died on January 24, 2014. He voiced in Bleach and Fullmetal Alchemist.
