Nobuyuki Hiyama

Nobuyuki Hiyama is a voice actor, narrator and readio personality. He is affiliated Art Vision. Hiyama grew up enjoying casual amateur baseball with friends. He was flourished in history and social studies class since middle school. He joined his middle school radio club but did not enjoyed it. In high school, he got an interest in radio. Hiyama got the Japan's Newspaper Scholarship grant to pay for college. He did a lot of minor between his first debut and training. He voiced in multiple Super Sentai seasons.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 25, 1967
  • Hometown: Hiroshima, Japan
  • Hobbies: Driving and Historical Research
  • Skills: Ability to speak the Hiroshima Accent
