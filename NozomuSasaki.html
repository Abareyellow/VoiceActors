Nozomu Sasaki

Nozomu Sasaki is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. Nozomu voiced Yusuke Urameshi in YuYu Hakusho, and Eriol Hirragizawa in Cardcaptor Sakura.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 25, 1967
  • Hometown: Hiroshima, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading Books and Collecting Fountation Pens
