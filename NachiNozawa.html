Nachi Nozawa

Nachi Nozawa was a Japanese voice actor, actor and theatre director. He was affiliated with Office PAC. On October 30, 2010, Nozawa died at Tokyo hospital of lung cancer. He won a Special Merit Award in the 5th Seiyu Awards. His roles were Cobra in Space Cobra and Hyakkimaru in Dororo.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 13, 1938
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Death: October 30, 2010

Character Voiced by Nachi Nozawa

Spenser Pokemon Advanced Generations Spenser is the Frontier Brain and master of the Battle Frontier's Battle Palace. He is a kind old man who specializes in herbal medicines.
