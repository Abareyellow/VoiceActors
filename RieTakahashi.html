Rie Takahashi

Rie Takahashi is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She represents 81 Produce. From an early age, she had an interest in after watching Ojamajo Doremi, Higurashi When They Cry and Soul Eater in her childhood and junior high. While in her junior high school, she found out that some male character were voice by Females. After learning that fact, she decided to become an voice actor. Takahashi participated in an audition sponsored by 81 Produce. She passed the audition, and after graduating from high school in 2012, she began attending 81 Produce's training school, 81 Actor's Studio. After graduating from the 81 Actor's Studio in 2013, she formally joined 81 Produce. Her debut as a voice actress was in 2013. She played Futaba Ichinose in Seiyu's Life! Later, she voiced Megumin in KonoSuba, Emilia in Re:Zero-Starting Life in Another World and Mash Kyrielight in Fate/Grand Order.
