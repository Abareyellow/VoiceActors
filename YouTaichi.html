You Taichi

You Taichi is a Japanese voice actress. He is affiliated with VIMS. She voiced Kenya from ERASED, Albus from Grimoire of Zero, and Anzu Yokoyama from Prison School.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 6
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobby: Watching Indian Movies
  • Skills: Piano and Memoriizing Music Scores
