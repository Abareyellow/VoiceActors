Ai Satou

Ai Satou is voice actress working with Ken Production. She has voiced in Detective Conan, City Hunter 2, Black Jack and Pokemon.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 12, 1955
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Education: Aoyama Gakuin University

Characters Voiced by Ai Satou

Mrs. Bradley
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood - 4-Koma Theater
Mrs. Bradley is the wife of King Bradley. She is kind and trusting wife. She speaks jovially of her familial life and romantic past.
Sachiko Yagami
  • Death Note
Sachiko Yagami is the mother of Light and Sayu.
