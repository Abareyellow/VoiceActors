Wakana Yamazaki

Wakana Yamazaki is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced Ran Mouri in Detective Conan, and Phoebe in Gulliver Boy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 21, 1965
  • Hometown: Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan

Janelyn Sailor Moon Janelyn is part of a famous figure skater duo with Misha.
