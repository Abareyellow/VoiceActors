Wakana Yamazaki

Wakana Yamazaki is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. She voiced Ran Mouri in Detective Conan, and Phoebe in Gulliver Boy.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: March 21, 1965
  • Hometown: Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan

Characters Voiced by Wakana Yamazaki

Janelyn
  • Sailor Moon
Janelyn is part of a famous figure skater duo with Misha.
Koan
  • Sailor Moon R
  • Sailor Moon R Memorial
Koan is the youngest sisters of the Ayakashi Sisters. She is the counterpart of Rei Hino/Sailor Mars. She, like Rei, use fire as her main attack. She is very vain about her appearance to the point of swearing to death upon anyone who smudges her make-up.
