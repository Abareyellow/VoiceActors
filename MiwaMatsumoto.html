Miwa Matsumoto

Miwa Matsumoto is a Japanese voice actress. She voiced Patamon in Digimon series and Chiharu Mihara in Cardcaptor Sakura series.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 15, 1971
  • Hometown: Fukuoka, Japan
