Tomokazu Seki

Tomokazu Seki is a Japanese actor, voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Haikyou. Seki is the co-founder of Atomic Monkey. His debut role is Tomoche Massarik from Mobile Suit Victory Gundam. His notable roles include Rob Lucci from One Piece, and Shinichi Chiaki from Nodame Cantabile.

  • Birthday: September 8, 1972
