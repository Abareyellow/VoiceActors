Junichi Suwabe

Junichi Suwabe is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Haikyo. His popular roles is Victor Nikiforov in Yuri!!! on Uce, Dandy from Space Dandy and Undertaker in Black Butler. He has a relationship with Masakazu Morita, Akira Ishida and Kenichi Suzumura. He won Best Actor in Supporting role in the 7th Seiyu Awards.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 29, 1972
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
