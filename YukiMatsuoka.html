Yuki Matsuoka

Yuki Matsuoka is a Japanese voice actress and narrator. She is affiliated with Production Baobab. She graduated from Otemae Women's University in Nishinomiya. She voiced Kanae Itou in Amagami SS and Nana in Elfen Lied.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 13, 1970
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Skills: Japanese Dance and Oil Painting

Characters Voiced by Yuki Matsuoka

Sanae Yukishiro Pretty Cure Sanae Yukishiro is a kind woman and Honoka's grandmother. She takes care of Honoka while her parents are on business. Yuki Matsuoka voiced the younger Sanae Yukishiro.
Senes Giat Scrapped Princess Senes Giat is the princess od the Giat Empire. She is short-tempered due to her childhood. She is a warrior skilled with her sword amd magic. She has natural strengths, giving the name "The Beat Princess." She was able to surpassed her older siblings.
