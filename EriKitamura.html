Eri Kitamura

Eri Kitamura is a Japanese voice actress and singer who is affiliated with Early Wing. She used to be a child actress that went by the name of Eri Okamura. Eri has a long relationship with Kana Hanazawa. Kitamura is an amateur manga artist. She voiced Madako from Uchu Sentai Kyuranger. She voiced in animes such as Toradora!, High School of the Dead, Puella Magi Madoka Magica and Angel Beats!

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 16, 1987
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Drawing Manga, Games, Watching Anime
  • Nickname: KitaEri
