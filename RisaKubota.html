Risa Kubota

Risa Kubota is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Mausu Promotion. During her preschool years, she was fond of playing outside and dodgeball. When Shindou was in elementary school, she developed skills in physical education and home education. She developed an interest in making sweets. Upon entering high school, she enrolled at an all-girls school, where she continued to pratice making sweets while making friends. Along making swwets, she began to become interested in pursing a career as a voice actress. In 2015, Kubota enrolled in a voice acting school operated by the talent agency Promotion. From there, she has voiced Iu Shindo in I'm Standing on a Million Lives, Rosetta in Lapis Re:Lights, and Roko Sekino in Dropout Idol Fruit Tart.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 2, 1995
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
