Minoru Inaba

Minoru Inaba is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Ken Production. He is the voice of Buzz Lightyear in the Japanese dub of Buzz Lightyear of Star Command and Cyclonus and Razorclaw in The Transformers. He voiced in The Small Giant, Microman as Chain Spider, and Cross Game as Sentaro Maeno.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: November 8, 1951
  • Hometown: Shizuoka, Japan
  • Hobby: Fishing

Characters Voiced by Minoru Inaba

Mr. Fuji
  • Pokemon: The Origins
Mr. Fuji is kind old man who lives in Lavender Town. He is the founder of Cinnabar island laboratory and loves Pokemon dearly.
Ramos
  • Pokemon XY
  • Pokemon XYZ
Ramos is the Gym Leader of Coumarine Gym and a gardener. He specializes in Grass-type Pokemon. He is an old, cheery man who believes that one has to take different actions rather than charge right ahead.
