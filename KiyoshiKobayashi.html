Kiyoshi Kobayashi

Kiyoshi Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actor, voice actor and narrator. He is attached to the Tokyo Actor's Consumer's Cooperative Society. He is a gradulate of the Tokyo Metropolitan Koishikawa High School and Nihon Unversity. He is best kown for his role in Lupin the Third as Daisuke Jigen and Shazzan as Shazzan.
