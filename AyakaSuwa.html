Ayaka Suwa

Ayaka Suwa is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Arts Vision. She voiced Clara Kisenaria in Fairy Gone and Matoi Sumeragi in Matoi the Sacred Slayer.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 27, 1988
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ayaka Suwa

Hijiri Kasuga
  • Trinity Seven
  • Trinity Seven Movie 1: Eternity Library & Alchemic Girl
  • Trinity Seven Movie 2: Heavens Library to Crimson Lord
Hijiri Kasuga is a childhood best friend and cousin of Arata Kasuga. She is a cheerful and poised young lady, as well as a strong devotion to hose to those close to her. Despite being his observer, she care deeply for Arata's well-being. Along with her caring side, she has a very cold and ruthless side to her. She can be forceful and intimidationg. During battles, Hijiri displays a calm and calculative attitude. She remains incredibly resolute in achieving her goals.
Nibi
  • Children of the Whales
Nibi is one of the members of the Moles and Ouni's best friend. He has a boisterious personality. He is confident but terrified that Ouni would leave him behind. He wants to stay with Ouni on and off the Mud Whale. Ayaka Suwa voiced younger Nibi.
Urumi
  • Children of the Whales
  • Children of the Whales Specials
Urumi is a young Marked girl of the Mud Whale. She is a part of the Skyros Inifiltration Force to save the Mud Whale citizens.
