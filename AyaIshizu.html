Aya Ishizu

Aya Ishizu is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with Theater Echo.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 22, 1972
  • Hometown:Tokyo, Japan

Character Voiced by Aya Ishizu

Rokuta The Twelve Kingdom Rokuta or Enki is the kirin of En abondoned by his parents at young age and found by his nyokai. Because he matured at young age, his appears as a young boy compared to other kirin. He and Shoryu chare a good relationship and able to treat one another in a casual manner. He was reluctant to choose a new king. Aya Ishizu voiced younger Rokuta.
