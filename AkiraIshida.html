Akira Ishida

Akira Ishida is a Japanese voice actor. He was formerly part of Mausu Promotion from 1998 until March 2009. He is currently part of Gerbera Peerless. He has worked in animes such Accel World, March Comes in like a Lion, Yona of the Dawn, Sailor Moon and Pandora Hearts. He was voted the most popular voice actor at the Animage Anime Grand Prix 2004 and Best Supporting Actor at Seiyuu Awards 2007.
