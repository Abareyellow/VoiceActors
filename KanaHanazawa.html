Kana Hanazawa

Kana Hanazawa is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Hanazawa started acting when she was in her early elementary school years. She appeared on Yappari Sanma Daisensi. At age 14, She made her debut as Holly in Last Exile. At age 17, she voiced Ryoko Kaminagi in Zegapain. She signed up to be a voice actor with Office Osawa. Kana went to college in 2007 and began her career as a voice actress. She graduated from college in 2011 and became a full time voice actress. Her major roles are Tenishi from Angel Beats!, Anri Sonohara from Durarara!! and Hinata Kawamoto from March Comes in Like a Lion.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: February 25, 1989
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
