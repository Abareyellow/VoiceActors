Rie Murakawa

Rie Murakawa is a Japanese voice actress, singer, and radio personality. She is affiliated with Haikyo. Her family consists of her father, mother, older sister and younger brother. She had an interest in playing games as well as magical girl animes such as Sailor Moon and Magic Knight Rayearth from an early age. During her fifth grade of elementary school, Murakawa started having an interest in voice acting after watching the anime series Hunter x Hunter, which had been recommened to her older cousin. She was inspired by the voice actress Yuki Kaida. Rie did theater club (for a little bit) and basketball club in high school. After high school, Murakawa went to Hailkyo voice acting school. Her debut role was CoCo from Fairy Tail.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: June 1, 1990
  • Birthplace: Saitama Prefecture, Japan

Characters Voiced by Rie Murakawa

Coco Fairy Tail Coco is a small yet fast, barefoot girl who serves as a messenger. She is a bright, cheery girl who is dutiful to her king and her country. She follows orders and unable to contain her energy. She is from Edolas, the alternative dimension to the Earthland.
Coco of Earthland Fairy Tail Coco of Earthland is the Eartland counter-part of Coco. She uses Athletic Magic as her magic. She is opposite of Edolas self. She takes pride in her abilities, giving a score everytime she attacks. She can be quite naive.
