Miho Okasaki

Miho Okasaki is a Japanese voice actress. She id affiliated with I'm Enterprise. Aspiring to became a voice actress since early in her life, she enrolled at the Japan Narration Actor Institude agency. She debuted in 2017 and did some minor, background characters. Her roles were 2018's Music Girls as Miku Nishio and That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime as Rimuru Tempest.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 22, 1998
  • Birthplace: Okayama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Karaoke and Shojo Manga

Characters Voiced by Miho Okasaki

Rifana
  • The Rising of the Shield Hero
Rifana is the childhood friend of Keel and Raphtalia. She loves to talk about love and boys, especially about Shield Hero. She lives in the demi-human village of Melromarc.
Rimuru Tempest
  • That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime
  • That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime OVA
Rimuru Tempest is Mikami Satoru reincarnated a slime. He is easygoing, yet calculating and cautious. Rimuru is known as a pacifist, he tries to avoid casualties and save people if possible. His wish is the main good relations with humans. He is the founder and King of the monster of Jura Forest. He has a kind heart and quirky personality.
