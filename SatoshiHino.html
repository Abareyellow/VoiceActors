Satoshi Hino

Satoshi Hino is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Axl-One. His major roles are Yuuji Sakai in Shakugan no Hana, Saito Hiraga in The Familiar of Zero, and Kamui in Gintamia. He is married to Saki Nakajima.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: August 4, 1978
  • Birthplace: San Francisco, California
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: English and Kendo

Characters Voiced by Satoshi Hino

Hiro Iokawa Living for The Day After Tomorrow Hiro Iokawa is Karada Iokawa's older brother. He once dated Shouko Nogami after they studied abroad in America. He is caring and kind man who works in a pharmacy.
Mikhail Renfred The Ancient Magus' Bride Mikhail Renfred is a sorcerer and mentor to Alice. He is serious and stendoffish yet deeply concerned for the one under his protection. Mikhail is a talented socerer and hate it.
