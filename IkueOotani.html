Ikue Ootani

Ikue Ootani is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and narrator. She is well known for her voice of Pikachu from Pokemon and Tony Tony Chopper from One Piece. she has played both male and female roles.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: August 18, 1965
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Hobbies: Japanese dancing and Jazz
