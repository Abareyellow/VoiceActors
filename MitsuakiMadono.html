Mitsuaki Madono

Mitsuaki Madono is a Japanese voice actor. He is well known for voicing Joker from Flame of Recca, Kotaro Yanagisawa in Assassination and Loki from Valkyrie Profile.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 28, 1964
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan

Character Voiced by Mitsuaki Madono

Tad Pokemon Tad is a Pokemon trainer with Poliwarth. He won his hometown's most powerful Pokemon competition.
