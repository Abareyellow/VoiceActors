Mitsuaki Madono

Mitsuaki Madono is a Japanese voice actor. He is well known for voicing Joker from Flame of Recca, Kotaro Yanagisawa in Assassination Classroom and Loki from Valkyrie Profile.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: July 28, 1964
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan

Characters Voiced by Mitsuaki Madono

Freddy O'Martian
  • Pokemon Black and White
  • Pokemon Black and White: Rival Destinies
  • Pokemon Black and White: Decolora Adventure
Freddy O'Martian is an announcer who hosts several Pokemon competitions in the Unova region. His is very enthusiastic, the go-to announcer for any event and his style is the same to a sports commentor. He is good friends with Don George.
Jaco
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
Jaco is a Pokemon Breeder from the Sinnoh region. He is a student at a training camp for Electric-Type Pokemon. He prefers Flying Pokemon than the Electric type Pokemon he works with.
Tad
  • Pokemon
Tad is a Pokemon trainer with Poliwarth. He won his hometown's most powerful Pokemon competition.
