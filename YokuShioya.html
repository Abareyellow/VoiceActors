Yoku Shioya

Yoku Shioya is a Japanese voice actor. His debut role was Triton in Triton of the Sea. He is brothers with Kozo Shioya.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: June 24, 1958
  • Birthplace: Kagoshima, Japan
  • Brother: Kozo Shioya

Character Voiced by Yoku Shioya

Misha Sailor Moon Misha is part of a famous figure skater duo with Janelyn.
