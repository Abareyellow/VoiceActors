Tessho Genda

Tessho Genda is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with 81 Produce. As of 2007, he has 234 roles under his name. He voiced Shu in Dragon Ball, Yutaka Goda in My Love Story!! and Umibouzu in City Hunters.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 20, 1948
  • Hometown: Okayama, Japan
  • Birth Name: Mitsuo Yokoi
