Ai Maeda

Ai Maeda came to the spotlight when she was 8 years, appearing in a McDonald commerical. She has an younger sister named Aki Maeda. Both of them had a model a model career together until Ai was 17 years old. At age 17, Maeda started in many movies and TV. Maeda is skilled in playing piano and speaking English. Ai graduate from Aoyama Gakuin University and studied abroad in Canada for a year in 2000. Her most notable performance is voicing the title role in the anime series Kino's Journey. She performed the end song of series "The Beautiful World." She is currently married to Nakamura Kankuro.
