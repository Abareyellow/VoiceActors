Keiji Fujiwara

Keiji Fujiwara was a Japanese voice actor. He originally affiliated with Ken Production but left to pursue his own agency. He founded his own talent management and production company, Air Agency. He graduated from Senshu Daigaku Kitakami High School. He attended Bungakuza Theater Research Institute. In 2008, he began lecturing at Japan Newart College. He has took hiatus in 2016 because he recieve medical treatment for a unspecified illness. Fujiwara died on Sunday, April 12, 2020 due to cancer. His debut role was in RPG Densetsu Hepoi. He is best known for his role of Maes Hughes in Fullmetal Alchemist and Holland from Eureka Seven. Fujiwara is Cheeda Nick from Tokemi Sentai Go-Busters.
