Keiji Fujiwara

Keiji Fujiwara is a Japanese voice actor. He founded his own talent management and production company, Air Agency. he is best known for his role of Maes Hughes in Fullmetal Alchemist and Holland from Eureka Seven. Fujiwara is Cheeda Nick from Tokemi Sentai Go-Busters.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: October 5, 1964
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
