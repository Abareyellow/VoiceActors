Mikako Komatsu

Mikako Komatsu is a Japanese actress, voice actress, and singer. She is affiliated with Hirata Office. She was a part of the idol group, Imouto. She leaved Imouto in September 2007. She is a fan of Mai Kuraki. Her debut as a seiyu was Joey Jones in Heroman.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: November 11, 1988
  • Birthplace: Mie, Japan
  • Skills: Playing Marimba Instrument and Singing
