Mari Natsuki

Mari Natsuki or Junko Nakajima is a Japanses singer, dancer and actress. She started work as a singer from a young age. In 2007, Natsuki announced her engagement with Nobu Saitou.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 2, 1952
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Skills: Singing
  • Husband: Nobu Saitou
