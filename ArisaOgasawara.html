Arisa Ogasawara

Arisa Ogasawara is a Japanese actor, voice actress and narrator. She voiced Cady Heron in Japanese dub of Mean Girls and Kim Possible in the Japanese dub of Kim Possible. Her main anime roles are Panty from Panty & Stocking with Garterbelt and Cheza from Wolf's Rain.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: September 30, 1980
