Masuo Amada

Masuo Amada is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated with Seinenza Theater Company. Amada gradulated from the Osaka University of Arts.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: January 20, 1958
  • Birthplace: Hyogo Prefecture, Japan

Character Voiced by Masuo Amada

Darius

  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood - 4-Koma Theater
