Sayaka Kanda

Sayaka Kanda is a Japanese actress and singer. She is the child of Masaki Kanda and Seiko Matsuda. Sayaka is currently married to Mitsu Murata. She voiced Anna in the Japanese dub of Frozen.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: October 1, 1986
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan
  • Husband: Mitsu Murata

Character Voiced by Sayaka Kanda

Yuna
  • Sword Art Online Movie: Ordinal Scale
  • Sword Art Online: Alicization - War of Underworld: Part II
Yuna is an idol in the AR world of Ordinal Scale. If a player encounters her in the game, they can acquire special upgrades. Yuna is an obedient, yet strong-willed girl.
