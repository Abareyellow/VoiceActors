Mirai Shida

Mirai Shida is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She was regognized after she was in The Queen Classroom, she played the role of Kazumi Kanda. After her role, she has appeared in many dramas and commercials.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 10, 1993
  • Birth place: Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan
