Manaka Iwami

Manaka Iwami is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Pro-Fit. She voiced in Kotoko Watatsumugi from Urahara and Lycos from Children of the Whales.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 30, 1998
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading and Strolling
  • Skills: Tuba
