Manaka Iwami

Manaka Iwami is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She is affiliated with Pro-Fit. She voiced in Kotoko Watatsumugi from Urahara and Lykos from Children of the Whales.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: April 30, 1998
  • Hometown: Saitama, Japan
  • Hobbies: Reading and Strolling
  • Skills: Tuba

Characters Voiced by Manaka Iwami

Kotoko Watatsumugi
  • Urahara
Kotoko Watatsumugi is a the smart one out of Rito's group. She loves her friend more than anything and cares for them deeply.
Lykos
  • Children of the Whales
  • Children of the Whales Specials
Lykos is a former emotionless soldier of the Allied Empire. She is introverted and self-reliant, having survived for a long time on her own. She cares and want to protect the people from the Mud Whale. She has a soft spot for Chakuro and members of the Mud Whale.
Maquia
  • Maquia - When The Promised Flower Blooms
Maquia is a 15-year-old Iorphian orphan and the adoptive mother of Ariel. At first, Maquia is extremely shy and soft spoken. She doubts her ability to be a mother to Ariel. She struggles to adapt to life outside of her birth nation. She is a skilled weaver. Many of her relationships have a melancholy undertone. Because she is Iorphian, Maquia ages slowier than humans.
