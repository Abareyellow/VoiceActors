Yuki Kaji

Yuki Kaji is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with VIMs. He voiced Eren Yeager in Attack on Titans, Clemont in Pokemon, and Akio Fudou in Inazuma Eleven.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: September 3, 1985
  • Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
  • Pets: Hamster
