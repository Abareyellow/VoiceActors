Ayaka Asai

Ayaka Asai is a voice actress who is affiliated with Seinenza Film Company. She become a member of the company in 2013, the same year she gradulated from the General Institute of Amusement Media. She worked in animes such as Watamoto and The Testament of Sister New Devil.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: May 11, 1992
  • Birthplace: Shizuoka, Japan

Characters Voiced by Ayaka Asai

Eva
  • Sword Art Online Alternative: Gun Gale Online
Eva is a player in Gun Gale Online. Her real name is Saki Nitobe. She and her friends are part of their school gymnastic team.
Kenya Misaki
  • That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime
Kenya Misaki is one of Shizu's student. He has fire magic. He has a wild and carefree personality.

  • Children of the Whales

Pokemon Voiced by Ayaka Asai

Glaceon Pokemon: Mewtwo-Prologue to Awakening Glaceon is a Pokemon from the Sinnoh region. Glaceon is one of the final forms of Eevee. This Pokemon is able to create small ice crystals around it by controlling its body temperature. Ayaka Asai voiced Vigil's Glaceon.
