Mayuki Makiguchi

Mayuki Makiguchi is a Japanese voice actress. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. She voiced Serena in Pokemon XY.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 9, 1984
  • Hometown: Aichi, Japan
  • Hobby: Ballet
