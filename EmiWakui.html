Emi Wakui

Emi Wakui is a Japanese actor. She has won three Japanese Academy Awards: the 1994 award for Best Actress award and 1992 awards for Best Supporting Actress and Best Newcomer for her roles.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: December 8, 1970
  • Hometown: Kanagawa, Japan

Character Voiced by Emi Wakui

Sakura's Mother I Want to Eat Your Pancreas Sakura's Mother is the mother of Sakura Yamauchi.
