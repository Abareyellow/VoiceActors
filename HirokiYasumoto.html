Hiroki Yasumoto

Hiroki Yasumoto is a Japanese voice actor. His roles are usually mature man or big guy roles. He voiced Germany in Hetalia, Agni in Black Butler and Agil in Sword Art Online.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: March 16, 1977
  • Education: Komozawa University
  • Hometown: Yamaguchi, Japan
