Hiroki Yasumoto

Hiroki Yasumoto is a Japanese voice actor. His roles are usually mature man or big guy roles. He voices in Hetalia, Black Butler and Sword Art Online

  • Birthday: March 16, 1977
  • Education: Komozawa University
  • Hometown: Yamaguchi, Japan
