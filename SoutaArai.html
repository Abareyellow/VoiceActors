Souta Arai

Souta Arai is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Aoni Production. He graduate from the Tokyo University of Science.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: April 12, 1986
  • Birthplace: Tokyo, Japan

Pokemon Voiced by Souta Arai

Umbreon Pokemon Black and White: Rival Destinies Umbreon is a Pokemon from the Johto region. Umbreon is an evolved form of Eevee. When it gets agitated, it can release a poisonous sweat. Souta Arai voiced Vigil's Umbreon.
