Saori Gotou

Saori Gotou is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She hosts MAG Net with fellow voice actor Daisuke Ono. She is currently free but was a part of 81 Produce.

  • Gender: Female
  • Birthday: January 8, 1987
  • Birthplace: Kanagawa, Japan
  • Hobbies: Cooking and Going to Shinto Shrines
  • Skills: Calisthenics
