Takuya Eguchi

Takuya Eguchi is a Japanese voice actor and singer. He is affiliated with Produce 81. In 2017, Eguchi launched a manga about his life called Eguchi Takuya no Gainen Plant. He is a part of Trignal with Ryouhei Kimura and Tsubasa Yonaga. He voiced Kazuya Kujo in Gosick, Takeo Godo in My Love Story, and William Twining in Devils and Realist.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: May 22, 1987
  • Hometwon: Ibaraki, Japan

Kazuya Kujou
  • Gosick
  • Gosick Recap
Kazuya Kujou is the youngest son of a soldier. He went to studied abroad in Sauville at Saint Marguerite Academy. He is described as a good and sincere person whose soul is pure enough to admit pride. He does have a blunt side when he is annoyed. He takes the honor of being an Imperial soldier's son to heart. He possesses an imperfect memory, remembering details that are helpful but unable to remember outside information.
