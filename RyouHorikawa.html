Ryou Horikawa

Ryou Horikawa or Makoto Horikawa is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He started out as a child actor in elementary school. His debut as a voice actor was Kenta Hirono from Dream Soldier Wing-Man. He is well known for his voice role in Super Smash Bros as Captain Falcon. He is a fluent English speaker. He for his anime voice role such as Vegeta from Dragon Ball, Andromeda Shun from Saint Seiya, and Reinhard von Lohengramm from Legend of the Galactic Heroes.

  • Gender: Male
  • Birthday: February 1, 1958
  • Hometown: Osaka, Japan
  • Hobbies: Baseball and Swimming
  • Wife: Hitomi Oikawa
